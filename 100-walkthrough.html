    Welcome to the Elden Ring 100% Walkthrough. The purpose of this guide is to assist players during their journey in the entire Lands Between map so they won't miss anything in the game. As you may know, in order to get the platinum trophy you need to defeat only specific bosses and get only the legendary items to unlock their respective trophies. However, the Lands Between have much more to offer, featuring overworld bosses and dungeons that you may not ever see if you are only looking to get the platinum trophy.

    For the players that want to get as much as possible from this game, though, this guide is here to help featuring the majority of items and bosses you can find. You will be able to get the platinum trophy by following this guide, but information on the trophies and boss strategies will be listed on the trophy guide exclusively. So this walkthrough should be used in conjunction with the trophy guide, whose link can be found below:

    This guide aims to list almost every item of importance that you can pick in the game, finding all optional dungeons, battling every possible boss and complete as many NPC quests as possible.

    The walkthrough WILL NOT mention the locations of the following:
    • Crafting materials found in the overworld or in dungeons (example Arteria Leaf, Sliver of Meat, Rune Fragments etc.) 
    • The majority of weapon and ashes upgrade materials (e.g. smithing stones, gloveworts) due to the sheer amount of them that can be found in the world and the dungeons. The guide will only mention some high-rarity / difficult to get.
    • Golden Runes, again because of their high number in the game with the exception of some really high-rarity ones.
    • Every mob and regular enemy unless some of their locations help with orientation in the overworld or in a dungeon or are necessary to obtain something.
    • Consumable items (such as any Grease, Arrows, Fowl Feet etc.) unless they are of great importance.
    • Weapons and armor that can be obtained as a loot by killing multiple / grinding enemies. These drops, on top of not being guaranteed but chance-based, can take an absurd amount of time until you get them. The guide will focus only on the equipment that are guaranteed as a drop from enemies.
      For example: The Godrick Knight Set can be dropped by killing Knights in the Limgrave area and the set is composed of the 1) Helm, 2) Gauntlets, 3) Armor and 4) Greaves. You may get the set's Helm after killing your first knight enemy and then not find the rest pieces of the set until after you kill 100 of them. The guide will not tell you to keep killing Godrick's knights until you have the entire set.
    • The guide will also NOT mention methods that allow you to access entire regions much earlier than when you are supposed to, also known in the community as wrong warp.

    One may argue that since not absolutely everything is listed in this guide (such as the stuff mentioned in the above list), it is not a true 100% walkthrough, so I apologize in advance if this guide is not what you look for. In the future some of these categories may be added to the guide, specifically the equipment that you can get by grinding enemies.

    Starting your playthrough

    The game begins in the character creation screen. The first major choice you will have to make is to choose the class of your character. You get to choose between 10 different classes, all of which have their pros and cons, some focused on melee combat, some in ranged combat and in using magic. The Wretch is the game's blueprint class. It starts at a lower level than the rest, has only one weapon at first and the beginning of the game can be pretty rough. It offers, however, the ability to shape your character's stats the way you want them. Unless you have previous experience with this type of games, it is recommended to use a class like Vagabond or Hero if you are somewhat new to FromSoft games, otherwise feel free to go with the Wretch. You are, of course, free to choose whatever class fits your style the most. The author of this walkthrough went with the Wretch class, since this guide was created based on the author's second run in the game. Take a look at the Tips & Strategies section of the trophy guide for a list of all classes and what they are focusing on.

    An important feature in this game is that after defeating a specific boss, you are allowed to use an item called Larval Tear and you can respec your character via the Rebirth process. This means that if at any point you have regretted your initial choice or if you want to re-distribute your levels to different stats, you have the ability to do so.

    Regardless of class you also get to choose one Keepsake. It doesn't really matter which one you choose, since almost everything is also obtainable in the game as well and it will not affect your overall progress for the platinum trophy and/or your complete run.

    However it is highly recommended that you choose one of the following 3 keepsakes:
    1. Golden Seed: This can be used at the Sites of Grace to give your Flask an extra charge. Especially early in the game an extra heal can be the difference between death and victory
    2. Stonesword Key: There are more of these in the game, which are used in special statues to unlock areas that contain some juicy loot. Some are found in the world, others can be purchased for a few thousand Runes from some Merchants.
    3. Lands Between Rune: Gives 3000 Runes. Later in the game that many Runes are not important but when you start the game it means an extra 4-5 levels which can make in a difference in the first battles. From a collector's perspective this is the ONLY one of its kind you will find in the game.

    You are of course free to choose whichever you want. The author of the guide went with the Golden Seed. If you want to have everything in the game, then picking the Lands Between Rune is a must. One final thing to note before starting this game, NEVER kill an NPC unless you are instructed to do so in the walkthrough. When talking to an NPC, ALWAYS exhaust the dialogue with them.

    Once you watch the introduction, you start the game in the Chapel of Anticipation area. There is nothing of importance here except the Tarnished's Wizened Finger found in the game's first room. Familiarize yourself with the controls and fight the Grafted Scion boss. You are meant to lose this fight, as once you die you will move on with the game and be presented with another cutscene. In the unlike case, however, that you manage to defeat the boss, you will obtain an Ornamental Straight Sword and a Golden Beast Crest Shield. These items can also be found later in the game, so there is no need to worry that you missed them if you failed to kill the boss. If you somehow beat the boss, then progress to the edge of the cliff in the next area to fall to your death and continue with the game.

    In the next area you will find yourself into, Stranded Graveyard, you can choose to fall down the hole by the ghost in the chair to access the tutorial dungeon. It is advised that you do this dungeon, especially during your first playthrough, to learn the controls and some of the game's mechanics while also getting a couple of extra Runes and the Strength! gesture at the end of the tutorial. The tutorial also features the Soldier of Godrick boss, which serves as a final test. You will be meeting this enemy regularly during your run. Once you complete the tutorial, you can then go up the stairs in the starting area, exit the building and begin your journey in The Lands Between.

    West Limgrave




    As you exit the tutorial area, Stranded Graveyard, you will see an enemy in golden armor riding a horse. This is a mini-boss called Tree Sentinel. You may wish to fight him right now, but at the beginning of the game you are too weak and he hits like a truck. It is highly advised that you ignore the fight for now and move on past him until you are much stronger.

    Talk to NPC Varre in front of the first Site of Grace and then your first stop should be the Church of Elleh, followed by the Gatefront Ruins after the forest to the northeast, where you can find this area's map near a pillar. After you obtain the map, make sure to touch and rest in the Site of Grace under Stormgate (northwest part of the Ruins). This will introduce you to Melina, who will give you your mount, Torrent and the ability to level up at the Sites of Grace by talking to her. You are now free to explore the entire region.

    1. Church of Elleh: Located past the Tree Sentinel in the beginning of the game, features Merchant Kale. Buy the Crafting Kit from him. Also sells various items including Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 1, Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 2, Missionary's Cookbook 1, Telescope, a Torch, a Large Leather Shield and a Chain set.
    2. Stormfoot Catacombs: Located northwest from Church of Elleh. The fire-spitting pillars can be disabled by attacking them once. Contains Prattling Pate (Hello), Wandering Noble Ashes and the Noble Sorcerer Ashes, which are dropped by boss Erdtree Burial Watchdog.
    3. Groveside Cave: Located north from Church of Elleh in the forest. Contains Flamedrake Talisman that is dropped by boss Beastman of Farum Azula.
    4. Gatefront Ruins: Located after the forest northeast from Church of Elleh. Contains this region's map at the bottom of a pillar and the Ash of War: Storm Stomp & Whetstone Knife in an underground room to the east part of the ruins. The chests on the back of the wagons contain a Flail and a Lordsworn's Greatsword.
    5. After you acquire Torrent, fast travel to the Church of Elleh and talk to Renna that will appear there. She will give you the Spirit Calling Bell and the Lone Wolf Ashes.
    6. Southeast of the Gatefront Ruins you will find a bridge. Between the bridge and a telescope near the road you will hear a voice calling out to you. Near some trees you will find a red bush/tree that if you attack, will reveal the NPC Boc. Exhaust the dialogue with him. Near the bridge you can find a scarab that drops the Ash of War: Determination. At nights you will find the Night's Cavalry boss on the bridge. Drops Ash of War: Repeating Thrust.
    7. Waypoint Ruins: Southeast from the bridge. In an underground dungeon, you will fight Mad Pumpkin Head boss. In the room after him, you will find Sorceress Sellen, who sells Sorceries after you become her apprentice. Accepting to become her apprentice also gives you the Nod in Thought gesture. These sorceries include:
      Glintstone Pebble, Glintstone Stars, Glintstone Arc, Crystal Barrage, Scholar's Armament and Scholar's Shield.
    8. Outside the ruins whenever you reload the area, you will see a huge convoy crossing the main road of Limgrave, carried by two giant Trolls. If you kill the Trolls, you can jump to the back of the wagon and open the chest for a Greataxe.
    9. South of the Waypoint Ruins you will find an unnamed enemy camp near the Agheel Lake South Site of Grace. In this area you can find a Royal House Scroll on top of a ruined building guarded by a sorcerer, which once given to Sellen unlocks two more Sorceries for buying, Glintblade Phalanx and Carian Slicer. In this area you will also find a Great Epee in a chest. To the southeast of the camp make sure to also pick up a Starlight Shard found in a huge bowl.
    10. Bridge of Sacrifice: South from the unnamed camp. You can find a Stonesword Key guarded by enemies on the bridge.
    11. Dragon-Burnt Ruins: West of Waypoint Ruins in the middle of Lake Agheel. Here you can find Twinblade in a chest underground (you must use Torrent to jump over the walls to find this one) and a Stonesword Key guarded by a rat at the bottom of a ruined tower back on the surface. A chest in another underground room emits smoke that will teleport you to a far-away location in Caelid/Dragonbarrow, which is way too hard for this point of the game. You can open it, but DON'T STAY IN THE SMOKE FOR LONG. If you somehow end up being trapped, you will find yourself in a mine with very tough enemies. You should exit the small cabin you are in and run to the low level of the area ignoring everything in your way until you find the Site of Grace. Rest and fast travel out of there.
    12. South of the lake under an overpass, you will find Bloody Finger Hunter Yura. Talk to him.
    13. In the southeastern part of the lake guarded by some giant crabs you will find a scarab with Ash of War: Unseathe.
    14. In the middle of the Lake Agheel, fight the Flying Dragon Agheel. Using Torrent is highly recommended for this fight. Drops a Dragon Heart. Talk again to Yura.
    15. West of Yura's location you can carefully jump down with Torrent to reach a beach. On the beach defeat the enemy to get Ash of War: Gravitas. In the northern part of the beach enter the cave, which leads you to the Haligdrake Talisman you could not reach while in the tutorial dungeon.
    16. Forlorn Hound Evergaol: West of the unnamed camp location where you found the Royal House Scroll on the other side of the cliff. Enter the area and defeat Bloodhound Knight Darriwil, who drops Bloodhound's Fang.
    17. Limgrave Tunnels: Northwest part of Lake Agheel. Contains Roar Medallion that is dropped by boss Stonedigger Troll. The boss room is accessed by jumping to a platform while using the dungeon's second elevator.
    18. Murkwater Cave: Follow the river in the northeast part of Lake Agheel. Outside of the cave you will be invaded by Bloody Finger Nerijus, who drops Reduvia. You will be aided by Yura for this fight.

      In the cave open the chest with the Cloth Garb to fight Patches, however DO NOT KILL him when he begs for mercy to get the Grovel for Mercy gesture and unlock him as a merchant. Revisit the area and buy from him Margit's Shackle. Also sells Parrying Dagger, Horse Crest Wooden Shield, Stonesword Key, Missionary's Cookbook 2. The second chest behind him is a trap, which teleports you to East Limgrave. If you open it, then return to him, he will give you the Calm Down gesture. If you attack him and start a fight, let him take you to low HP and you will get the Extreme Repentance gesture. Use the gesture on him to calm him, otherwise even if he kills you, return and talk to him and he will be friendly again.
    19. Murkwater Catacombs: Located higher up the river past Murkwater Cave. On your way there talk to Yura. The dungeon contains the Banished Knight Engvall Ashes, which are dropped by boss Grave Warden Duelist.
    20. Feel free to fight the Tree Sentinel now with the help of Torrent. Drops Golden Halberd. Afterwards descend to the beach west of his location using Torrent.
    21. In the beach go to the northern end and attack the invisible scarab (spotted by its footprints on the sand) to get Ash of War: Stamp (Sweep). The merchant beneath the ruin you used to get on the beach sells a Club, Broadsword, Shortbow, Iron Roundshield, Armorer's Cookbook 2.
    22. Coastal Cave: The entrance is on the beach. Talk to Boc near the Site of Grace. Defeat the Demi-Human Chiefs to get Sewing Needle and Tailoring Tools, then give the needle to Boc.
    23. Church of Dragon Communion: Found after you exit Coastal Cave. Offer the Dragon Heart to the statue in the church to unlock an Incantation. It doesn't matter which one you choose, we'll get the rest later. You can choose between Dragonfire, Dragonclaw and Dragonmaw.
    24. Return to Stormgate. Follow the path and after you start ascending out of the canyon, pick up the Golden Seed by the gold tree.
    25. Stormhill Shack: North of the Golden Seed. Get the Stonesword Key outside the shack then go in and talk to Roderika. Exhaust the dialogue with her to get the Sitting Sideways gesture and the Spirit Jellyfish Ashes.
    26. Southwest of the Shack near a forest area, you will find a graveyard with some jellyfish spirits. Here you can find Godrick Soldier Ashes.
    27. If you head to the southwestern part of this elevated area you can find a Starlight Shard. On top of a ruined building a little to the east you can find a corpse with the Crossed Legs gesture.
    28. South of the Stormhill Shack if you follow the path on the right side of the cliff until you reach the top area of Stormgate, you can find the Arrow's Reach Talisman in a chest guarded by some enemies. The entrance is east from the Evergaol. We will visit the Evergaol later as the boss here can be a bit difficult at this point of the game.
    29. Warmaster's Shack: East of Stormhill Shack in the forest. At days you will find NPC Knight Bernahl, who sells Ashes of War, including Ash of War: Stamp (Upward Cut), Ash of War: Kick, Ash of War: Endure, Ash of War: War Cry, Ash of War: Spinning Slash, Ash of War: Impaling Thrust, Ash of War: Quickstep, Ash of War: Storm Blade, Ash of War: Parry and Ash of War: No Skill.

      At nights you will find the Bell Bearing Hunter once you enter the cabin, who drops the Bone Peddler's Bell Bearing, which you can give to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold a bit later. If the boss is too hard for you right now, feel free to return later once you are stronger.
    30. Northwest of Warmaster's Shack on an elevated area you can find the Strength-Knot Crystal Tear in a huge bowl past a giant Troll. This special tear can be used for an item called the Flask of Wondrous Physik, which will be obtained in a later region.
    31. North of Warmaster's Shack, follow the path until you are invaded by Recusant Henricus. Drops the Hammer Talisman.
    32. Southeast of Warmaster's Shack during the night on top of a ruin in the plateau with the giant Trolls you can fight the Deathbird boss. Drops the Blue-Feathered Branchsword.
    33. Northeast of Warmaster's Shack you will find an enemy camp. Here you can find Beast Crest Heater Shield in a chest. On the cliff to the north overlooking the camp you can find a mounted enemy who drops Ash of War: Golden Vow. On the top of a ruin accessed from that cliff is a Lance.
    34. Deathtouched Catacombs: Northeast from the camp, accessed if you follow the road and then climb to the cliff on the left. Contains an Uchigatana, the Assassin's Crimson Dagger that is dropped by boss Black Knife Assassin and a Deathroot in the chest in the boss' room.
    35. Northeast of the Catacombs past the bridge's pillars you will find the Lance Talisman on a corpse.
    36. South of the Catacombs on the other cliff you will hear a voice asking for help. That voice belongs to NPC Iron Fist Alexander. Help him by striking the pot in the back a few times until he's released to get the Triumphant Delight gesture. Exhaust the dialogue with him.
    37. Highroad Cave: East of the Catacombs you will find the Saintsbridge Site of Grace. Head south along the cliff until you are able to reach the river at the bottom or jump down using the Spiritspring. To the northwestern part of the river you can find the cave. Contains Shamshir and Blue Dancer Charm that is dropped by Guardian Golem boss.
    38. From the cave follow the river heading south until you reach the Murkwater Coast Site of Grace. Southwest from the Site is an unnamed enemy camp with Armorer's Cookbook 1.
    39. North of Stormhill Shack, follow the road beneath the bridge until you reach another collapsed one and get Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 7 from the corpse at the edge. Carefully descend with Torrent and find the scarab to the right that drops Ash of War: Storm Wall.
    40. Fringefolk Hero's Grave: This dungeon is located inside the tutorial dungeon, Stranded Graveyard. Right next to its Site of Grace, you can use 2 Stonesword Keys to access it. Make sure to bring a bow with you. Progress through the area avoiding the Chariot by hiding in the alcoves to the side of the path as you descend. When you reach a bridge, fall carefully to the right side and proceed. You will soon reach a bigger area with a bridge in the middle, which once you try to cross, will make two Grafted Scions drop from the ceiling. You can avoid the fight if that is too hard for you, but make sure to grab the Erdtree's Favor on the other side of the bridge. If you carefully go down the area from where you first accessed the room with the Grafted Scions, you will eventually make it on a ledge with an enemy overlooking the hallway you crossed with the Chariot. On the ceiling there are 3 huge pots hanged by ropes, which you must shoot when the Chariot is right beneath them to destroy it. Destroying the Chariot will give you the Erdtree Greatbow. As you continue past the bridge and at the next intersection, if you take the right path going up, you will reach an enemy at the end of this path that will drop the Dragon Communion Seal. Move on to the end of the area and fight the Ulcerated Tree Spirit boss that drops a Golden Seed and the Banished Knight Oleg Ashes.
    41. MAIN STORY: Follow the path from Stormhill Shack towards the castle. On the way get the Ash of War: Wild Strikes from the scarab and fight  Margit, the Fell Omen, who drops a Talisman Pouch.
    42. After fighting Margit, rest at a Site of Grace, talk to Melina and she will offer to take you to Roundtable Hold. Here talk with all NPCs and buy any Incantations you want from NPC Corhyn, including Urgent Heal, Heal, Cure Poison, Magic Fortification, Flame Fortification, Rejection, Catch Flame and Flame Sling. You will also get the Prayer gesture from him.

      Interact with NPC Ensha leaning to a wall to get the What Do You Want? gesture.

      The Twin Maiden Husks after him sells a Memory Stone, three Stonesword Keys, a Knight set, a Heater Shield and many basic weapons including Battle Axe, Dagger, Finger Seal, Longbow, Longsword, Mace, Rapier, Scimitar and Short Spear. Also sells the Multiplayer items White Cipher Ring, Blue Cipher Ring, Host's Trick-Mirror and Furled Finger's Trick-Mirror.

      If you drop down from the balcony, you will be invaded by Mad Tongue Alberich, who drops a Taunter's Tongue. Let him complete the bow he does when he invades in order to get the Reverential Bow gesture. This gesture is MISSABLE so be careful not to kill him before you get it. Explore the lower level after the invader to find a Cipher Pata.

      If you go down the stairs past the blacksmith and use 2 Stonesword Keys, you can find a chest with Crepus' Black-Key Crossbow. If you use another 2 on the next statue, you will find another chest with the Assassin's Prayerbook, which you can give to Corhyn for two more Incantations, Assassin's Approach and Darkness.
    43. Stormveil Castle: A huge area right after Margit, contains a multitude of items. As you approach the gate and find it closed, you can talk with NPC Gatekeeper Gostoc. He will offer you to take a shortcut or use the main gate. Getting in through the main gate is actually a trap, as multiple ballistas will fire at you when you pass the gate, so feel free to use the shortcut. Once you manage to open the main gate from inside the castle, Gostoc will also act as a merchant. There is a catch, however, to this NPC; every time you die in the castle 30% of your total held runes at the time of your death will be stolen from him regardless of your choice. If you kill this NPC, you will get back all the Runes that he stole from you along with Gostoc's Bell Bearing, which you can give to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold to access his stock as a merchant. The moment you defeat the area's main boss, Godrick, the Grafted, Gostoc will move to the boss' arena, will stop acting as a merchant (unless you complete his quest near the end of the game) and killing him after defeating Godrick will NOT give you back the Runes he stole (but will still drop his bell bearing). If you have died a lot in this area and lost a handful of Runes, be sure to kill him before defeating Godrick.
      Patch 1.03: With patch 1.03 Gostoc got a new quest. Killing him to get your runes back will end his quest there and mark it as failed. Gostoc can also NOT be revived by doing an Absolution in the Church of Vows accessed later in the game, so be extremely careful. If you want to access his store again, you will have to wait until after you complete his and some other NPCs' quests near the end of the game.

      After you reach the Stormveil Cliffside Site of Grace you can find a Marred Leather Shield as you climb the stairs. Inside the cellar you can find Hookclaws by the enemy with the huge axe patrolling the hallway. In the dark room with the Banished Knight you will find Curved Sword Talisman in a chest. On the higher level of the area if you jump through a hole and navigate through the ramparts you can find a Brick Hammer.

      The next Site of Grace you will reach is the Rampart Tower Site. From there go back up the stairs to access the Rooftops. You can navigate the roofs by jumping on the ledges by the sides of the buildings. Inside a broken tower you can find a Stonesword Key. When you reach the area with a Bladed Talon Eagle enemy, the corpse opposite from it has the Dozing Cross-Legged gesture. Near the same roof climb to a broken pillar and follow the path to a tower. At its top accessed by climbing a ladder you can get the Claw Talisman. If you carefully fall from near the eagle enemy to a dark area in the lower level that has a statue with a Somber Smithing Stone, you can use a Stonesword Key to access a room with the Hawk Crest Wooden Shield, Misericorde and Iron Whetblade. Back to the rooftops there is another level you can fall to near the eagle, which overlooks the courtyard. Following this path after a Banished Knight and a small bridge you will find the Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 10. If you keep moving forward through the roofs, you will find yourself behind the ballistas that guard the main gate. Once you open the main gate by the Site of Grace, talk to Gostoc to unlock him as a merchant. Sells a Bandit set, Caestus, Buckler.

      From the Rampart Tower Site of Grace head outside the nearest door to an area with enemy eagles. To the left there is a small alcove from which you can carefully drop down to the cliffs. To the bottom you will find a Marred Wooden Shield, a scarab with Ash of War: Storm Assault and a Crucible Knight enemy that drops Aspects of the Crucible: Horns. Back to the balcony with the eagles continue until you reach a church with Sorcerer Rogier who sells Ashes of War, including Ash of War: Glintstone Pebble, Ash of War: Carian Greatsword and Ash of War: Spinning Weapon. Continue until you reach a Banished Knight with a chest next to him that has Mimic's Veil. In the balcony before the Banished Knight, you can jump on some sacks and proceed until you find a chest with a Stonesword Key. In the kitchen after the Knight you can fight a Grafted Scion enemy that will not respawn upon its death. In the same room you can find a Highland Axe. If you enter a room with a pile of corpses near the kitchen, you can find the Chrysalid's Memento, which can be given to Roderika in Stormhill Shack. It is not necessary to give it to her, as defeating the castle's main boss will automatically advance her quest allowing you to keep the Memento in your inventory. But it is advised to give it to her now nonetheless to get some extra dialogue from Roderika.

      Past the room with the Grafted Scion once you exit to the courtyard, turn left and go underground to use a Stonesword Key. Here you can find a Godslayer's Seal and a Godskin Prayerbook that you can give to Corhyn in Roundtable for more Incantations, Black Flame and Black Flame Blade. Back in the courtyard one of the corpses has a Pike and on a wooden balcony you can find a Wooden Greatshield. In the room to the left of the Omen enemy you can find the Prophecy Painting. Go back to the courtyard and head towards the castle's main gate. You can reach it by going down a small slope past the mounted flamethrowers in the courtyard. In the next slope after some enemies you will be under attack by two ballistas. If you hug the right cliff as you descend and then jump on the cliffs, make your way towards the ballista to get an Arbalest.

      From the next Site of Grace past the Omen enemy, Liftside Chamber, use the lift and follow the path to find a scarab that drops Ash of War: Stormcaller. If you climb the platforms to the top of this area, you will reach a room with enemies and find the Manor Towershield beneath some stairs. Remember to open the door leading back to the Site of Grace to create a shortcut. Go to the lowest levels of the castle from the shortcut. Past a giant rat you can find a scarab with the Rancorcall. If you fall to the next area, you can fight an Ulcerated Tree Spirit that will drop a Golden Seed. In the same area you can loot a Stonesword Key and the Prince of Death's Pustule.

    44. Defeat  Godrick, the Grafted at the end of Stormveil Castle, who drops Remembrance of the Grafted & Godrick's Great Rune. In the rooms past his arena, you can find a Shabriri Grape.

      Talk to Nepheli Loux who has moved to the Hold to get the Arsenal Charm, then talk to Sir Gideon Ofnir in the room next to her about Nepheli.

(Unlocks  Great Rune)
    49. Return to where you met Varre outside the tutorial dungeon. In his place you will find a message and get the Bravo! gesture. This gesture is MISSABLE as when you meet him again in his next location, you will not be able to get this.

    Weeping Peninsula





    This region is located south of West Limgrave and is much smaller in comparison. This region covers the southern part of the Lands Between but has important upgrades and items.

    1. Your first order of business is to find the region's map. From the Bridge of Sacrifice follow the road heading south, past some enemies and through an opening in a wall. Keep following the road until you reach the base of a column with the region's map.
    2. Bridge of Sacrifice: Near the Site of Grace and to the south by some dead bodies you will find NPC Irina. Talk to her and accept to bring Irina's Letter to her father in Castle Morne. We'll do this at the end of our journey in the Peninsula.
    3. Head south down the road. In the back of a destroyed convoy you can find a Morning Star. A little further to the south by the road you will find a scarab that drops Ash of War: Mighty Shot.
    4. Near the near Site of Grace, Castle Morne Rampart, just before the hole in the wall that leads to the clearing with the region's map, you can find a Merchant that will sell Bastard Sword, Light Crossbow, Red Thorn Roundshield, Round Shield, an Iron set and the Crimson Amber Medallion. At nights you can fight another Night's Cavalry by the road who drops Ash of War: Barricade Shield and Nightrider Flail.
    5. Use the Spiritspring near the Site of Grace and jump to the bastion to the south near the broken wall, which has the Great Turtle Shield.
    6. Oridys's Rise: Use the Spiritspring again and head east. The tower's entrance is sealed and can be unlocked by finding and hitting three spirit turtles. The first is right behind you across the tower's entrance. The second is cleverly hidden in the bushes to the left of the tower's stairs. The last one is to the right of the entrance in the middle of a lake. This turtle is invisible so you must look carefully at the water to locate it. At the top of the tower you will find a Memory Stone in a chest. East of the tower you can find a huge bowl with a Starlight Shard.
    7. Impaler's Catacombs: North from Oridys's Rise, contains Prattling Pate (Please Help) and the Demi-Human Ashes that is dropped by Erdtree Guardian Watchdog boss. If you go to the cliff above the Catacombs, you can find a corpse on a chair with a Stonesword Key.
    8. Callu Baptismal Church: West of the Castle Morne Rampart Site of Grace at the top of the cliff. Contains The Flame of Frenzy and a Sacred Tear. Sacred Tears are used in Sites of Grace to increase the amount that your Crimson and Cerulean Flasks heal for your HP and FP respectively.
    9. Ailing Village: North of Callu Church. Contains Flame Crest Wooden Shield in a shack.
    10. West from the village, if you carefully follow the path to the cliffs heading south, you will end up in an area with a scarab protected by lightning strikes and Giant Bats that drops the Lightning Strike.
    11. Morne Tunnel: West from the village on the bottom of the canyon. Contains Rusted Anchor that is dropped by Scaly Misbegotten boss.
    12. Forest Lookout Tower: Located northeast from the Ailing Village. At the top of the tower you can find a Hand Ballista.
    13. Earthbore Cave: West from the Lookout Tower by the seaside. There is also a crystal tree at the southwest of the cave in the forest that can help you locate the cave. Once you interact with the tree, it will spawn a phantom, which will lead you to the cave. Contains Spelldrake Talisman that is dropped by Runebear boss.
    14. Demi-human Forest Ruins: West from the cave. Contains Demi-Human Queen's Staff and the Crystal Burst, both dropped by the Demi-Human Queen enemy. In an underground room to the southwest of the area you can find a chest with the Shield of The Guilty. North of the ruins on a huge bowl and guarded by some poison flowers, you can find the Faith-Knot Crystal Tear.
    15. Church of Pilgrimage: Northwest of the Ruins, at the northern part of the Peninsula. Contains Gilded Iron Shield and Sacred Tear. If you go to the cliffs to the north, you will find a ghost in a chair that will disappear once you get closer and give you the Warhawk Ashes. This ghost will appear only if you have found the Prophecy Painting in Stormveil Castle.
    16. Tombsward Catacombs: South of the Church of Pilgrimage. Contains Prattling Pate (Thank You), Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 9, which is accessed by using a Stonesword Key and Lhutel the Headless legendary ashes that is dropped by Cemetary Shade boss.
    17. Minor Erdtree: South from the catacombs. Fight the Erdtree Avatar boss to get the Opaline Bubbletear and Crimsonburst Crystal Tear for your Flask of Wondrous Physic, which we will get in the next region. If you head to the cliffs west from the Erdtree, carefully fall to the lower ledge to find a corpse with the Eclipse Crest Heater Shield.
    18. Tombsward Ruins: West from the catacombs. Contains the Winged Scythe underground in a chest. At the top of a ruin southwest from the underground room you can find a scarab with the Divine Fortification.
    19. Weeping Evergaol: West of the Ruins, requires a Stonesword Key to be unlocked. Defeat Ancient Hero of Zamor, who drops Ragadon's Scarseal.
    20. Fourth Church of Marika: West of the Evergaol. Contains a Sacred Tear.
    21. Tombsward Cave: Southeast from the church. Contains Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 8 and the Viridian Amber Talisman that is dropped by boss Miranda The Blighted Bloom.
    22. Witchbane Ruins: South of the Church in a poisonous swamp. In the underground you will find Ambush Shard and Sorceress Sellen again. Take note of her, as you will find more about this later.
    23. Isolated Merchant's Shack: Southwest of the Ruins. Sells Lantern, Zweihander and Lost Ashes of War. The latter is used to duplicate any Ash of War you currently have.
    24. Northeast of the Shack you will find a huge four-legged creature with a bell on its underbelly. The structure on the top of it is called a Mausoleum. What you need to do is attack the creature's legs to clear off the white skulls. Use Torrent for this as it often stomps its legs and can quickly kill you. Once it sits down, you will be able to enter the Mausoleum and be allowed to regain a charge for a Remembrance you already have obtained up to this point. By now you have only obtained the Remembrance of the Grafted, for which you can gain another charge. If you gain another charge, go to Roundtable Hold, talk to Finger Reader Enia and get the other weapon that you didn't choose earlier on, Axe of Godrick OR Grafted Dragon. You should read the following note, however, before you do this.

      *NOTE*: There is only a limited amount of Mausoleums in the game (each one can be used only ONCE per playthrough) and they are less than the total amount of Remembrances you will get in the game. You will have a total of 15 Remembrances as opposed to 7 Mausoleums, so you will have to choose which Remembrances you want to duplicate. The Mausoleums are split into two categories: The ones that have a bell on their underbelly and the ones that don't have one. There are 5 Mausoleums of the bell-bearing variant and 2 of the non bell-bearing variant. The ones with the bell can duplicate a Remembrance from any boss that drops a Remembrance, while the ones without a bell can replenish a charge for the Remembrances from only non-Shardbearer bosses. Finally, as for the Remembrances, you get 7 Remembrances from Shardbearer bosses and 8 from non-Shardbearer bosses.

      This means that during your first run you will not be able to get both rewards from 8 Remembrances in total. To get the second reward for the ones you decided not to gain another charge, you will have to go on a second playthrough. Choose wisely which ones you want for your first run. It is generally advised to use these Mausoleums for the Remembrances you get late in the game. Since you will have to go to a second playthrough anyway, if you decide you want to get everything in the game, it makes sense to duplicate the Remembrances you get near the end of the playthrough, so you have everything a bit faster during your second run.
    25. Tower of Return: Southeast from the Shack, this tower only features a trap chest at the top that will teleport you in Leyndell, Royal Capital. That area is generally too hard for this point of the game, but we can head there to get an item for now. Open the chest, stay in the smoke and you will go to Leyndell. There is luckily a Site of Grace right next to where you teleported, so rest there. At the end of the path you can find a Giant Golem and near it in a chest you can get the Blessed Dew Talisman.
    26. Back in the field where you got the region's map, in the northeastern part of the clearing and east from the column with the map, you can find a scarab that drops the Poison Mist. This scarab teleports further away when you get close to it, so use a ranged attack to kill it.
    27. West from the map's location at nights you can fight another Deathbird boss near some ruins, which drops a Sacrificial Axe. South from the Deathbird you can find a Golden Seed near a gold tree.
    28. Castle Morne: Found at the southern part of the region. In the building behind the huge pile of corpses with enemies on top, you can find a chest with a Claymore. Climbing the ladder past this building will lead you to an area with a Steel-Wire Torch. Back in the courtyard go to the very back past a Mad Pumpkin Head enemy and climb the ladder. Before you drop to the next Site of Grace, look to the left and find a ladder that will lead you to an area where enemies are fighting. Past this area on top of a bastion you can find NPC Edgar, who is Irina's father. Talk to him to get a Sacrificial Twig and then deliver Irina's letter to him.

      After the Site of Grace Behind the Castle if you drop to a platform by the cliffs you can get a Stonesword Key. At the top of the tower past the bridge you can find a chest with the Twinblade Talisman. Falling down to the lower levels of the area, you will end up in an area with rats and a Misbegotten enemy, where you can find a Whip.
    29. At the end of the area defeat  Leonine Misbegotten boss, who drops the legendary armament Grafted Blade Greatsword.
    30. With your new sword you can talk to Edgar again. Then go back to Irina's location and talk with Edgar once again. We will finish his quest in another region. If you defeated the castle's boss without talking to Edgar beforehand, then simply talk to him after defeating the boss, deliver the letter and then go to Irina's place. The only thing you will miss if you forgot to talk to him, is some dialogue after you defeat the boss and the ability to summon him for Leonine Misbegotten.

    East Limgrave




    As the name suggests, this region is located to the east of the game's first region. Contrary to its west counterpart the east side is much smaller in comparison, only having a few points of interest. In order to have a visual representation of the area, you want to get the map first.

    1. From the Waypoint Ruins in West Limgrave, follow the road north and then heading east. Follow the road ignoring any locations and NPCs you may see/hear and keep going until the road turns south. A little later you will be close to another Minor Erdtree. Just before the tree on the road and at the bottom of the pillar you can find this region's map.
    2. From the Saintsbridge Site of Grace in West Limgrave, cross the bridge and you will find a merchant to the right, who sells Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 3, Halberd, Bandit Mask.
    3. Summonwater Village: From the merchant, follow the road to the east. On your way to the left side of the road you can talk to NPC D. This NPC is also found in Roundtable Hold. If you talked to him in the Hold about the Deathroots, then he will not appear here. The village contains the Green Turtle Talisman in an underground room, accessed by using a Stonesword Key and also the Skeletan Militiaman Ashes & Deathroot, both of which are dropped by boss Tibia Mariner. After defeating the boss, D will automatically move back to Roundtable.
    4. Southeast from the village carefully descend the cliffs until you see a Spiritspring. At its base you can find a scarab with Ash of War: Sacred Blade.
    5. Smoldering Church: Follow the road eastwards from the village. Outside you will be invaded by Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater. Drops Sacred Scorpion Charm. Inside the church you can find Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 14 and Missionary's Cookbook 3.
    6. At the road you followed to get this region's map, you will find the Mistwood Outskirts Site of Grace. Next to it (to the northeast as you follow the road) there is an overpass with NPC Kenneth Haight at the top asking for help. For now talk to him and agree to help him.
    7. Artist's Shack: West from Kenneth Haight on top of a cliff. Inside you can find the Homecoming Painting. With the painting in your possession go southwest from the Dragon-Burnt Ruins in Lake Agheel in West Limgrave to find a ghost in front of a graveyard. When you get close to the ghost, it will disappear and leave the Incantation Scarab.
    8. Northeast from Kenneth you can find a graveyard with many sarcophagi. On top of a sarcophagus you can find a corpse with Fevor's Cookbook 1.
    9. Third Church of Marika: Located east of Kenneth Haight when the road turns south. Contains a Sacred Tear, the Flask of Wondrous Physick and the Crimson Crystal Tear.
    10. West from the map's location, you can find the Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 4 next to a Runebear enemy scratching a tree.
    11. Mistwood Ruins: South of where you picked up the map. There is also a crystal tree in the road that, once interacted with, will spawn a phantom that will lead you to the ruins. Contains the Axe Talisman underground. To the south of the ruins on a ledge overlooking the area, you can find a Sacrificial Twig in a graveyard guarded by poison flowers and skeletons.

      In the ruins you will hear a howl. When you hear it, go back to the Church of Elleh in West Limgrave and talk to the merchant Kale. Talk about the howling and he will give you the Finger Snap gesture. Return to the ruins, using the new gesture when you hear the howl. This will make NPC Blaidd to appear. Talk to him, exhaust the dialogue and then head to Forlorn Hound Evergaol in West Limgrave to find him again. Talk to him once more. His quest will be continued later.
    12. Minor Erdtree: Near its base you can find more Tears for your Flask of Wondrous Physick on a huge bowl, the Spiked Cracked Tear and the Greenspill Crystal Tear.
    13. Southwest of the Minor Erdtree you will find a scarab with Ash of War: Ground Slam
    14. South of the Erdtree you can find a merchant that sells Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 5, Armorer's Cookbook 3, Hand Axe, Riveted Wooden Shield, Blue-Gold Kite Shield.
    15. Fort Haight: At the southern part of the region. Kill all enemies in the fort. Contains Golden Seed outside the Fort by a gold tree, the Dectus Medallion (Left) in a chest, Ash of War: Bloody Slash (dropped by the knight at the top) and the Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 6 in a chest.
    16. After you clear the fort, go back to Kenneth Haight. He rewards you with the Erdsteel Dagger. If you accept to join him, he will move to the fort, where you should go and talk to him again. We'll finish his quest later near the end of the game.
    17. If you go to the Roundtable Hold, talk with NPC D you met on your way to the Summonwater Village. Show him the Deathroots you have and he will mark a place in your map where you can use them. This mark is right next to the Third Church of Marika. Go there and teleport to a location we will cover later as it is way too difficult for this point of the game. For now open the doors in front of you, touch the Site of Grace and talk with the Beast Clergyman. Offer him the two Deathroots you have collected by now (offer ONE AT A TIME as the interaction may be bugged and you may not receive all rewards) to get the Beast Eye, which will notify you once you are close to another Deathroot, the Clawmark Seal and Bestial Sling.
    18. By now you can see in your map that a big chunk of the area past the Smoldering Church to the east is uncovered. Officially, however, it belongs to another region so it will be covered a bit later. Instead go back to the Minor Erdtree in East Limgrave and to the north you will find the Siofra River Well. Enter the building and use the lift to go underground.

    Siofra River (Underground)





    The underground runs beneath the Lands Between (duh) and is also quite big, although not nearly as much as the overland area. There are 5 maps you can obtain in the underground, which means 5 regions. However, there are multiple levels in each of these regions to the point where we will have to split some regions to multiple parts. The first map will show the Siofra River region, however a big part of this place can be accessed from another entrance point later on. For now we will cover the lower levels of the region.

    1. In the beginning of the area you can use Torrent to climb to the higher levels of the structure in front of you. You will have to parkour a bit to move higher but you will occasionally reach a platform with two mage Claymen enemies that throw some magic bubbles at you. At the back of this platform, use Torrent to carefully fall down the ledge below, then roll carefully to the column and kill the scarab to get Oracle Bubbles.
    2. Move on the back of the area to access another elevator. Keep moving upstream until you reach a huge open area under the starry sky. Touch the Siofra River Bank Site of Grace there and move on the pillar at the base of the stairs nearby to grab this region's map.
    3. West of the Site on top of the small cliff, you can find two Minotaur Spirit enemies in a small camp. A corpse in this camp has the Armorer's Cookbook 6.
    4. South of the Site you can climb to another cliff and past three Minotaur Spirit enemies you can find a corpse with the Inverted Hawk Heater Shield.
    5. Under the stairs where you picked the map, use Torrent to jump on the platform to find the Horn Bow.
    6. Following the cliff from these stairs you will soon reach a Spiritspring. Riding it to the broken bridge to the east, you can find a Stonesword Key at the very end.
    7. Moving north up the river you will find a scaffold at the base of the pillar of a bridge in the middle of the region. If you climb the ladders and move to the back in the caves you can find a merchant, who sells Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 17, Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 18, Shotel. He also sells Stonesword Keys and he is one of the merchants that sells Larval Tears. The latter is an important item used to respec your character after we defeat a boss a little later, so take note of this location in case you ever want to change your character's build/stats.
    8. East of the scaffold you used and beneath the bridge you can find the Worshipper's Woods Site of Grace. Northwest of the Site at the edge of a cliff you can find a scarab that drops the Ash of War: Square Off.
    9. North of the scarab you can cross a bridge heading towards the cliffs. Past the bridge you can find a Golden Seed by a gold tree. South of the seed, if you use Torrent and carefully jump at the column, then follow the beam to the next column you can find a Stonesword Key. The path to the north of the Golden Seed leads to a cave with a Rune Arc. Upon use this item enables the special effects of a Great Rune that you have equipped until you die. For example Godrick's Great Rune increases your total stats when using a Rune Arc. Once you die, this stat increase will stop until you use another Rune Arc.
    10. At the north end of the area near an elevator that takes you to Deep Siofra Well, you will find Clarifying Horn Charm at the stairs. The lift can be activated by using 2 Stonesword Keys. At the top of the new area touch the Site of Grace and return underground. We'll cover that area later.
    11. In the lower part of the Siofra River you can find three waygates. The waygate closest to the Siofra River Bank Site of Grace is linked with the waygate the furthest away from it. The second waygate is found in-between these two and to the eastern part of the area. South of the Worshipper's Woods Site of Grace and on top of a ruined structure guarded by a bow-wielding Minotaur Spirit enemy. This waygate teleports to the highest area of the region that you can reach at this point.
    12. In the southwest part of this elevated area enter a building that has a lot of Claymen enemies guarding a scarab that drops Great Oracular Bubble.
    13. In the center of the elevated area you will find the Dragonkin Soldier boss. Drops Dragon Halberd.
    14. Past the boss hidden under a waterfall to the east is Marika's Scarseal.
    15. Return to the lower level. Across the region you have to find and light up all 8 obelisks in order to fight the  Ancestor Spirit. Once you find them, interact with them to light a fire. Their locations can be seen in the map above, indicated by the X. When you find all of them, return to the stairs where you found the map. Touch the skeleton at this area to be transferred to the boss room. Once defeated, it will drop the Ancestral Follower Ashes.

    Liurnia East







    This region is located directly north from West Limgrave and it is accessed by passing through the Stormhill Castle after defeating Godrick or if you take a small path near the broken bridge east of the castle. This is another huge region in the game with a lot of important locations and items.

    1. To find the region's map, you need to ride with Torrent west after you enter the region from inside the castle and then go north until you reach the water. The map will be located at the base of a pillar in a small forest in the middle of the swampy lake a little to the north.
    2. Next to the region's first Site of Grace, Lake-Facing Cliffs, you can find NPC Hyetta. Give her the Shabriri Grape and she will give you the As You Wish gesture.
    3. At the Site you will also find NPC Boc after you rest there. Exhaust the dialogue with him.
    4. Northwest from Hyetta and Boc in a graveyard, you can find the Academy Scroll. Give it to Sorceress Sellen to unlock new Sorceries, Great Glintstone Shard and Swift Glintstone Shard.
    5. Church of Irith: South from Hyetta. Inside you can find a Sacred Tear and NPC Thops who teaches Sorceries, including Glintstone Pebble, Glintstone Arc and Starlight.
    6. As you head towards the water level you will pass through a camp, where you can find the Glintstone Craftsman's Cookbook 1.
    7. At the water level by the Site of Grace Liurnia Lake Shore you can find a merchant who sells Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 11, Estoc, Astrologer's Staff, Kite Shield and the Astrologer set.
    8. Stillwater Cave: South end of the swampy lake, which you can reach once you get to water level. Contains the Sage set and the Winged Sword Insignia that is dropped by boss Cleanrot Knight.
    9. Malefactor's Evergaol: A little north from the Liurnia Lake Shore Site of Grace you will find a Spiritspring. Climb to the cliffs to the west to find the Evergaol and enter the area to fight Adan, Thief of Fire. Drops the legendary Incantation Flame of the Fell God.
    10. Follow the road leading west from the Evergaol. At the very end you will find an enemy camp which has the Fire Spur Me gesture and the Fire Monks' Prayerbook. Give it to NPC Corhyn in the Roundtable Hold to unlock new Incantations, O Flame! and Surge, O Flame!
    11. Lakeside Crystal Cave: Approach the cliff with the Evergaol and the Prayerbook from the north on the water level. Inside you can find Spear Talisman and Cerulean Amber Medallion that is dropped by Bloodhound Knight boss.
    12. Slumbering Wolf's Shack: This shack is found past the boss room in the Crystal Cave. We need an item to start a quest here, so for now take note of this location and we will return later.
    13. Laskyar Ruins: Northwest from the map's location. Here you can find Glintstone Craftsman's Cookbook 2 near the Site of Grace. The waygate here teleports you to the main entrance of Raya Lucaria Academy in this region. We have a lot of stuff to find in-between so there is no reason to use it. In a chest underground you can find the Wraith Calling Bell.
    14. Northwest from Laskyar Ruins at the edge of the region you will find Patches as a merchant along with the Scenic Isle Site of Grace. We'll use this Site as a point of reference for some locations later.
    15. Purified Ruins: Northeast from the region's map. Contains Shabriri Grape and Two Fingers Heirloom. West of the ruins you'll find Hyetta again, give her the Sabriri Grape you just picked. The waygate north of the ruins also leads to Raya Lucaria Academy, so you can ignore it.
    16. Cliffbottom Catacombs: East of the ruins down the cliff. Carefully descend with Torrent. Contains Prattling Pate (Wonderful), Nox Mirrorhelm that requires a Stonesword Key to be accessed, a Scythe, Page Ashes and Kaiden Sellsword Ashes that is dropped by Erdtree Burial Watchdog boss.
    17. Get back up the cliff and follow the road all the way to the southeast to find Treespear in a chest in the back of a destroyed convoy.
    18. North of the Purified Ruins on the road you can find a Site of Grace called Liurnia Highway North. A little more to the north you can find a Lucerne near some enemies.
    19. Highway Lookout Tower: North of Liurnia Highway North Site of Grace. Contains Carian Glintblade Staff and the Glintstone Craftsman's Cookbook 3.
    20. In the forest north of the Lookout Tower, patrolling the road you can fight another Night's Cavalry boss during the night. Drops Ash of War: Ice Spear.
    21. West from the Night's Cavalry fight you can find the Gate Town Bridge Site of Grace. Nearby you can find Hyetta again. We don't have a Grape to give her now, so take note of this location for later.
    22. Northeast from the Night's Cavalry up in the cliffs that can be accessed from the Liurnia Highway North Site of Grace you can fight another Tibia Mariner boss. Drops Skeletal Bandit Ashes and a Deathroot that you can give to Beast Clergyman for Bestial Vitality.
    23. Jarburg: East of the Tibia Mariner, down the cliffs. To access this village you need to carefully descend down the cliffs via the platforms with Torrent. Once in the village, rest at the Site of Grace for easy access and talk to NPC Jar-Bairn. Keep reloading the area and talk to him until you exhaust the dialogue.
    24. North from the Tibia Mariner you can fight a knight patrolling the road who drops Dragon Cult Prayerbook. Give it to NPC Corhyn in the Roundtable Hold to unlock new Incantations, Lightning Spear, Honed Bolt and Electrify Armament.
    25. Artist's Shack: North from the patrolling knight. Contains the Resurrection Painting. We will find the painting's location in another region.
    26. From the Gate Town Bridge Site of Grace head north on the lower level following the cliffs. You will soon come across a lot of poisonous blobs. The corpse near the pillar next to them has the Land Squirt Ashes.
    27. Carian Study Hall: On the eastern part of the region. Follow the cliffs from the blobs to find a path north from the Artist's Shack leading to the building. Contains Carian Glintstone Staff, Magic Downpour (dropped by enemy Preceptor Miriam) and Cerulean Seed Talisman at the wooden beams/rafters at the top of the area. Ranged combat is highly recommended to kill Miriam as he can be very annoying to hunt as a melee character.
    28. Head north from the Artist's Shack to find an unnamed Flame Monk camp past the canyon. Contains Flame, Cleanse Me. A little west from the camp you can find some an enemy by some fire slugs who will drop Briars of Sin.
    29. Ainsel River Well: Northeast from the unnamed camp. Guarded by a stone giant. Take note of this place as we will return here shortly. Head north past the Well's entrance to find a Stonesword Key on a corpse sitting on a chair.
    30. Church of Vows: West from the Well. Contains Gold Sewing Needle and Golden Tailoring Tools. Here you can also find NPC Miriel, Pastor of Vows, who sells Sorceries and Incantations, including Magic Glintblade, Carian Greatsword and Blessing's Boon. Any scrolls or books that can be given to Sellen and Corhyn can also be given to Miriel, supposing you haven't already given them to the other NPCs. This Church is important as here you can perform the Absolution ritual. If you have at any point attacked and made an enemy of an NPC or if you have killed some merchant NPCs, you can use a Celestial Dew here and make peace with these NPCs and revive the merchants.

      NOTE: This mechanic does not work for every NPC in the game. Some NPCs, once killed, will forever remain dead until you start another playthrough.

      During the nights in this church you can find a Bell Bearing Hunter, which drops the Meat Peddler's Bell Bearing. You can take this one as well to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable. If for some reason the Hunter does not spawn, you can rest at the Site of Grace outside the Church, progress the time to Nightfall, leave the Site of Grace and then rest again. This should spawn the enemy boss when you enter the church.
    31. Raya Lucaria Crystal Tunnel: Go back down to water level. The dungeon is located north of the church. Contains Crystal Knife, Shatter Earth and the Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing 1 that is dropped by Crystalian Ring Blade boss, which you can take to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold. This is an important bell as it will allow you to buy levels 1 & 2 of Smithing Stones, materials required for upgrading your normal armaments. Ignore the bell-bearing Mausoleum found outside the Tunnel for now.
    32. Northwest of the Ainsel River Well, on the higher level you can find the Ruined Labyrinth Site of Grace. Northwest from the Site and directly south from a non bell-bearing Mausoleum you can find the Spiralhorn Shield on a corpse.
    33. Ignore the Mausoleum and head north to touch the Site of Grace Mausoleum Compound. Head west from there, ignoring the second non bell-bearing Mausoleum. Hug the cliff to your left until you find a hole in the wall. Go through it and to the left to find a corpse with the Stalwart Horn Charm.
    34. Minor Erdtree: East from the Mausoleum Compound Site of Grace. You can defeat the boss Erdtree Avatar to get the Magic-Shrouding Cracked Tear, Lightning-Shrouding Cracked Tear and Holy-Shrouding Cracked Tear for your Flask of Wondrous Physick.
    35. Black Knife Catacombs: Located in the northeastern part of the region and can be accessed by taking the road east from the Ruined Labyrinth Site of Grace. You will also have a map showing this location if you started Rogier's questline and you talked with Fia. Contains Rosus' Axe, which is accessed by using a Stonesword Key and the Twinsage Sorcerer Ashes that is dropped by Cemetary Shade boss. The chest in the boss' room also has a Deathroot that you should take to Beast Clergyman to get Ash of War: Beast's Roar.

      This is one of the few dungeons that features a second boss. After you have used the guillotines to reach the higher level, you will reach a room where you have to drop down a hole to pull the dungeon's lever. If you attack the wall you face as you enter the room, it will disappear and you can fight Black Knife Assassin boss, who drops the Assassin's Cerulean Dagger and the Black Knifeprint, which is required for Rogier's questline. You can also summon D to help you in this boss fight.
    36. Head back to Roundtable Hold and talk with Rogier. Reload the area and talk with him again. His quest will be continued a little later.
    37. West of Raya Lucaria Crystal Tunnel at the base of the cliff you can find a Spiritspring which will allow you to access the northern part of the region. Interact with the East Raya Lucaria Gate Site of Grace. Once you rest there, Boc will appear, to whom you should talk.
    38. Bellum Church: Northeast from the Site of Grace by a forest. Contains a Sacred Tear. On your way there patrolling the road during the nights is a Night's Cavalry boss, which drops Ash of War: Giant Hunt and Nightrider Glaive.
    39. Follow the path to the lower level through the church to find a merchant. Sells Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 13, Composite Bow, Rift Shield, Blue Crest Heater Shield.
    40. Continue northeast from the church to find an unnamed enemy camp where catapults are shooting at you. At the right side of the camp on the higher level you can find a chest with a Great Mace.
    41. Grand Lift of Decus: Northeast from the camp. Interact with the Site of Grace at the bottom for easy access later. You cannot use the lift yet, so we will return later.
    42. Frenzy-Flaming Tower: South from the Grand Lift. You can easily spot it as there is frequently a huge fireball at the top. This quickly rises your Madness level so quickly get to the top and kill the enemies. Contains Howl of Shabriri.
    43. Frenzied Flame Village: South from the tower. Contains Shabriri's Woe, a Stonesword Key at the top of the walls and the Frenzied's Cookbook 1.
    44. Church of Inhibition: West from the tower but accessed after passing through the village. Contains a Finger Maiden set and a Sacred Tear. Outside you will be invaded by Festering Fingerprint Vyke, who drops a Fingerprint Grape and Vyke's War Spear.
    45. Southeast from the church is a clearing with a scarab surrounded by rats. The scarab drops Frenzied Burst.
    46. To the northeast end of the region in the elevated part you will find a Troll Knight enemy that usually drops Ash of War: Giant Hunt. If, however, you have defeated the Night's Cavalry near Bellum Church, then you will already have this ash of war, so this troll will not drop a second one.
    47. Converted Fringe Tower: South from the Troll Knight. Take note of the location for now, as you will need an item we will get in the Academy to unlock the entrance.

    Once you are done with this region, head back to Ainsel River Well and ride the elevator to head underground once more.

    Ainsel River Part 1 (Underground)





    The second underground region that we will explore, Ainsel River, will be covered in two parts. Not because of its length as it is smaller than Siofra River, but because more than half of this region cannot be accessed yet. It will become available from another location later in the story. This part is pretty straightforward as there are not too many things you can get at the moment. It can be accessed by riding the lift from Ainsel River Well.

    1. From the region's first Site of Grace, Ainsel River Well Depths, follow the river past some ant nests. Nothing unique can be found here.
    2. Uhl Palace Ruins: You will soon reach the ruins after the tunnels. You can reach it by following two paths. Regardless on the path you chose, find the elevator and use the Site of Grace Ainsel River Sluice Gate at the top level.
    3. Past the first building you will enter another open area where a huge bug-enemy will be throwing magic towards you. You cannot reach it yet, so head to the building beneath it while taking cover. In this building you will find this region's map on a corpse. In the same building you will also find a merchant, who will sell Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 16, Perfumer's Cookbook 4 and a Prisoner set. This merchant also sells Celestial Dew (for the Absolution ritual) and Lost Ashes of War (for duplicating any Ash of War), so take note of him if you need any.
    4. Follow the river to the west to enter more tunnels with ant nests. In this area you will find Immunizing Horn Charm.
    5. Continue northwards until you fight  Dragonkin Soldier of Nokstella. Drops Frozen Lightning Spear. The chest beneath the huge statue has a Great Ghost Glovewort, the material you need to upgrade a renowned Ashen Remain to the max level.

    This is all that can be done for now in this region, so we will now return back to the surface and continue exploring Liurnia.

    Liurnia North





    The next region to visit is naturally the northern part of the Liurnia Lake, as it is the next accessible region from its eastern counter-part. This area features a major location in the game, Raya Lucaria Academy, so we will clear the surrounding areas and leave the Academy for the end.

    1. The map is quite easy to get; from the Scenic Isle Site of Grace, where you found Patches as a merchant, head directly north until you reach the Academy Gate Town. Near the Academy Gate Town Site of Grace you will find the pillar with this region's map.
    2. Head east from Patches to a structure near the telescope to find NPC Rya. Talk to her and agree to help her find her necklace.
    3. A little to the northwest from Rya during the nights you can fight another Deathbird boss. This one drops the Red-Feathered Branchsword.
    4. Boilprawn Shack: Northwest from the Deathbird boss. Here you can find NPC Blackguard Big Boggart, who is the one holding Rya's necklace. You can either kill him to get the necklace (NOT ADVISED TO DO SO) or buy Rya's Necklace from him for 1000 Runes. He is also the only NPC that will sell the Boiled Prawn consumable item. You should buy some from him to progress his quest, then talk to him and get the Spread Out gesture. Take the Necklace back to Rya and she will give you a Volcano Manor Invitation.
    5. Southwest from the shack you can find the Dexterity-knot Crystal Tear for your Flask in a huge bowl on a small island. Then head west from this small island around the rocks to find the Folly On The Lake Site of Grace.
    6. Rose Church: Northwest from the Folly On The Lake Site of Grace, the church is on an island. You can talk to NPC Varre here again to continue his quest. In the church you can get the Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 12.

      To continue with Varre's quest, you will need to engage the Multiplayer part of the game. If you are not interested, you can ignore it but you will miss out on a few items. You will have to use the Festering Bloody Fingers that Varre gave you and invade other players' worlds by leaving a red sign on the ground and waiting to be summoned. You need to do this 3 times and it is not important if you win or lose the encounter. You can even use the Finger Severer as soon as you join and it will still count. Talk with Varre again once you are done and he will give you the Lord of Blood's Favor. Go to the Church of Inhibition that was covered in Liurnia East region. Go to the dead maiden there and interact with her body to soak the Lord of Blood's Favor with her blood.

      Return to Varre. He will give you the Bloody Finger, which has the same use as the Festering Bloody Finger, only you can use this one an unlimited amount of times. Talk with him again to get the Pureblood Knight's Medal. Using this item will teleport you to a region underground, called Mohgwyn Palace. This region is incredibly difficult and should be tackled near the end of the game. We will return here much later to finish Varre's quest and to explore the palace.

      Patch 1.06: As of this patch, Varre has got an alternative way to complete his quest without needing to ever touch the Multiplayer, making all items obtainable while playing offline. If you want to try to the offline method, then you have to get the Festering Bloody Fingers from him and continue playing until a later region, Altus Plateau, where we will continue his quest.
    7. Northwest from the church you can find a scarab with Bloodflame Blade.
    8. Temple Quarter: Northwest from the church. Contains Icerind Hatchet in a chest to the south.
    9. Northwest from the Quarter you will find an island and fight the Glintstone Dragon Smarag, who drops a Dragon Heart. Also guards the Academy Glintstone Key. You can take the Heart back to the Church of Dragon Communion in West Limgrave to purchase the second Incantation.
    10. Academy Crystal Cave: Northeast from the dragon. You can enter by using 2 Stonesword Keys. Contains another Stonesword Key, the Crystal Staff, which is accessed by following a path from an illusory wall. The wall is located to the left side of the tunnel right after you open a wooden door. Also has the Crystal Release, dropped by bosses Crystalian Staff & Crystalian Spear. It is advised to focus on one boss at a time preferably the Spear enemy first. Enter the elevator after the bosses and follow the path to the top to get Terra Magicus.
    11. Testu's Rise: Northeast from the Crystal Cave on an island. To unlock its entrance find and hit the three spirit turtles on the island. The first turtle is found to the north of the tower near some cliffs.The second one is to the south below some cliffs. The last and hardest to find is on top of a tree east of the tower. The tower contains a Memory Stone.
    12. North from the Academy Gate Town that you found the region's map you can find the Gate Town North Site of Grace. To the south of the Site during the nights you can fight the Death Rite Bird boss, which drops the Ancient Death Rancor. South from the boss you can find a Stonesword Key in a chest, on one of the small towers.
    13. Academy Gate Town: The area that you found the region's map. North from the Site of Grace you can find NPC Diallos on top of a roof, to whom you talked before in Roundtable Hold. Exhaust the dialogue with him, then go back to Roundtable Hold and talk with him again. In the middle of the area, on top of a tower surrounded by enemies you can find Glintstone Craftsman's Cookbook 4 in a chest. A little to the west you can find a Golden Seed by a gold tree. On the western side of the lower level beneath an arch with some poisonous blobs, find the scarab at the ceiling that has Ash of War: Charge Forth. Continue to the higher level and to the west part, where an enemy is patrolling alone, you can find the Blue-White Wooden Shield.

      At the end of the path you will find the main entrance to Raya Lucaria Academy. Near it you can find the Meeting Place Map, which shows the location of the key you need to pass through the barrier. We already got the key after defeating the dragon Smarag, so there's no need to go there again.
    14. Raya Lucaria Academy: Use the Academy Glintstone Key to go through the barrier. At the starting location head down the path to the southeast to find a merchant that sells the Fanged Imp Ashes, Fevor's Cookbook 2, Lost Ashes of War, and a Warrior set. Back to the top go towards the broken bridge to the northeast and find a Golden Seed by a gold tree and a red sign on the ground, which allows you to be summoned by Yura to help him fight Bloody Finger Ravenmount Assassin. Once you defeat the enemy, you will get Ash of War: Raptor of the Mists. Make sure to also talk to Yura, who can be found where his summoning sign was. Use the lift to enter the academy's main area.

      In the graveyard area find the Marionette Soldier Ashes on a cliff with two Marionette enemies. As you progress through the graveyard, you can jump down a ledge to the right to find a Carian Knight set near some praying enemies. At the end of the graveyard find a scarab below a cliff to the left with Ash of War: Spectral Lance. In the area past the conveyor lift fight the enemy who drops the Gravity Well. Take the lift to reach the top and interact with the Schoolhouse Classroom Site of Grace. Return back to the lift and use it to reach a lower cliff where you can find the Avionette Soldier Ashes and then ride it to the bottom to find the Longtail Cat Talisman. The Iron Maiden in this area can teleport you to Mt. Gelmir if it grabs you, which is very hard for this point of the game, so try to avoid getting caught. You can also use sneak here by hugging the left wall to grab the talisman to avoid confronting the Iron Maiden.

      Back at the top of the lift and past the Schoolhouse Classroom Site of Grace, turn left in the hallway to find the Conspectus Scroll on a corpse, which you can give to Sellen or Miriel for more sorceries (Glintstone Cometshard and Star Shower), and the Glintstone Craftsman's Cookbook 5 in a chest. Attack the wall behind the corpse to reveal a path leading to Olivinus Glintstone Crown. Right before the boss room attack a library/wall to the left to reach an area with a Stonesword Key, Comet and Graven School Talisman. Fight the  Red Wolf of Radagan boss, which drops a Memory Stone.

      After the boss continue forward and when you reach the courtyard, take a sharp turn to the right to the corner of the balcony. Fall to the ledge below, go up the ladder and in the building via the broken window. To the right is a chest with the legendary talisman Radagon Icon. In the courtyard you can find a Stonesword Key on the fountain right in the middle of the courtyard. If you climb from the rubble to the left you can enter a room with the Glintstone Whetblade. To the left of the courtyard you can find a Golden Seed by a gold tree and the Karolos Glintstone Crown, dropped by the crystal crab in a corner further to the left from the tree. From the room with the Whetblade, if you go to the higher level and jump off the ledge, you can open a door to create a shortcut to the courtyard. From this area follow the path and go to the rooftops.

      In the rooftops area head towards the southwestern side of the rooftops and you will climb a ladder that leads to a battlemage. On the rooftops to the west of the area and past some Marionette enemies, you can climb a big ladder to reach the top of a bell tower. This tower has a chest with the Full Moon Crossbow. Moving on past the bell tower you can enter inside the next tower and then carefully fall through the holes on the wooden floor. A few levels below you can find a crystal crab that will drop the Lazuli Glintstone Crown. As you progress further into the rooftops you can find an Imbued Sword Key on a corpse on top of another tower that you have to jump to reach. Near the end of the rooftops area, you will reach the rooftop of the first area/church you entered in the Academy. If you fall carefully from the beams to the chandelier you can find another Academy Glintstone Key. The ledges below have the Shattering Crystal and if you exit to the balcony in the corner you can get a Twinsage Glintstone Crown from a crystal crab. Another room from the church's higher level leads to a room full of crystals and an enemy that guards Azur's Glintstone Staff.

      Back to the courtyard move on to the academy's final building by jumping to the beams on the right. Run past the boulders to fight Moongrum, Carian Knight who drops Carian Knight's Shield. In the gate to the left of this area that has a sorcerer you can jump to another building to take a ladder leading to Glintstone Scarab in a chest guarded by a Mad Pumpkin Head enemy and many sorcerers that were throwing the giant boulders at you.

      Move on the lift past Moongrum to fight  Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon. She will drop the Remembrance of the Full Moon Queen and the Great Rune of the Unborn. This Great Rune does not need to be activated or equipped, as it allows you to respec your character by talking to Rennala. You need to give her a Larval Tear to be able to go through the Rebirth ritual, which is quite handy if you want to re-distribute your stats to your liking or change your overall build. Doing this ritual for the first time will give you the Balled Up gesture.

      We will now clean up some stuff in the areas we have unlocked so far.
    15. Go back to Thops in Church of Irith and give him the Academy Glintstone Key you picked from the rooftops in the Academy to get the Erudition gesture. Then go to find him in the academy outside the building with the Schoolhouse Classroom Site of Grace to get Thops' Bell Bearing (which you can give to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable), Thops' Barrier and Academy Glintstone Staff. Return to the church where he originally was and find a scarab there that drops Ash of War: Thops' Barrier.
    16. In the Roundtable Hold you can now talk to D to get Incantations from him, Litany of Proper Death and Order's Blade. Talking to Corhyn will reveal that he's thinking of leaving the Hold to look for Goldmask. Talk to Finger Maiden Enia to get a Talisman Pouch and use the Remembrance of the Full Moon you got. Choose from either Carian Regal Scepter OR Rennala's Full Moon. You can also buy Rennala's set from her.
    17. Go back to Raya Lucaria Crystal Tunnel. To the west you can find a bell-bearing Mausoleum that we ignored earlier. To access this one you need to climb on the cliffs above via the Spiritspring and use ranged attacks to clear the skulls near the door. Once it sits down, you can enter and choose if you want to gain another charge for Rennala's remembrance to get what you didn't get earlier; Carian Regal Scepter OR Rennala's Full Moon.
    18. Return to the Converted Fringe Tower. In order to unlock the entrance you need to wear one of the Glintstone Crowns you picked in Raya Lucaria Academy, stand in front of the statue and use the Erudition gesture. Contains Cannon of Haima and Gavel of Haima.
    19. From the East Gate Bridge Tresle Site of Grace go under the bridge to find a scarab that will drop the Ash of War: Shield Bash.

    Liurnia West Part 1







    Before we move on with the main story, we will explore the western and last part of the Liurnia Lake. This region will be done in two sections as the elevated area of its southwestern part will be accessed after we progress an NPC's questline.

    1. From Testu's Rise in Liurnia Lake head north-northwest to find the region's map at the base of a pillar.
    2. South from the map follow the road until you meet a convoy at the back of which you can get Carian Knight's Sword in a chest.
    3. Heading south you can find the Jellyfish Shield surrounded by some Jellyfish spirits.
    4. The Four Belfries: Found further south down the road in an elevated area. In this area at the very top tower you can find an Imbued Sword Key. These keys can be used in the remaining three towers to access remote areas that cannot be reached otherwise. You currently have 2 out of 3, so let's visit what we can for now.
    5. The second tower from the top takes you back to the Chapel of Anticipation, the game's first area. If you didn't defeat it the first time, take revenge on the Grafted Scion boss and you will finally get the Ornamental Straight Sword and the Golden Beast Crest Shield. Moving on to where you started the game you can also find a door which was locked before and past it you can get The Stormhawk King and Stormhawk Deenh.
    6. The third tower from the top takes you back underground to the Siofra River (although the game tells you it's the Nokron City). Here you can find a Mottle Necklace and a Crucible Knight enemy that drops nothing.
    7. Cuckoo's Evergaol: South from the belfries. Contains Greatblade Phalanx that is dropped by the boss Bols, Carian Knight.
    8. Revenger's Shack: Further south down the road. If you have completed Irina's and Edgar's questline in the Weeping Peninsula region, you will be invaded by Edgar the Revenger, who drops a Shabriri Grape and Banished Knight's Halberd +8. This concludes their questline.
    9. Take the Shabriri Grape to Hyetta, who can be found by the Gate Town Bridge Site of Grace in Liurnia East. Exhaust all the dialogue with her, use the Site to rest, then talk to her again. You will then find her at Bellum Church in Liurnia North. Give her the Fingerprint Grape. That is all we can do for her quest at the moment.
    10. Minor Erdtree: South from the shack, fight another Erdtree Avatar boss that drops Cerulean Crystal Tear and Ruptured Crystal Tear for your Flask of Wondrous Physick.
    11. West of the tree at the lower level you can find a scarab with Ash of War: Sword Dance.
    12. Road's End Catacombs: South from the Erdtree at the lower level down the road with the scarab. At the end of the catacomb attack the wall to find Raya Lucaria Soldier Ashes. As you head back to the exit, attack the wall near the bottom of the stairs leading to the exit. You will find Watchdog's Staff. Climb the first set of stairs and at the base of the second set, head right and attack the second archway wall. At the end of the new area attack the wall for another hidden path. At the end of the path is the final hidden path. Contains Glintstone Sorcerer Ashes that is dropped by boss Spirit-Caller Snail.
    13. Converted Tower: Southeast from the Erdtree. To unlock its entrance stand in front of the statue and use the Erudition gesture until a ladder appears. Contains a Memory Stone.

      You cannot access the elevated area of the region's southern part now as it is unlocked later in the story.
    14. Kingsrealm Ruins: Northeast from where you found the region's map. The underground entrance is hidden by an illusory wall/floor in the northeast section. Defeat the Royal Revenant boss and get the Frozen Needle in the next room.
    15. Past the ruins talk to NPC Iji about Blaidd. This will unlock the Carian Filigreed Crest in his stock for purchase.
    16. Continue towards the manor and activate the Main Caria Manor Gate Site of Grace in the entrance. Before you go in, head to the small lake to the east to find an invisible scarab with the Ash of War: Hoarfrost Stomp.
    17. Southeast from the manor at the bottom of the cliffs you can get the Intelligence-Knot Crystal Tear on a huge plate guarded by some hand enemies. This area should be accessed by the water level starting from Testu's Rise.
    18. Caria Manor: In the courtyard go to the eastern side and find the Glintstone Craftsman's Cookbook 6 in a small room. Back to the courtyard find the Ice Crest Shield and Carian Piercer from a scarab behind the gardens. Access the higher level from the building to the southwest and touch the Manor Lower Level Site of Grace.

      From the second floor, head towards the walkways. Turn left at the first tower and after you pass the second one. look to the left in the middle of the bridge. Drop to the platform below and then drop again to another building. You will find a hole that you can descend via a ladder. In the room you will find a chest with the legendary armament Sword of Night and Flame. Before you use the lift to reach the next Site of Grace, run and jump to the ramparts on your right. Guarded by some hand enemies you can get Urumi.

      Continue to the next Site of Grace, Manor Upper Level, and get the Golden Seed by a gold tree. Fight the Troll Knight after a while to get Troll Knight's Sword. After the large set of stairs and before going in the huge open area, head through a hole to the left and keep descending the cliffs carefully until you get the Ash of War: Carian Grandeur. Return to the manor's last area to fight  Royal Knight Loretta, who drops Loretta's Greatbow and Ash of War: Loretta's Slash.
    19. Exit the manor and to the west in some ruins is a scarab with Ash of War: Chilling Mist.
    20. Renna's Rise: The tower to the north of ruins with the scarab. It is locked for the time being, so we will come here later.
    21. Take the road north of the manor leading to the east part of the region. On the way you will find a scarab with Frozen Armament. Interact with the Behind Caria Manor Site of Grace nearby.
    22. Southeast from the Site you can find an unnamed graveyard with Albinauric Ashes. If you found the Resurrection Painting from the Artist's Shack in Liurnia Lake (East) you will find a ghost that will drop a Juvenile Scholar set.
    23. Royal Grave Everagol: Northeast from the graveyard. Contains Meteorite that is dropped by boss Onyx Lord.
    24. South from Renna's Rise you will find the boss Glintstone Dragon Adula roaming the area. Once its HP reaches 50%, it will disappear, so don't worry that it bugged or that you moved too far away. You will meet the boss again much later.
    25. East from where you fought Dragon Adula, fall towards the Caria Manor and find NPC Pidia, Carian Servant after descending a ladder. Sells Glintstone Craftsman's Cookbook 7, Ash of War: Carian Retaliation, Ripple Blade, Black Leather Shield. Also sells Celestial Dew and Larval Tear. You can also get a Stonesword Key guarded by a giant crab found in the nearby ramparts. Climb the ladder again and carefully fall to the right. Move through the platforms until you reach the crab.
    26. Ranni's Rise: Past the dragon on the western part of the elevated area. At the top you will find Renna, whose real name turns out to be Ranni the Witch. Talking to her will just result in her telling you to go away. This happens because we have started Sorcerer Rogier's questline. Once she tells you to go away, return to Rogier in the Roundtable, talk to him and then go back to Ranni. You can now start her quest with no issues. Accept to become her servant in order to start her very long side-quest. After you exhaust her dialogue, head to the lower level and talk with all three NPCs there; Iji, Blaidd and Seluvis. After you exhaust their dialogue, go back to Ranni and talk to her.
    27. Return to Rogier and talk to him. Once you reload the area and return to him, you will see that he has fallen asleep.
    28. You must now go back underground to Siofra River region. Follow the cliff from where you fought the Ancestor Spirit boss and you will soon meet with Blaidd near the Spiritspring. Exhaust the dialogue with him.
    29. Seluvis' Rise: Southeast from Ranni's Rise, talk to Seluvis to continue Ranni's quest and also accept to help him for his own quest. He will give you Seluvis' Introduction, which you must take to Sorceress Sellen in Waypoint Ruins, West Limgrave and Seluvis' Potion. At the top of the tower you can find a Memory Stone and on the elevated rocks behind the tower you can find the Black Wolf Mask. After you are done, go to Sellen to talk to her and then you must head back to Blaidd in Siofra River and talk with him. This is all you can do for Ranni's quest up to this point.
    30. We can however, continue with Seluvis' quest a bit. You can choose to give his Potion to any of the following three NPCs: 1) Nepheli as Seluvis asked, 2) Sir Gideon Ofnir or 3) Dung Eater. Dung Eater is located in the room past the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable and he will appear there only after you have reached Altus Plateau in a little bit. However, you will be able to give it to him after you find a certain item for him.

      At this point you will have to make a choice which option you will follow, as giving the potion to any of the NPCs will make some items unobtainable. There is no way unfortunately to get all of them in the same run:
      1) Giving the potion to Nepheli will make her quest fail and also make Dung Eater Puppet Ashes unobtainable.
      2) Giving the potion to Dung Eater will make his quest fail and also make Nepheli Puppet Ashes unobtainable.
      3) Giving the potion to Sir Gideon will allow you to complete both Nepheli's and Dung Eater's quests, but will make both of their Ashes unobtainable.

      If you are aiming to get every item, you will have to do two playthroughs, giving the potion to Nepheli and Dung Eater over the course of two playthroughs. If you want to get the most items in one playthrough, you should give the potion to Nepheli, as you would lose only the Dung Eater Puppet Ashes. If you are focusing on completing as many quests as possible, you should give the potion to Sir Gideon Ofnir. Giving it to Gideon will also allow you to complete Seluvis' quest, despite him asking you to give the potion to Nepheli.

      The author of this walkthrough gave the potion to Gideon. Note, however, that if you give the potion to him and you want to get EVERY item in the game, then you will have to play the game two more times to get both Nepheli's and Dung Eater's Ashes.

      If you want to give it to Nepheli, read up until #33 in this section. If you want to give it to Dung Eater, you must skip reading everything from the Nokron, Eternal City section up until Leyndell, Royal Capital. You will have to give him a Seedbed Curse, talk to him in Roundtable, then read #23 and then #27 in Leyndell's section. The important thing you want to avoid is giving an item you get at the end of Nokron, Eternal City to Ranni. This is the point of no return for Seluvis' quest.
    31. Head south from the Folly On The Lake Site of Grace to the swamp area underneath the mountain. To the southeastern part of the swamp you can find a scarab with Ash of War: Vow of the Indomitable.
    32. Village of the Albinaurics: Head further west from the scarab in the swamp underneath the mountain. You will find a slope that will lead to the village. On the way make sure to talk to Nepheli and exhaust the dialogue with her. Close-by and near some enemies you will find a Larval Tear. Continue to find the Village of the Albinaurics Site of Grace. From the Site cross the bridge and at the end find the Crystal Sword. From the Site climb the next slope and past the enemy using fire powder you will find a shack with a huge pot next to it. If you attack the pot, you will reveal NPC Albus. Exhaust the dialogue with him to get the Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right). Next to him you can find an Ivory Sickle in a sarcophagus. Cross the stone bridge to find the boss Omenkiller and get the Crucible Knot Talisman. In the ledge near the boss there is also a Rune Arc.
    33. Return to Roundtable. Now that you got the right part of the secret medallion, upon entering the Hold you will be invaded by Ensha of the Royal Remains. Defeat him to get the Clinging Bone. When you are back in the normal Roundtable area, go to Gideon to find the NPC outside his room missing and in his place you can find the Royal Remains set.

      It is around this time that you will find Sorcerer Rogier dead. He will leave behind Sorcerer Rogier's Bell Bearing, the Spellblade set and Rogier's Letter. This concludes Rogier's questline.

      If you head down the stairs past the blacksmith, you can find Nepheli there. Talk with her, then go and talk to Gideon about her. You can also talk about Ensha and finally about Seluvis' potion. As mentioned, this walkthrough will follow the route to give the potion to Gideon. After giving the potion to Gideon, return to Nepheli and keep talking to her until you give her the Stormhawk King. Exhaust the dialogue with her. We will see the conclusion of Nepheli's quest near the end of the game.
    34. With the Haligtree Secret Medallion (Right) return to the Slumbering Wolf's Shack in Liurnia Lake (East). You can now talk to NPC Latenna to start her quest and you will get the Latenna the Albinauric Ashes. You can also find a Rune Arc near the Shack.
    35. After giving the potion to Gideon, you can go to Seluvis and he will believe that the potion didn't work. You will now be able to buy Sorceries from him, including Carian Phalanx, Carian Retaliation, Glintstone Icecrag and Freezing Mist. Go back to Ranni's Rise and find his secret chamber behind an illusory wall on the floor to the ruins north of the tower. Talk to Seluvis again and he will allow you to choose one of his Ashes, Finger Maiden Therolina Puppet or Jarwight Puppet. Once you buy a few Sorceries from him and also have in your possession a few Starlight Shards, he will allow you to buy the other Ash you didn't choose from him. At this point he will continue by telling you of his plot to betray Ranni and for this he requires an item that can be found in Altus Plateau.

      NOTE: If, for some reason, Seluvis does not offer you access to his puppet shop, you can easily fix this by obtaining another Starlight Shard. The closest one to get is by teleporting to the Beast Clergyman, exit his building, then ride with Torrent to the east side of the area. Continue going south while hugging the cliff until you find the Shard.
    36. Ruin-Strewn Precipice: From Testu's Rise follow the river to the northeast inside the canyon. On the way pick up the Golden Seed by the gold tree. Before using the ladder leading to the Precipice find a scarab in a small alcove protected by bats with the Ash of War: Barbaric Roar.

      Inside the Precipice when you reach an area with a singing bat protected by another bat who casts magic, you can find Lost Ashes of War in the platform behind them. Instead of using the lift, climb the ladder at the other side of the same area on to the rooftops. At the end of the area guarded by a giant octopus you can find the Serpent-God's Curved Sword. Defeat  Magma Wyrm Makar, who drops Magma Wyrm's Scalesword and a Dragon Heart, which you can use to get the final Incantation from the Church of Dragon Communion in West Limgrave. You can also summon Great Horned Tragoth in this fight and receive the Casual Greeting gesture from him. This is one of the three chances you have to get this gesture, which can be received exclusively by summoning this NPC in a boss fight. After exiting the room from the Magma Wyrm, you will find yourself in Altus Plateau.

    Altus Plateau Part 1





    This region is quite important as it is the entrance to the Lands Between's northern areas and also the continuation of the main story. However, we will divide this area to two parts. The only reason that we are getting to Altus Plateau at this moment and not a bit later is to progress Seluvis' quest and get a talisman from him. If we were to leave this area until when we will actually explore it, then we would miss on it.

    1. As soon as you exit from the lift, interact with the Site of Grace for easy access later. One of the coffins in this area has a corpse with the Ruler's set.
    2. As you move on up the slope, you will be attacked by Ancient Dragon Lansseax. You will see that the dragon is much stronger than the ones you've faced so far, but thankfully much like the dragon in West Liurnia, once you reach its HP down to 75%, it will fly away. You will meet this boss again later.
    3. Your next order of business is to get this region's map. Until you get it, ignore everything you come across with, except any Sites of Grace to help you with the exploration when we return here later. After you exit to the Plateau's main area, keep heading to the east until you find the pillar with the map in the middle of a road.
    4. To the northeast of the map you can fall down a small hole with Torrent to find the Amber Starlight, guarded by some small octupi. This is the item we need for Seluvis and the reason we came here.
    5. Return to Seluvis and hand him the starlight. You will get the Magic Scorpion Charm. You can now choose to ignore the rest of Seluvis' quest at this point. Once you reload the area, Seluvis will hand to you the Amber Draught and ask you to administer this potion to Ranni. If you do this, Ranni will be disappointed with you and her quest will fail. You CAN continue her quest by going to the Church of Vows and doing an Absolution ritual by using a Celestial Dew and she will forgive you, however since you gain absolutely nothing from Seluvis by giving her the Draught (except from sadness), the walkthrough will end his quest with receiving the Amber Draught as the final step.
    6. Return to Roundtable Hold. Now that we accessed the Altus Plateau, a few things will change here. Roderika will warn you about NPC Dung Eater that has now appeared in the Hold. If you have any Spirit Ash at +4 level, choose to Talk with Roderika and she will give you the Curtsy gesture. You can find him in the room past the Twin Maiden Husks and talk with him. If you talk to Corhyn, he will inform you that he will be leaving from the Hold soon, so use this opportunity to buy any Incantations you need from him, as when you reload the area he will be gone.

    Altus Plateau, and Caelid that we will visit next, feature a very big difficult curve for the game. The enemies are now much stronger than what you encountered in Limgrave and Liurnia, so it is highly recommended that you upgrade your equipment as much as you can. Weapons should be around +10 (for normal armaments) or +4 (for special armaments). Any Spirit Summons that you are using should be at around +4 as well. Your character should also be around Level 60 MINIMUM, with level 70 preferred. You have been warned.

    Caelid





    Caelid will be our next stop, found east of East Limgrave. As you can see by checking your map, the western part of the region is already uncovered with East Limgrave's map, which will be helpful at the beginning of our exploration. The enemies here are much stronger than the first areas so it is again advised that you are well prepared.

    1. You can access this region by heading east from the Smoldering Church in East Limgrave. You can follow the main road southwards ignoring all new enemies (and bosses) until the road turns east. A little later down the road you will find the pillar with the region's map.
    2. Minor Erdtree: It can be found in the northern end of Caelid and to access it you need to follow the cliff east from the Smouldering Church. Here you can fight a Putrid Avatar that drops the Greenburst Crystal Tear and the Flame-Shrouding Cracked Tear.
    3. Minor Erdtree Catacombs: South side of the Minor Erdtree. Contains the Imp Head (Wolf) and the Mad Pumpkin Head Ashes that is dropped by boss Duo Erdtree Burial Watchdogs.
    4. Back in the Smoldering Church, follow the road east past the Shack of the Rotting. Soon you will encounter a convoy that was ambushed by enemies. There is a scarab guarded by two T-Rex dog enemies that drops Whirl, o Flame. The chest behind the destroyed convoy has a Greatsword.
    5. Caelem Ruins: Located south from the ambushed convoy. Underground you will fight Two Mad Pumpkin Head bosses. The room after them contains Visage Shield.
    6. Forsaken Ruins: West from Caelem Ruins, contains the Sword of St. Trina underground, which can be accessed by using a Stonesword Key.
    7. Gael Tunnel: In the western part of the region and south of the Forsaken Ruins. Contains Cross-Naginata and Moonveil & Dragon Heart, both of which are dropped by Magma Wyrm boss. Usually you can also find Alexander here, but since you have started Ranni's quest, he will automatically move to Redmane Castle.
    8. South from the tunnel you will find the Fort Gael North Site Site of Grace. To the east you will see the footprints of an invisible scarab. Hit it and it will drop Ash of War: Flame of the Redmanes. Be careful as you will aggro three T-Rex dogs while you hunt it down.
    9. Fort Gael: Further south from the tunnel. Contains Ash of War: Lion's Claw that is dropped by the Lion Guardian, Katar, Starscourge Heirloom in a chest and the Flame: Grant Me Strength that is guarded outside the fort by two fire-spitting tanks.
    10. Gaol Cave: East of the fort in the poisonous river. Can be accessed by using two Stonesword Keys. Contains Stonesword Key, Pillory Shield and Wakizashi in the cells. It also has the Putrid Corpse Ashes that is dropped by boss Frenzied Duelist. After the boss room you can find the Regalia of Eochaid.
    11. Caelid Waypoint Ruins: South of the cave. Contains Meteoric Ore Blade in a chest underground.
    12. Northeast of the ruins you can find the Astray from Caelid Highway North Site of Grace. Near it you can find a scarab with Ash of War: Lifesteal Fist. You can also find a merchant nearby to the east, although he doesn't sell anything of importance.
    13. Street of Sages Ruins: Northeast from the Site of Grace. Explore this area and the swamp on Torrent, otherwise you will accumulate Rot. Contains a Traveller set, Meteorite Staff and Rock Sling, which you can find underground.
    14. Head east into the swamp and you will be invaded by Millicent, who doesn't drop anything of importance.
    15. Continue east to find the Inner Aeonia Site of Grace and then go south to fight Commander O'Neil who drops Commander's Standard and Unalloyed Gold Needle.
    16. East from the boss you will find a scarab with Ash of War: Sacred Ring of Light.
    17. Head to the southwest end of the swamp to find a scarab on an island with Ash of War: Poisonous Mist.
    18. Head to the northeast end of the swamp to find an invisible scarab with the Poison Armament.
    19. Back to the Astray from Caelid Highway North Site of Grace head south until you find the Caelid Highway South Site. A little to the east you will fight the dragon boss Decaying Ekzykes. Drops Dragon Heart.
    20. Cathedral of Dragon Communion: Southeast from the dragon. You will be able to buy new Incantations here with the Dragon Hearts you've gathered so far. You will also see that any Incantations you didn't buy from the Church of Dragon Communion are also available for purchase here. Some of the new Incantations require more than one Dragon Heart to be purchased and new ones will be added here as you slay more dragons. The new Incantations up to this point are Dragonice, Rotten Breath, Glintstone Breath, Ekzykes's Decay, Agheel's Flame and Smarag's Glintstone Breath. Also has the Ancient Dragon Apostle's Cookbook 3.
    21. Caelid Catacombs: East from the cathedral after you descend down the cliff. Contains the Miranda Sprout Ashes, hidden behind an illusory wall right of the staircase in the swamp with the huge poisonous flower, and the Kindred of Rot Ashes that is dropped by boss Cemetary Shade. The lever to open the boss door is hidden behind an illusory wall directly opposite from the boss door.
    22. Head to the pillar where you found this region's map. East from this location is a Nomadic Merchant, who sells Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 15, a Champion set and a Greathelm. North of the merchant during nights you can fight a Night's Cavalry, who drops Ash of War: Poison Moth Flight.
    23. South of the merchant on the road you can find a Golden Seed by a gold tree.
    24. Northeast of the seed, in a pit and protected by two huge bird enemies you can find the Windy Crystal Tear.
    25. Further northeast of the tear at nights you can fight a Death Rite Bird, which drops the Death's Poker.
    26. Head to the south end of the region near the bridge leading to Redmane Castle. Climb the tower near the beginning of the bridge to find Arrow's Sting Talisman. West from the tower there is a scarab with Ash of War: Cragblade. We will head to the castle a bit later at the end of our exploration in Caelid.
    27. North on the road from where you fought the Death Rite Bird, you will come across the Sellia Gateway.
    28. Gowry's Shack: Past the gate and a little to the east. Make sure to kill the T-Rex Dog outside the shack or at least do not let it spot you, as there may be a slight chance that it will follow you inside the shack and accidentally kill the NPC there. Talk with Gowry here and exhaust his dialogue to start his quest about helping a girl named Millicent, a name that may be familiar to you since she invaded us earlier. He will ask for the Unalloyed Gold Needle, which you already have by now. Give it to him, reload the area and talk to him again. He will ask you to find Millicent in the church past Sellia, where we will head next.
    29. Sellia, Town of Sorcery: North from Gowry's Shack. In order to progress from this area to the north, you will need to solve the town's puzzle, which includes lighting three flames at the top of the town's towers. In the streets find the Redmane Painting. If you descend some stairs to the west part of the town, you will find a Site of Grace. Past it in a ruined building you can find the Rotten Stray Ashes. Back in the main street, at the end of the town you can see that there are two huge sealed gates, one to the left and one to the right up some stairs. Climb the stairs to find a Golden Seed by a gold tree. You can use Torrent to climb the nearby root and access the town's rooftops where you can climb the 3 towers to light the flames. On the rooftops you can find a Stonesword Key, a scarab with Ash of War: Double Slash, the Cerulean Tear Scarab and the Staff of Loss on a balcony. After you light the 3 flames, go back to the ground level and find three chests that were blocked before by magic seals. These chests have the Night Comet, the final Imbued Sword Key and the Spelldrake Talisman +1. At the left gate at the end of the area fight the duo Nox Swordstress & Nox Priest to get the Nox Flowing Sword. In the chest in the back of the boss room is a chest with Lusat's Glintstone Staff.
    30. Since we got the last Imbued Sword Key return to the Four Belfries in West Liurnia to use it at the last waygate. This one takes you to Farum Azula, a very dangerous area we will visit at the end of the game. For now you can jump down the platforms and find the Pearldrake Talisman at the last one. Be very careful as there are two Beastmen in the second last platform, which are extremely strong at this point of the game. You can run, grab the talisman then immediately leave the area or you can try knocking them off the platform.
    31. Church of the Plague: Back in Sellia head through the right gate at the end of the area and follow the path until you reach the church. Inside you can find a Sacred Tear and NPC Millicent. Talk with her and then give her the Needle you got from Gowry. Rest at the Site of Grace, then exhaust her dialogue to get the Prosthesis-Wearer Heirloom. Rest at the Site again and she will disappear. Return to Gowry's Shack to find Millicent in Gowry's place. Exhaust the dialogue with her and then reload the area. Gowry will be back at his place and Millicent will be gone. You can now buy Sorceries from Gowry including Glintstone Stars, Night Shard and Night Maiden's Mist. As for Millicent we will meet her again later.
    32. Swamp Lookout Tower: Follow the road south from the church and cross the road through Sellia Gateway. You can find the Eternal Darkness in a cell guarded by a Bloodhound Knight enemy.
    33. Redmane Castle: On the southeastern part of Caelid. Since you started and progressed through Ranni's quest, the castle will be almost empty of enemies starting from the bridge leading to the castle. To enter the castle you must go from the cliffs to the side of the closed gate. You will reach a graveyard with some bats and a scarab that has the Ash of War: Flaming Strike. Enter the castle and you can find Armorer's Cookbook 5 when you open the shortcut door to the graveyard. Almost every door cannot be opened at this point because of the festival going on, so rest at the Site of Grace near the end of the castle and then go forward.

      You will find a lot of NPCs including Blaidd and Alexander. Make sure to talk with them. There is also a Finger Maiden that once you talk with, will give you the Polite Bow gesture. Everyone is here to prepare for a war festival in order to fight Radahn. When you are ready for the fight, go talk with the festival announcer, who is NPC Witch-Hunter Jerren and you will get the Heartening Cry gesture. You can then take the elevator and use the waygate to teleport to the vast field to the north and fight  Starscourge Radahn, who will drop the Remembrance of the Starscourge and Radahn's Great Rune. During the fight make sure to also summon Great Horned Tragoth in order to get the Casual Greeting gesture, if you didn't summon him during the Magma Wyrm Makar boss fight in Liurnia West.

      After you defeat Radahn, make sure to talk to Blaidd and Alexander near the Site of Grace to progress their quests.
    34. Return to Roundtable Hold and talk with Finger Reader Enia. Use the Remembrance you got to choose between Starscourge Greatsword OR Lion Greatbow. You can also buy from her Radahn's set. If you haven't used one of the two bell-bearing Mausoleums we found so far (1 in Weeping Peninsula and 1 in Liurnia Lake) and you want the other weapon as well, feel free to use them to regain a charge for Radahn's Remembrance.
      Another thing you must do is talk to Fia. Let her embrace you and keep talking with her until she gives you the Weathered Dagger. Give the dagger to D in Roundtable and then reload the area. You will find them in the newly unlocked room past the blacksmith. Talk to Fia and she will disappear. Pick up the Twinned Set and D's Bell Bearing.
    35. With Radahn dead, return to Redmane Castle and talk with Jerren again. Once you exhaust his dialogue, reload the area to return the castle to its normal status, now filled with enemies and all doors opened. From the Chamber Outside the Plaza Site of Grace head towards the courtyard with the two lion guardians and enter the building near the waygate, then climb the ladder. Guarded by a Mad Pumpkin Head you can find a Flamberge on a corpse. Enter the building in the courtyard with the two Lion Guardians to find the Armorer's Cookbook 4. In the building past the Iron Maiden you can find the Red-Hot Whetblade. At the end of the castle where you met the NPCs earlier, you will find the duo boss Misbegotten Warrior & Crucible Knight, who will drop the legendary armament Ruins Greatsword.
    36. War-Dead Catacombs: Go back to the field where you fought Radahn and go to the very north part of the area to find this dungeon hidden by a door in the cliffs. Contains the Collapsing Stars in a chest on the lowest level of the dungeon and Radahn Soldier Ashes in a smaller swamp guarded by a Cleanrot Knight ghost. Also contains a Golden Seed & the legendary Ashen Remains Redmane Knight Ogha Ashes, both of which are dropped by the Putrid Tree Spirit boss.
      NOTE: This dungeon can be quite hard, so if you are having difficulties, you can leave it for later until you are better prepared.
    37. Before we proceed to the next region we can continue with a few side-quests. First for Alexander, head to Liurnia Lake (East) a little south from the Carian Study Hall where you fought a Tibia Mariner boss. You will find Alexander stuck in the ground once again and you must throw at him an Oil pot or a Roped Oil Pot to let him out, both of which can be crafted with the Crafting Kit. If you need Cracked Pots, Merchant Kale sells some in the Church of Elleh in West Limgrave. Alexander will be back in another region.
    38. We will also continue Blaidd's quest. Go and talk with Iji about Blaidd and then head to Forlorn Hound Evergaol in West Limgrave to find Blaidd and release him. Then return to Iji and talk with him again. His quest will be finished a little later.

    Dragonbarrow





    Dragonbarrow is the region north of Caelid and the last one in the south Lands Between. The enemies to the very north of this area are even stronger than the ones in Caelid, so caution is highly advised. If you are having problems completing this region, you can leave this for a little later, but it is definitely possible to do it now.

    1. First you need to find the region's map. From the Caelem Ruins in Caelid, head east and use Torrent to jump across the canyon. Head east and you will find the Dragonbarrow West Site of Grace. A little to the east on the road you will find the pillar with the map.
    2. Sellia Hideaway: Return to the Church of Plague where we found Millicent. Take the road to the northeast towards the higher level and then head straight northeast. You will soon find a graveyard and a battlemage wielding a Staff and a Club. Behind him if you attack the rocks you can reveal this hidden dungeon. You can ignore the next part and continue straight to the next location (#3), since we will return to this dungeon after we obtain a specific item to properly clear it or feel free to continue if you want to get everything you can up to this point.

      Inside the dungeon attack the two illusory walls to continue. If you drop into the first pit, you will find Lost Ashes of War guarded by an enemy. Moving on past the Stake of Marika, if you follow the crystal path to the top, you can fall in a hole and find the Crystalian Ashes in a chest guarded by 3 sorcerers. Back in the crystal room, on a ledge with a corpse if you attack the wall behind it, you will find the Crystal Spear in a chest. Also contains Crystal Torrent that is dropped by the Trio Putrid Crystallians boss. In this cave you may also come across a magic seal guarded by a sorcerer at the bottom of the second pit. Take note of it, as we will have to return here later before we can go through.
    3. Return to the Site of Grace you touched upon exiting Deep Siofra Well. Moving through this canyon you can find the Spiked Palisade Shield under a Giant. If you move on to the end of the path, you can find the NPC Great-Jar. Talk to him and then in front of him you can fight 3 invader enemies, the Knights of the Great Jar. If you defeat all three of them in succession, you will be awarded with Great-Jar's Arsenal.
    4. Sellia Crystal Tunnel: Return to Sellia Town and go to the Sellia Under-Stair Site of Grace. Follow the path to the north near the cliffs and you will soon find the entrance. This is also the place where you might have been teleported from the trap chest in Dragon-Burnt Ruins in West Limgrave. Contains the Faithful's Canvas Talisman, Rock Blaster and the Somberstone Miner's Bell Bearing that is dropped by boss Fallingstar Beast. This is another important bell as it will allow you to buy levels 1 & 2 of Somber Smithing Stones, materials required for upgrading your special armaments, when given to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable.
    5. Abandoned Cave: Found in the southwestern part of the region, this dungeon can be accessed only by crossing the canyon through a tree log from Caelid to the west. Head east from the Smoldering Wall Site of Grace in Caelid and use a tree log over the canyon to enter the cave. Inside you can find the Venomous Fang, Serpent Bow and Golden Scarab that is dropped by duo boss Cleanrot Knights. This is a great talisman when you want to grind for levels, as it will give you 20% more Runes upon killing enemies (bosses included).
    6. Isolated Merchant's Shack: Northwest from where you got the map, in an elevated area. Northwest from the shack you can find a scarab with Ash of War: Sky Shot. The merchant here sells Spiked Caestus, Beast-Repellent Torch and a Samurai set. At nights you can fight another Bell-Bearing Hunter, who will drop the Gravity Stone Peddler's Bell Bearing. This is the strongest version of the bell-bearing hunters so it is advised to cheese him by using ranged attacks via bows, spells or throwable items (like Kukri) and using Torrent to maintain distance from him.
    7. Sellia Evergaol: South from where you found the map, on the cliff above the Abandoned Cave. Contains Battlemage Hugues Ashes that is dropped by boss Battlemage Hugues.
    8. Divine Tower of Caelid: North from the map's location. You can access this only with careful platforming at the sides of the tower. As you climb the platforms to enter the tower, you can find a Stonesword Key and a Numen's Rune, which is used to obtain a lot of Runes. When inside the tower, if you head to the very bottom after careful platforming, fight the boss Godskin Apostle, who drops the Godskin Apostle set and the next room has Godslayer's Greatsword. If you go to the top of the tower, you can activate Radahn's Great Rune.
    9. Fort Faroth: Follow the road east of the map's location and then take the right road at the intersection. The fort is found at the end of the road. Contains the Dectus Medallion (Right) in a chest on the roof and if you fall through a hole in the roof and fight some rats, you can reach a corpse with the legendary talisman Radagon's Soreseal. Now that you have both halves for Dectus Medallion, you can use the Grand Lift of Dectus to access Altus Plateau, but we will go there later.
    10. West from the fort you can find a huge dragon surrounded by smaller ones. The huge dragon is Elder Dragon Greyoll and while she doesn't have a boss bar, her health bar is enormous. You can either kill the smaller dragons around her to slowly deplete her HP or use a weapon with bleeding and attack the dragon between her right wing and leg to slowly bring her down. Upon her death, the dragon will give 5 Dragon Hearts, which can be used in the Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid to gain more Incantations. Killing this dragon also unlocks in the cathedral for purchase the legendary Incantation Elder Dragon Greyoll's Roar.
    11. Southwest from the dragon you can find on the cliffs (on top of the giant stone head) a Stonesword Key, on a corpse sitting on a chair.
    12. Minor Erdtree: Follow the road east of the map's location and then take the left road at the intersection. Here fight another Putrid Avatar boss that drops the Stonebarb Cracked Tear and the Opaline Hardtear for your Flask of Wondrous Physik. South of the Erdtree use the roots to carefully descend the cliffs. At the edge of the small plateau and just before the giant golem you can find a ghost that will drop the Ash of War: Rain of Arrows, only if you have picked up the Redmane Painting in Sellia Town.
    13. Dragonbarrow Cave: Found north from the Erdtree if you follow the cliff road. Contains the Bull-Goat's Talisman and Flamedrake Talisman +2 that is dropped by duo boss Beastmen of Farum Azula.
    14. Head northeast from the Erdtree to find a scarab with Bestial Constitution. The scarab is on a slope to the left side of the big bridge.
    15. Lenne's Rise: East from the Erdtree. You have to use a Spiritspring to enter the tower via a balcony. Contains a Memory Stone at the top.
    16. North from Lenne's Rise at nights you can fight another Night's Cavalry who drops Ash of War: Bloodhound's Step.
    17. On the bridge leading to the region's northern area you can fight the boss Flying Dragon Greyll. Drops a Dragon Heart that you can use for another Incantation in the Cathedral of Dragon Communion.

    1. The first thing to do is get the region's map, especially if you came here from Altus Plateau Part 2 section to have a better map visual for some of its areas. From the Writheblood Ruins in Altus Plateau cross the bridge to the west to find the Bridge of Iniquity Site of Grace. Follow the road to the north until you reach a camp where some soldiers are fighting some Iron Maidens. At this point stick to the cliffs on the left until you find a ladder that you can climb. Follow the path to the west ignoring everything. Occasionally you will see a manor to your south. This is the Volcano Manor where you may have been teleported earlier with Rya. Northwest from the manor and on a lower level you can find the region's map on a pillar.
    2. We will first explore the lower levels of the region. Head to the Wyndham Ruins in Altus Plateau. Behind the ruins you will find the Seethewater River Site of Grace. Follow the river to the north to find a scarab with Ash of War: Barrage.
    3. Further to the north you will find a Golden Seed by a gold tree.
    4. Seethewater Cave: North of the Golden Seed. Requires 2 Stonesword Keys to access. Contains a Mushroom set in an area with a huge poisonous flower and the Kindred of Rot's Exultation that is dropped by duo boss Kindreds of Rot.
    5. Fort Laiedd: Found at the western part of the region, west of the Seethewater Terminus Site of Grace. Contains Fire Scorpion Charm, Armorer's Cookbook 7 and Prelate's Inferno Crozier that is dropped by the Prelate enemy inside.
    6. Northeast from the fort near the cliffs surrounded by Jellyfish spirits you can find a Stonesword Key.
    7. South from the fort in the lava you can fight a Magma Wyrm that will drop a Dragon Heart that you can use for another Incantation in the Cathedral of Dragon Communion. Defeating this boss will also unlock the Magma Breath for purchase in the Cathedral.
    8. South from the boss you will find Alexander once again. Talking to him will give you the Jar. There is a safe spot to talk to him just near the cliffs where the magma won't hurt you.
    9. Hermit's Shack: Follow the road south of fort Laiedd. Contains Roiling Magma.
    10. Craftsman's Shack: East from the previous shack. Contains Pulley Crossbow.
    11. Hermit Village: Northeast from the previous shack. Contains Errant Sorcerer set in a shack. The set's helm Hierodas Glintstone Crown can be found to the south of the village. You can also find Prattling Pate (You're Beautiful) near the crown. At the end fight the Demi-Human Queen Maggie who will drop a Memory Stone. Behind the enemy in a corner by the Site of Grace you can find NPC Primeval Sorcerer Azur. Interact with him to get the legendary sorcery Comet Azur.
    12. Now that you have this legendary spell, it is time to do Sellen's quest. Return to Sellen in West Limgrave and show her your new sorcery. She will hand you the Sellian Sealbreaker. Head to Sellia Hideaway in Dragonbarrow. Remember the magic seal that we found there? With the new item Sellen gave us, we can break the seal and head inside the small alcove. Here we will find NPC Master Lusat. Interacting with him will give us another legendary sorcery, Stars of Ruin.

      Return to Sellen once again and this time she will tell you to meet her in the Witchbane Ruins in the Weeping Peninsula. You may recall that we saw Sellen in the underground room there during our first visit. She will give you Sellen's Primal Glintstone. In the Ruins you will also find Witch-Hunter Jerren after reloading the area, the announcer from Radahn's war festival. Exhaust the dialogue with him.

      Your next stop is Seluvis' secret laboratory north of Ranni's Rise in West Liurnia. Next to Seluvis' message you can find Sellen behind an illusory wall, to whom you should give the Glintstone she gave us. She will now be selling your Sorceries from this location.

      The final place for her quest is Raya Lucaria Grand Library, the place where Rennala is. Exit the building and you will see two summon signs. One for invading Sellen and assist Jerren and one for summoning Sellen to invade Jerren. You will have to choose which character you want to assist in defeating the other. Undoubtedly the best option here is to assist Sellen. The only thing you'll miss for not siding with Jerren is an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone, the material required to max level a normal armament, but you can find more of this material elsewhere during your run, while if you don't side with Sellen you will miss a lot of unique stuff.

      Summon Sellen and defeat Witch-Hunter Jerren to receive the Eccentric set. Exhaust the dialogue with Sellen to receive the Glintstone Kris and unlock Shard Spiral for purchase from her.
    13. Before you continue, make sure to re-visit Master Lusat in Sellia Hideaway to get Lusat set.
    14. Back in Mt. Gelmir and Hermit Village, re-visit Azur to get Azur set. You must now return to Raya Lucaria Grand Library and to the side of the room near Rennala you will find Sellen in her new form. Get the Witch's Glintstone Crown near her to complete her quest.
    15. Gelmir Hero's Grave: Northeast from Hermit Village across the canyon. This is another dungeon containing chariots but this time they cannot be destroyed. As you go down the slope with the first chariot, if you go to the very end past the magma instead of progressing via the alcove to the left, you can find a chest with the Ringed Finger. When you reach the second chariot, the top of the path has a Stonesword Key, however it is very difficult to get it and return to the alcove without dying. Once you reach the bottom of the path past a bridge the alcove to the right has a Cemetery Shade enemy that will drop the Mantis Blade. To progress you need to carefully fall down the side of the bridge you just passed. In the new room fight the Bloodhound Knight enemy to get the Bloodhound Knight set and the Gelmir Knight set it guarded. Climb the ladder and then jump through the window to go onto the beams. You need to carefully jump on the chariot and ride it to the bottom of the lava path. Defeat the boss Red Wolf of the Champion to get the Bloodhound Knight Floh Ashes. The chest in the boss room has a Deathroot, which you should take to the Beast Clergyman to get Stone of Gurranq.
    16. Corpse-Stench Shack: Return to the Bridge of Iniquity Site of Grace. Follow the road heading north to the very end. Outside you will be invaded once again by Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater, who drops the Sacred Butchering Knife. Inside find the Golden Vow. The broken bridge past the shack has a Stonesword Key.
    17. Back at the higher level of the mountain, after the ladder you climbed to get the region's map, you will find the First Mt. Gelmir Campsite Site of Grace. Follow the road to the northwest and climb the top of the siege tower to find the Pulley Bow.
    18. Move to the northwest on the road and to the left before crossing the bridge you will see a message by Patches. Follow the stones he threw to view a cutscene with Patches to continue his quest. You will then be near the Seethewater Cave with the frogs that cause Death.
    19. Return back up and continue through the bridge. Past the bridge and a Grafted Scion enemy you can find the Scavenger's Curved Sword. Climbing the mountain you will find a merchant who sells Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 20, Guilty Hood and a Confessor set.
    20. Volcano Cave: North from the merchant at the end of the path on top of the mountain. Contains Coil Shield and Jar Cannon that is dropped by boss Demi-Human Queen Margot.
    21. Moving to the next path to the west, past the Ninth Mt. Gelmir Campsite Site of Grace, at the top of the mountain you will fight the Full-Grown Fallingstar Beast boss that will drop the Fallingstar Beast Jaw.
    22. Head further west to reach the plateau north of the volcano manor. To the north part of this area you can find a Golden Seed by a gold tree and a little to the south an invisible scarab with the Ash of War: Through and Through.
    23. Minor Erdtree: Follow the path past the pillar with the region's map. Here you will fight an Ulcerated Tree Spirit that will drop the Leaden Hardtear and the Cerulean Hidden Tear.
    24. Volcano Manor: The central and main area of the region, there's a lot of things to do and stuff to pick here. For starters as soon as you enter the manor, head to the second floor to be invaded by Inquisitor Ghiza who will drop Ghiza's Wheel.

      Head to the first floor and talk with NPC Tanith. Accept to become part of the manor to receive the Drawing-Room Key. Explore the hallway and the rooms of the manor to find NPCs Patches, Bernahl, Diallos and Rya. Make sure to talk to them. The big room also has the Recusant Finger.

      1st Assassination Quest: In the same room with the NPCs you can find the Letter from Volcano Manor. Reading this will reveal a mark on your map, where you have to go and do an assassination quest. Talk to Tanith then head to the mark in West Limgrave and defeat Old Knight Istvan, who will drop a Scaled set. Return to Tanith and she will give you Magma Shot.

      After the first quest is done, talk to Rya. Bernahl will now also sell Ash of War: Eruption and Ash of War: Assassin's Gambit. You will see that Diallos is missing, so reload the area to find him again and talk to him. You will also find a ghost you should talk to at the end of the hallway. Rya has now moved to another room, so find her in her new form and talk to her. Then talk to Patches, who will give you the Letter to Patches. Read it to reveal a mark on your map and then do his assassination quest in Ruin-Strewn Precipice. The invasion sign is found in the boss' arena. Defeat Great Horned Tragoth who will drop a Bull-Goat set. Return to Patches and talk to him, then reload the area and talk to him again to get the Magma Whip Candlestick.

      *NOTE* By assassinating Great Horned Tragoth you will not be able to summon him as an NPC for a future boss fight. This is not important as long as you summoned him either for the Magma Wyrm Makar or Radahn boss fights in order to receive the Casual Greeting gesture from him. If you somehow did not summon him to help you against these two bosses and missed on the gesture, you have one more chance to summon him. Please check section #21 in the next section Leyndell, Royal Capital for the location of the boss that you can summon Tragoth. If you want the gesture, hold off on completing this assassination until you summon him for the listed boss fight.

      2nd Assassination Quest: Return to the dining room and find another Letter from Volcano Manor. Reading this will reveal another mark on your map, where you have to go and do the assassination quest. Head to the mark in Altus Plateau and defeat Rileigh the Idle, who will drop Crepus' Vial. Return to Tanith to be rewarded with the Serpentbone Blade. Also talk to her about Rya.

      Talk with Bernahl who will give you Letter to Bernahl and ask you to help him assassinate his targets. This target will be found in Leyndell, Royal Capital, so we will have to do this later. For now accepting this quest is enough.

      Talk to Rya and Diallos, then reload the area. You can talk to Tannith once more about Rya's troubles. At this point Diallos will leave the manor. Return to Jarburg in Liurnia Lake to find him. If he's not there, reload the area and keep talking to Jar-Bairn until you exhaust his dialogue. Keep reloading the area and exhausting both of their dialogues, until they repeat the same words.

      3rd Assassination Quest (Part 1): Return to the dining room and pick the Red Letter. The third and final target is located in the Mountaintops of Giants region, which will be accessed later in the game. For now accept this quest and we will do it later.

      To continue, go to the room next to Rya's room and roll at the wall behind the corpse to reveal a hidden wall. This will allow us to access the rest of the region and explore it.
    25. In the manor's dungeon area find the Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 21 in the path with the snails falling from the ceiling. At the end of this path attack the wall to get in a room with the Depraved Perfumer Carmaan Ashes. Continue to the next room at a lower level and defeat the Bloodhound Knight enemy to get the Bloodhound Claws. Continue until you reach the Prison Town Church Site of Grace. Before you head to the next area, make sure to open the church doors then return to Rya and talk to her about your findings.

      In the next area head through the streets until you reach a mist gate on a building to the right. Use a Stonesword key to get in a room with the Crimson Amber Medallion +1. Go to the lower level past the Omenkiller enemy to get in a cell with the Erdtree Seal. In the same level in a small alley with some dogs carefully go to the side of the building and past some stairs to find a Stonesword Key. Continue to the lower level near the magma to find a graveyard that has the Smoldering Shield, guarded by two pots. Return to the imp statue and progress to the next building. Before you go to the left side to get into the building, climb the ladder to the right side and go to the roof. Once at the end, fall carefully to the right side to enter a room with the Albinauric Mask and the Albinauric Staff.

      The next Site of Grace you will find is the Guest Hall. As you move through the area you will soon reach two elevators. One will take you to a lower level where you can fight a Magma Wyrm boss for a Dragon Heart. Once you go to the higher level with the other elevator, make sure to push the lever at the bridge to create a shortcut to the Prison Town Church Site of Grace before you head inside the church. When ready, go inside to fight the  Godskin Noble, who drops Noble Presence and Godskin Stitcher. In the altar past the boss get the Serpent's Amnion. Give this to Rya, then reload the area and she will disappear. Talk to Tanith and she will give you the Tonic of Forgetfulness.

      From the boss' room head back outside past the enemy at the stairs. Head to the left side of the area where a corpse is hanging by the edge. Carefully drop to the ledge below and follow the path to enter the area with magma and bats. If you follow the path to the end, you will reach the Subterranean Inquisition Chamber Site of Grace. Past this area you will fight the duo boss Abductor Virgins, who will drop the Inquisitor's Girandole. This is also the area where you are transported, if you are abducted by the Iron Maiden at the bottom of Raya Lucaria Academy.

      Back in the Godskin Noble boss' room, go to the upper floor and off the balcony to continue. When you enter the next building, climb the ladder to the bottom and in a cell find the Missionary's Cookbook 6. As you exit the building, look over the ledge to your left to find a corpse with the Crimson Tear Scarab. In the next building stand on the elevator to create a shortcut to the Site of Grace. In the next balcony one of the snake statues overlooking the magma has the Commoner's Headband. Opposite from this statue there is a ladder you can use by a window to go to a lower level. Here you will find Rya. Talk to her but do NOT give her the Tonic that Tanith gave you. Instead, we will leave her alone and return to her later. Exit the window past Rya and jump in the building through another window to find a Stonesword Key. Back in the balcony and into the next building after you open a door, you will find the Man-Serpent Ashes in an altar in front of some praying enemies. On the top floor use 2 Stonesword Keys to unlock a mist gate. Before you jump to the cages to make your way down, carefully jump to a ledge right below the entrance of the dark room. Past the new room, go up some stairs and you will end up in another ledge in the dark room that has the Dagger Talisman. Carefully fall to the bottom to find a Seedbed Curse and Ash of War: Royal Knight's Resolve. The path past the Ash of War opens a shortcut to the entrance of the Volcano Manor. Back in the room with the Ashes if you go to the balcony and use the waygate, you'll be taken to the next Site of Grace.
    26. When you reach the Audience Pathway Site of Grace, you will be right before this region's main boss Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy. Defeating this boss, however, will make Tanith's and Bernahl's missions fail and we still have to complete their assassination quests and get their rewards. For this reason, it is advised to NOT fight Rykard until after you have accessed Mountaintops of Giants and done the last assassination quest for Tanith. We will return to fight this Shardbearer boss later.
    27. Return to Roundtable Hold and talk with Dung Eater now that you have the Seedbed Curse. You will get the Sewer-Gaol Key.

    Leyndell, Royal Capital





    Leyndell is the center of the world and the largest single area of the Lands Between. It is also where you have to go to progress the main story. You need a total of 2 Great Runes to continue the main story, but if you've been following the walkthrough you will have 3 by now.

    **NOTE**: EVERYTHING inside the city of Leyndell should be considered missable, since after you complete the Crumbling Farum Azula section, this region will change dramatically. Make sure to pick up and do everything before you do that section. The Subterranean Shunning-Grounds and Leyndell Catacombs are an exception and they can still be accessed at the end of the game.

    1. To get this region's map, start from the Altus Highway Junction Site of Grace and then head east up the stairs leading to the capital. Keep following this path past the Tree Sentinels and through the door. At the next Site of Grace, Outer Wall Phantom Tree, you will find a pillar with the region's map.
    2. Right next to the map are 2 Golden Seeds by a gold tree.
    3. Perfumer's Grotto: Return to the Altus Highway Junction Site and instead of heading up the stairs follow the path north of them but still heading east. Keep following it until you find the cave. Contains Great Omenkiller Cleaver that is dropped by duo boss Omenkiller & Miranda the Blighted Bloom.
    4. Back in the Altus Highway Junction Site if you follow the path heading east but south of the stairs you can fight a Fallingstar Beast that doesn't drop anything unique.
    5. Now head back up the stairs following the path you took to get the map to fight the duo boss Tree Sentinels. Drops Erdtree Greatshield.
    6. South from the region's map you can find an Ulcerated Tree Spirit that will drop a Golden Seed. In a chest behind it you can find the Giant-Crusher.
    7. East of the map's location in the lake on the lower level, find an invisible scarab that drops Ash of War: Prayerful Strike.
    8. Sealed Tunnel: At the low level in the lake, a little south from the scarab. Contains the Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing 2 in a chest, which will allow you to buy levels 3 & 4 of upgrade materials for normal armaments from the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable. Behind the chest is an illusory wall that allows you to progress through the tunnel. There is also a Stonesword Key guarded by a sole enemy in the area with the Iron Maiden. Also contains Onyx Lord's Greatsword that is dropped by boss Onyx Lord.
    9. Divine Tower of West Altus: At the end of the Sealed Tunnel. Cannot be unlocked yet, as you need to defeat Rykard in Mt. Gelmir first. We will return here later.
    10. Minor Erdtree Church: Southwest from the tunnel. Contains Missionary's Cookbook 4 and Golden Order Seal. Rest at the Site of Grace here and talk to Melina to get the Outer Order gesture.
    11. Follow the path northeast from the map and soon a Valiant Gargoyle will appear that will drop Gargoyle's Great Axe.
    12. Further northeast from the Gargoyle you can find 2 more Golden Seeds. On the stairs heading east you can find a scarab with Ash of War: Golden Parry. At the end of this path by the sealed gate you can find Lost Ashes of War.
    13. North from the Golden Seeds you will hear someone saying "I see you, little Tarnished..". This is Margit, the first main boss we fought and now appears as a normal enemy. Defeat him to get the Viridian Amber Medallion +1.
    14. Hermit's Shack: Northeast from Margit. Here you can find a merchant that sells Sentry's Torch, Distinguished Greatshield, a Prophet set, Upper-Class Robe and Consort's Trousers.

      At nights you can fight another Bell-Bearing Hunter, who will drop the Medicine Peddler's Bell Bearing.
    15. East from the Shack at nights you can fight another Deathbird boss that drops the Twinbird Kite Shield.
    16. A little northeast from the Deathbird, surrounded by some skull enemies you can find the Winged Crystal Tear.
    17. Minor Erdtree: East from the Deathbird. On a huge plate find the Twiggy Cracked Tear and Crimson Crystal Tear.
    18. South from the Erdtree by the lake you can find Blackguard Big Boggart again. Exhaust the dialogue with him. You can now buy Boiled Crabs from him, the only way to get this item.
    19. Auriza Side Tomb: At the northeast end of the path east from Boggart below Leyndell's east gate. The trick to navigate through this dungeon is via the treasure chests, which teleport you to different areas in the dungeon. You need to use the chests to reach the lever that opens the boss' arena. Contains Perfumer's Cookbook 3 and Soldjars of Fortune Ashes that is dropped by Grave Warden Duelist boss.
    20. Auriza Hero's Grave: South of the side tomb. As with the other Hero's Grave dungeons this one also has Chariots. Contains the Golden Epitaph (requires a Stonesword Key to access). As you head down the path with the chariots, you will reach a path that has two different lanes, each of which has a chariot. In one of the small alcoves on this path is a Stonesword Key. At the bottom of this area where you have to stop between some pillars, look at a corpse hanging on a ledge. You need to carefully drop on the wooden beams beneath it to proceed. At the end of the wooden beams you can reach a room filled with Death Toads that has the Vulgar Militia Ashes. Return to the wooden beams and fall to the cement ones below. At the end of this path you will reach another hallway with a chariot. You need to climb to the top of this path and hit the fire-breathing pillar, but first head further below to find the Crucible Feather Talisman. Then climb the ladders to reach the previous ramp and a third chariot will soon appear. This will make the chariots crash and destroy them for good. You will also get Ash of War: Holy Ground and Tree Sentinel set. The end of the dungeon contains Ordovis' Greatsword and the Crucible Axe set, both dropped by defeating the duo boss Crucible Knight & Crucible Knight Ordovis.
    21. Head to Leyndell's east gate northeast from Boggart. Once there, fight the Draconic Tree Sentinel, who will drop the Dragon Greatclaw and Dragonclaw Shield. This is the last boss fight, where you can summon Great Horned Tragoth to receive the Casual Greeting gesture from him, if you didn't summon him during Magma Wyrm Makar and/or Radahn. His summon sign will not be available, however, if you have completed his assassination during the previous section in Mt. Gelmir. You can now enter the capital.
    22. Leyndell, Royal Capital: As mentioned above, this place is immense and it is extremely easy to get lost. For this reason, this section will be a lot more detailed than usual to help with navigation. Head in the city past the first Site of Grace Capital Rampart and then to the next one East Capital Rampart. As soon as you rest to this Site, you will have a conversation with Melina. You will also find Boc nearby. After you rest and talk to him, you have 2 options to complete his quest. You can either give him a Larval Tear, which will make him go to Raya Lucaria to be rebirthed and ultimately die OR you can use the Prattling Pate (You're Beautiful) and then talk to him. You don't get any items from either option, however the walkthrough will use the second option to keep Boc alive.

      Move on through the only path available, until you enter a building past a fire-breathing statue and then use the lift to the lower level. In the next room you can climb the ladder to open a chest with a Perfume Bottle and on the next floor you can find another Seedbed Curse. If you exit the balcony on the level with the chest that had a Perfume Bottle, reach the end of the balcony to get an Upper-Class Robe. Return to the bottom and keep moving through the buildings fighting Perfumers and Knights. After the Knight that stands up to fight you, you will see a destroyed convoy. You have now reached the Capital's main street.

      In the street you can find an Erdtree Avatar that will drop a Lord's Rune, which will give you a lot of Runes upon use. The gate at the very east of the main street can be opened and this is where you will have to go after you defeat this region's main boss to continue with the game. At the end of the path you can find a Leonine Misbegotten enemy that guards a Perfume Bottle and an elevator, but once you get to the top, you will be blocked by a mist gate that can be dispelled only after you defeat the main boss. We will return here at the end of the region to continue with the game.

      Return to the main street through the gate. On the left you can enter an alley that has a Knight and many enemies. At the end you can find the Imp Head (Corpse). Opposite from this alley and across the main street you can reach a clearing with a fountain. The building to the left of the fountain has an elevator that takes you to the capital's graveyard, we will return here shortly. The building to the right has a Crucible Knight and after him you can create a shortcut to the East Capital Rampart Site. In the main street keep moving towards the west and to your right you can enter a small building to find the Avenue Balcony Site of Grace. You can also access the graveyard from this place. The areas on the west side of the main street contain a lot of high-grade Runes and upgrade materials if you want to further explore and also features a Lion Guardian enemy. The only item of importance for this walkthrough is a Stonesword Key on top of a gazebo.

      In the graveyard area from the Avenue Balcony Site, if you head down the stairs to the left, you can find a scarab with Ash of War: Thunderbolt. Return to the lift that leads to the graveyard and access the area from there. As you move forward, one of the graves guarded by some skeletons wielding spears has a Stonesword Key. You can find another Stonesword Key in a chest found in a cell in the room full of enemies. The ladder in the same room leads to a shortcut to the Avenue Balcony Site of Grace. If you go to the roofs from where you exited the elevator in the graveyard area, you will find the Black Bow after jumping on a few roofs. Heading to the very west part of the area in a clearing and past a small bridge, you will spawn an Ulcerated Tree Spirit that will drop a Golden Seed. Enter the next building to the south that has a Cracked Pot and move on up a small ladder and into a building with the Lower Capital Church Site of Grace. This building also has Lionel's set and Deathbed Dress. The Omenkiller enemy outside this building drops the Omensmirk Mask.

      Climb down the ladder to the sewers and then take the path to the northeast end past the rats to find the Guilty Hood. If you take the other path into the building and take a left turn and then another left turn, past the Giant Hand enemy you can find Lost Ashes of War. Now take the right path when you enter the building from the sewer level and you will find yourself behind a fire-breathing statue. In the area below one of the corpses has the Erdsteel Dagger. Climb the ladder to find yourself at the base of the statue dragon's wing. You will need to climb it to reach the capital's higher levels, but we have another location to explore first. Straight from here you can also find yourself again in the Avenue Balcony Site of Grace.
    23. Subterranean Shunning-Grounds: This area is located beneath Leyndell. From the Avenue Balcony Site of Grace head towards the dragon statue and jump down the left rail. Then head towards the statue dragon's wing. Beneath the wing you can find a well and you can use a ladder to descend, in order to access this area. This area is also quite complex to navigate, so this section will be detailed as well. Follow the only path until you climb down a ladder and go to a room to the left to find the Underground Roadside Site of Grace.

      In the hall with the Omenkillers you can find the Bloodsoaked Mask and Bloodsoaked Manchettes. Taking the ladder down from the hall you will have to navigate on top of some pipes that has a lot of Imps. At the end of the area you can go up some stairs to enter the area with the Shadow Bait. There is also a lever here that will connect this room with the hallway that has the Site of Grace. Return to the area with the pipes and this time instead of going up the stairs, carefully fall down to the lower pipe and then to the bottom of the area. Here you will find two giant crayfish enemies. One of the corpses in this area has Mohg's Shackle. From here you can also enter the Leyndell Catacombs, which will be covered a bit later. Touch the Site of Grace for easy access later and then head up the lift in the other path. In the area with the ghost archers, there is an illusory wall at the base of the first set of stairs hiding another ghost archer. Then there is another illusory wall that hides a Spirit-Caller Snail enemy that guards Haligdrake Talisman +1. The top of this area creates a shortcut to the area where you found the Shadow Bait.

      Go to the other end of the hallway and drop down the hole. In the sewer past the rats, instead of taking a right turn, continue to the end of the path past some poison flowers and up a ladder. Guarded by a Giant Hand enemy is Lost Ashes of War. Here you can also find Dung Eater and release him by using the Sewer-Gaol Key he gave you. Back in the sewer with the rats you want to take the right turn to continue. In this sewer full of slugs you want to jump over two holes and once you reach the end of the path, go right through the open iron gate. Following this tunnel will lead you to an open area with a ladder that leads back to the hallway with the Omenkillers. Use the lever to create a shortcut to the Site. Return back to the tunnel with the slugs and fall down the first hole you saw to fight some enemies and get a Stonesword Key.

      Opening the door from the hallway, you need to carefully fall down to the lower level. Keep following the path until you descend some stairs and you have the option to descend a ladder or head into another sewer with some poisonous flowers. First, go down the ladder and past a giant poisonous flower and some Death Frogs is the Nomad Ashes. Then go to the tunnel and past some more Death Frogs you will reach an open area with an Omenkiller enemy. Defeating this enemy will give you the Omen Bairn. Climb the ladder and open the door in front of you. This creates yet another shortcut to the Site of Grace.

      From where you created the shortcut, climb on the nearest pipe and reach its end before jumping to the platform on the left. Past the doorway you have to carefully jump onto the next pipe and then through the hole into the pipe. This part is among the most complex in the game and you will easily get lost in here. It is highly recommended to craft some Rainbow Stones, so you are aware of the paths you took. What you are looking for in this area are two holes you can fall into. The first hole takes you to the area with the Death Frogs you passed earlier, while the second hole is the one you need to progress. You will know which is the right hole as there is a corpse with an item at the bottom of the hole you want to fall through. Continue from this hole to reach an elevator, which will take you to the end of the dungeon.

      At the end of this dungeon fight  Mohg, the Omen, who will drop the Bloodflame Talons. Open the chest after the boss for the Erdtree's Favor +1 and then attack the altar behind it to reveal a secret path. The next area is currently blocked by another mist gate and will be accessible after defeating Leyndell's main boss, so we will return here later.
    24. Leyndell Catacombs: Located inside the Shunning-Grounds, at the bottom of the area with the pipes that has two giant crayfish enemies. This dungeon has an interesting gimmick that can be a little hard to explain. As you progress through the dungeon, you will reach a hall with a fire-breathing column. Reach the column, attack to lower it down and then attack again while climbing on it to reach an area with an Omenkiller enemy that guards the Crucible Scale Talisman.

      Continue until you fall down a ledge with a grave in the middle of the area. At this point you may think that you just made a circle, but this is how you progress through the dungeon. The area is exactly the same as before, but you may notice that some extra gloveworts and enemies have appeared. You have to reach the top of the cliff once again and fall once more to the area with the grave. After you fall there for the second time, keep progressing until you drop through a small window. This time instead of going towards the ledge, move back to the hallway with the fire-breathing column. Ride the column to the room that seems the same with the one that you picked up the talisman, but this version of the room has an extra path you can follow that will lead you to the dungeon's lever. Be careful, as if you fall before using the level you'll have to do all of the above to reach it again. The end of the dungeon has the Lord of Blood's Exultation that is dropped by boss Esgar, Priest of Blood.
    25. Return to the Avenue Balcony Site of Grace in Leyndell and head to the base of the statue dragon's wing. Climb the dragon's statue to reach Leyndell's higher levels. Past the first rooms you will find the West Capital Rampart Site of Grace. In front of the Site you can descend some stairs and in a corner you can find the Cane Sword. In the same level you can also use a lever that will connect the bottom area we will explore with the main street. Back on the Site of Grace level when you move forward you will find another Valiant Gargoyle enemy that will drop Gargoyle's Halberd. In front of this Gargoyle you can also find a Golden Seed.

      Before you move to the huge tree root, fall to the area on the right. If you climb on top of the stables and into a window you will enter a version of the Roundtable Hold, called Fortified Manor. This Manor is exactly the same with the Roundtable Hold with the exception that no NPCs are here. If you go to the blacksmith's location, you can find the Sanctified Whetblade and a Hammer. Inside Fia's bedroom you can find a Hero's Rune and the By My Sword gesture. Continue to the Two Fingers' room to find the Coded Sword. Then go to Dung Eater's room to find another Seedbed Curse. To go to the lower level, descend the stairs near the blacksmith's location. In the dining room you can find the Two Fingers' Prayerbook, which you can give to Corhyn or Miriel to get two more Incantations; Lord's Aid and Lord's Heal. Exit to the hallway and go to the location where Alberich invaded you to find Alberich's set. In another room by the hallway you can find a Site of Grace and the Flightless Bird Painting. Since you are close to a Site of Grace, travel to Dominula, Windmill Village to the Site of Grace that you touched after you defeated the Godskin Apostle boss. Go to the south end of the elevated area past a house with three enemies to find a ghost that will drop the Fire's Deadly Sin.

      Return to the Manor's hallway and you will find an invasion sign. This is Bernahl's assassination quest, so interact with it to invade Vangram the Raging Wolf and Errant Sorcerer Wilhelm. You will also be accompanied by Bernahl for this fight. Once you defeat them, you will get the Raging Wolf set. Return to Bernahl in the Volcano Manor in Mt. Gelmir to get Gelmir's Fury. This leaves only one assassination quest for the Volcano Manor. Back in the Manor in Leyndell, go to the hallway and open the door next to the one leading to the Site of Grace. In the courtyard there are two Iron Maiden enemies that guard a Stormhawk Axe. The elevator leads to the area that you were teleported from the waygate in Weeping Peninsula. In the hallway open the main doors to leave the building. On the left as you exit are two Leyndell Knights. One of them drops the Gravel Stone Seal. If you climb to the nearby stables, then run and jump on the scaffold near the Manor, you can find a Stonesword Key.

      Return to the area that you fought the Valiant Gargoyle. Instead of climbing the huge tree root, go underneath it and go towards the big coliseum. As you climb the cliffs, in front of one Duelist enemy you can find the Ritual Shield Talisman. At the end of the path you can find Goldmask and Corhyn. Exhaust the dialogue with them and then look in the alcove behind them to find the Star Fist. Return to the giant tree root and climb it to finally progress through the area. Once you enter a building you will fight  Godfrey, the First Lord, who will drop a Talisman Pouch. Touch the Erdtree Sanctuary Site of Grace.

      Exit the building where you fought the boss and take the nearby elevator. In the next path is an invisible scarab with Barrier of Gold. As you move on, you will occasionally reach a place where you can oversee the giant bolt statue in the center of the city. Carefully fall to the ledge below you and then climb on top of the statue. At the end you can find the legendary armament Bolt of Gransax. Return to the Erdtree Sanctuary Site and move forward and up the tree branch. Before you cross the bridge jump to the roof on the left and then through a window to find the Erdtree Bow in a chest and a Celestial Dew. Then climb to the tree branch to get the Golden Order Principia, which you can give to Corhyn or Miriel to get two more Incantations; Law of Regression and Radagon's Rings of Light. Make sure to buy the Law of Regression as it is required to progress Corhyn's and Goldmask's quest. You need 37 Intelligence to use this incantation. Even if you have not invested in an Intelligence build, if you have followed the walkthrough so far, you will have the necessary equipment to use this spell. You can equip certain talismans and armor in order to increase your total intelligence. Some of these items include:

      Grafted Blade Greatsword's skill (Weapon, but requires 40 Strength and 14 Dexterity to use), Twinsage Glintstone Crown (Helm), Marika's Scarseal (Talisman), Stargazer Heirloom (Talisman). You can also equip Godrick's Great Rune and then use a Rune Arc to increase your Intelligence and all other stats by 5 levels temporarily. Finally, you can mix the Intelligence-Knot Crystal Tear in your Flask of Wondrous Physik and drink the mixture to get another 10 levels temporarily. If this is too big a hassle for you, you can always respec your stats at Rennala and add 37 points to Intelligence, then once you are done with this part of the quest, you can respec again to your primary build.

      With 37 points in Intelligence, go in front of Radagon's statue in the area that you found the Bolt of Gransax after the Erdtree Sanctuary Site. Stand on top of the message and cast the Law of Regression. Then read the new message. With the new information return to Goldmask and Corhyn to exhaust their dialogue and receive the Golden Order Totality gesture. Corhyn will now also sell the Immutable Shield.

      Back in the Erdtree Sanctuary Site move forward to find the next Site of Grace, Queen's Bedchamber. Here you can find the Blessing of the Erdtree. Move on to the next area and defeat  Morgott, the Omen King, who will drop Morgott's Great Rune and Remembrance of the Omen King. Try to enter the Giant Erdtree and then return to rest at the Site of Grace. Melina will now give you the Rold Medallion.
    26. Now that you defeated Morgott, return to the room that you defeated Mohg and go through the hidden path behind the altar. For the next part you have to fall all the way to the bottom. This is an incredibly difficult platforming area, so you should be prepared to die a few times. As you are about to descend, cross the wooden beam. You will see that there is a tent structure that hides an item. In the same area there are some enemies that you need to aggro and bait one of their attacks towards these tents to destroy them. One of the tents in this level has the Nomadic Merchant's set. In a small alcove accessed by one wooden beam you can also find the Frenzied's Cookbook 2. Climb down near the bottom and you will find the Inescapable Frenzy on a corpse. Climb inside the same level and in a corner guarded by a sitting enemy you can get the Fingerprint Stone Shield. At the bottom touch the Site of Grace, Frenzied Flame Proscription. In this room you will find NPC Hyetta and at the end of the path you will find a door.

      BEFORE you do anything, roll to the wall to the right of Hyetta to reveal a secret path. At the end you will find a chest with a Rune Arc. Behind the chest attack the wall again to reveal another secret area. Following this path will take you to Deeproot Depths, a region underground, but we will check this later. For now touch the Site of Grace and return to the previous room.

      To finish Hyetta's quest, you must take off ALL of your equipment and go through the door to inherit the Frenzied Flame by the Three Fingers. If you talk to Hyetta after that, you will receive the Frenzied Flame Seal. Before doing that, make sure to read below:

      IMPORTANT NOTE: By doing so, you will be locked into the Lord of Frenzied Flame Ending (or the game's "bad" ending). You CAN reverse this by following this walkthrough up to the end of Millicent's quest and then going to Crumbling Farum Azula. By reversing the Frenzied Flame, you will be able to get the other endings again. However upon reversing it, then for THIS RUN you will be PERMANENTLY locked out of getting the Lord of Frenzied Flame Ending. Receiving the Frenzied Flame before going to Crumbling Farum Azula for the first time will also have another implication in a cutscene and the conclusion of a character's story (read the spoiler below):

      After you complete the Mountaintops of Giants East section and you reach the Giant's Forge, you will have to burn the Giant Erdtree to continue to Farum Azula. If you don't have the Frenzied Flame at that time, Melina will sacrifice herself in order to burn the tree. If you have the Frenzied Flame, Melina will scorn you and threaten to kill you if you become the Lord of Frenzied Flame, followed by abandoning you. You will then burn the tree yourself. There is no difference to the rest of the story, as you will be able to get any ending you want, if you choose to reverse the Frenzied Flame. The only difference is that in one case Melina will die and in the other case she will survive but still abandon you and hate you for your choice.


      CONCLUSION: It ends up to your personal preference on how you want Melina's
       story to end. This walkthrough will postpone inheriting the Frenzied Flame until we reach Crumbling Farum Azula. If you want the other conclusion to this character, feel free to inherit the Frenzied Flame now and complete Hyetta's quest.
    27. Return to Roundtable and go to Dung Eater's room. Now that you freed him, you will find a message in his place. Return to Leyndell and go to where Boggart is next to Leyndell's east gate. Exhaust his dialogue. Reload the area to receive a Seedbed Curse, Blackguard's Iron Mask, Iron Ball and Blackguard's Bell Bearing. You will then be invaded by Dung Eater. Drops Sword of Milos. Return to Roundtable and talk to Dung Eater. He will ask you to give the Seedbed Curses you have to his corporeal from in Subterranean Shunning-Grounds. You have 4 by this point, which you should hand over to him. We will find the rest in another region.

      *NOTE*: It is at this point that you can give him Seluvis' Potion and receive his Ashes, if you want to obtain them (the walkthrough gave the potion to Gideon). If you decide to give it to him, you must have not completed Seluvis' quest up to the point that you gave Ranni the Fingerslayer Blade after Nokron, Eternal City section. Giving the potion to Dung Eater will not allow you to complete his quest later on.
    28. Talk with Enia in Roundtable and use Morgott's Remembrance to get Morgott's Cursed Sword or Regal Omen Bairn. If you want both items, you can choose to gain another charge for this Remembrance at a bell-bearing Mausoleum. You can also buy the Fell Omen Cloak from her. Gideon also has some additional dialogue now that you defeated Morgott.
    29. Return to Leyndell's main street and then follow the road to the east and up the elevator where the mist gate was blocking your progress before. On the bridge you will find the Flamedrake Talisman +1.
    30. When you reach the second elevator, instead of activating it, head towards the other exit on another bridge to fight the Fell Twins duo boss. Drops Omenkiller Rollo Ashes.
    31. Divine Tower of East Altus: At the end of the bridge past the Fell Twins. At the top activate Morgott's Great Rune.
    32. Return to the elevator that you ignored. When you activate it, look for an opening near the bottom to jump into and get the Blade of Calling and Official's Attire. The opening is towards the southwest direction of the elevator passage in case you have trouble locating it. After you reach the bottom with the elevator, touch the Site of Grace outside. We will explore the region ahead a bit later.

    Deeproot Depths (Underground)





    The Deeproot Depths are the second last underground region we are going to explore. There are two ways to reach this area and we have found both of them during the previous sections. The first way to access it is by resting in the coffin by the waterfall in Siofra Aqueduct after beating the Valiant Gargoyles. The second is by descending the giant tree root from after the two hidden walls in the room with the Frenzied Flame at the bottom of Leyndell. Since nothing of importance is gained from descending the root and it is also very easy to fall off and die, we will use the coffin from Siofra Aqueduct. Both ways lead you to the same starting point.

    1. After you exit the coffin, touch the Site of Grace in front of you. In front of you will be an Erdtree Avatar that drops the Staff of the Avatar.
    2. Past the Avatar, find a scarab guarded by some giant ants with the Ash of War: Golden Land.
    3. Use Torrent and jump on the giant tree roots ahead. One of the roots leads to a cave in the cliffs to your right. These caves have some Ant Queens and multiple ants guarding them. Each of the queens drops a Numen's Rune, which awards a lot of Runes upon use. At the other exit of the cave, you can find the legendary Incantation Elden Stars.
    4. Keep moving forward to the west, until you find the Deeproot Depths Site of Grace.
    5. Head north towards the water until you find a gazebo. A corpse there has the region's map.
    6. East of the map you can find a cave behind a waterfall. Defeat the Runebear enemy inside to get the Prince of Death's Cyst.
    7. Northeast of the map you can enter a building to find Ash of War: Vacuum Slice in front of a fire-breathing statue.
    8. Head west from the map to find The Nameless Eternal City Site of Grace.
    9. Northwest from the Site jump on a Spiritspring to get to the top of the building that has a Stonesword Key.
    10. Northwest from that building fight the Crucible Knight Siluria boss, who drops Siluria's Tree. The chest behind the knight has the Crucible Tree set.
    11. Northeast from the building with the Stonesword Key you will soon see a bell-bearing Mausoleum. To access this one you need to climb to the giant tree roots and then jump on top of it. Destroy the white skulls there to make it sit down. Choose any Shardbearer's Remembrance you have obtained and you may want to gain another charge for or save it for later.
    12. North of the Mausoleum at the very north and near a petrified tree that can be destroyed you can find the Mausoleum Soldier Ashes.
    13. Return to The Nameless Eternal City Site. To the east you can find a giant tree root that you can use to climb to the area's higher levels. On the northwest tower as you are climbing, you can find the Prince of Death's Staff.
    14. At the top of the root path you can find the Across The Roots Site of Grace.
    15. Before you continue, make sure that you have completed Fia's quest up to the point with handing D the Weathered Dagger. This was covered at Caelid after defeating Radahn.
    16. Return to the Across The Roots Site in Deeproot Depths and move on to the big open area. As you head towards the big dragon's body, you will be invaded by Fia's Champions. After the fight you will get Fia's Mist.
    17. After the fight talk to Fia there. You MUST choose the option to let her hug you. Do so and keep talking to her until you give her the Cursemark of Death. Rest at the nearby Site of Grace and then talk to her. Repeat this process until she goes to sleep. Interact with her body to fight  Lichdragon Fortissax. You will get the Remembrance of the Lichdragon and then the Mending Rune of the Death-Prince near Fia. This Rune is required to get a specific version of one ending at the end of the game.
    18. Return to Enia in Roundtable and use the Remembrance to get Fortissax's Lightning Spear or Death Lightning. You can use this Remembrance to gain another charge at any Mausoleum, if you wish to get both spells in the same run.
    19. Return to Fia and you will find Fia's set. Reload the area and talk with D's Twin Brother. Reload it again to find the Twinned Set again and the Inseparable Sword. This concludes Fia's and the D brothers' quests.

    Mountaintops of Giants West







    Found east of Leyndell, this area can only be accessed after defeating Morgott. This area is the continuation of the main quest and is pretty straight-forward, albeit with few Sites of Grace.

    1. From the Forbidden Lands Site of Grace you touched at the end of Leyndell's section, head east. At nights you can find another Night's Cavalry boss who will drop Ash of War: Phantom Slash.
    2. Follow the path and you will soon find a Golden Seed by a gold tree.
    3. North from the Seed you can fight a Black Blade Kindred that drops Gargoyle's Black Axe and Gargoyle's Black Blades.
    4. Grand Lift of Rold: Found at the end of the icy path. Here you can use the Rold Medallion you got from Melina to access the rest of the region.
    5. Follow the path from the top of the grand lift and you will soon find a pillar with the region's map.
    6. Zamor Ruins: Found a little east after the map. Near the Site of Grace you can find Yura to finish his quest. Talk with him. You can either attack him now or wait until you have inherited the Frenzied Flame. Either way you will get the Ronin's set. The walkthrough author fought Yura now. In the south section of the ruins you can find the Zamor Ice Storm and in the underground chamber near the spell you can find the Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing 3 that you should give to the Twin Maiden Husks for level 5 and 6 upgrade materials of normal armaments.
    7. Make your way to the beginning of the long bridge. Near the Fire Monks by a tree to the west you can find the Briars of Punishment.
    8. Giants' Mountaintop Catacombs: Southeast from the beginning of the long bridge. Contains Fire Monk Ashes that you can find in a chest after going below the dungeon's first elevator. Also contains Glovewort Picker's Bell Bearing 2 and a Golden Seed, both dropped by boss Ulcerated Tree Spirit. You should give the bell to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable for level 4, 5 & 6 upgrade materials of normal ashes. The chest after the boss contains another Deathroot, which you should take to the Beast Clergyman for Beastclaw Warhammer.
    9. Cross the long bridge. On the way as you cross it you can find a Lost Ashes of War on a corpse.
    10. After the bridge you will find the Ancient Snow Valley Ruins Site of Grace, where you will find Millicent. Exhaust the dialogue with her to continue her quest
    11. Shack of the Lofty: Continue north from the Site of Grace until you reach the frozen river, then turn west. The shack is near the end of the river. Contains a Traveling Maiden set.
    12. A little east of the shack you can find the invasion sign from Tanith's assassination quest. Invade and defeat Juno Hoslow, Knight of Blood, who will drop Hoslow's set and Hoslow's Petal Whip. Wait for him to complete his bow when he appears to get the Hoslow's Oath gesture.
    13. With this we have completed every assassination quest from the Volcano Manor, so it is time to return and finish the area. Talk to Tanith to get Taker's Cameo. You will then be asked to meet their lord, who is  Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy. As you enter the boss room, make sure to pick Serpent-Hunter. Defeat him and he will drop Rykard's Great Rune and the Remembrance of the Blasphemous. You will also find the Eye Surcoat.
    14. Take the Remembrance to Enia in Roundtable and choose between Rykard's Rancor OR Blasphemous Blade. If you want both items, you can choose to gain another charge for this Remembrance at a bell-bearing Mausoleum.
    15. Go to the Divine Tower of West Altus, which is now unlocked and go to the top to activate Rykard's Great Rune.
    16. Return to Tanith and talk to her. Make sure to also talk to Bernahl and Patches. The ghost at the end of the hallway has left for you the My Thanks gesture. Reloading the area will make all NPCs disappear from the manor.
    17. Go to Rya's location where we last left her. Talk to her and DO NOT give her the Tonic of Forgetfulness, then reload the area to find Daedicar's Woe and Zorayas' Letter. This concludes her quest.
    18. Go to the Shaded Castle right before the boss room in the hallway with the statues, where you will find Patches. Talk to him to receive Dancer's Castanets.
    19. Return to the room where you killed Rykard. Here you will find Tanith. You can give her the Dancer's Castanets, although nothing will happen. As of Patch 1.03, this is the end of her quest. If you attack and kill her, you will get the Consort's set. You will then be invaded by Tanith's Knight, who will drop Aspects of the Crucible: Breath. With this we have completed everything in the Volcano Manor.

      As of Patch 1.04, after giving the Castanets to Tanith, Patches moves back to his original location, in Murkwater Cave in West Limgrave. If you enter his room, you will initiate a boss fight with him, however once he notices that it is you he's fighting, he will immediately surrender. Talk to him after that to receive Patches' Crouch gesture. Reloading the area will return him to his merchant status. This is the end of his quest. If you attack and kill him, he will drop Spear +7, a Leather set and Patches' Bell Bearing. Since it is the only way to obtain the Leather set, feel free to take him down to get every item.
    20. Return to the Ancient Snow Valley Ruins Site of Grace in the Mountaintops. Head north like last time until you reach the frozen river, but this time turn east. After you pass the Minor Erdtree, turn right into the small valley to find a Golden Seed.
    21. Continue further east to reach the frozen lake. Near the cliffs to the north you will find the Freezing Lake Site of Grace.
    22. Heretical Rise: Across the frozen river. You can access this only from the north side of the cliff. As you head west from the cliff above the Freezing Lake Site of Grace, you will find a broken bridge with some skeleton enemies guarding it. The path leading to the tower is an invisible bridge. You can throw Rainbow Stones, that you can craft with your Crafting Kit by using Ruin Fragments, on the ground in front of you to see if you can safely move forward or if you will fall to your death. Once you get inside, you will find the legendary sorcery Founding Rain of Stars in a chest at the top. This is also the last legendary spell you need for  Legendary Sorceries and Incantations.
    23. Following the path to the west you will find some giant skeletons spawning. This is the work of a Tibia Mariner enemy, who can be found in the northern part of the elevated area overlooking the castle. The enemy will drop Helphen's Steeple and another Deathroot. Take the Deathroot to the Beast Clergyman to get Gurranq's Beast Claw.
    24. Stargazer's Ruins: Reach the west end of the elevated area, then cross the bridge heading south to find the ruins. Inside the ruined buildings you can find a Spirit Jellyfish. Summon your own Spirit Jellyfish Ashes to unlock the underground area. That way you will also get the Fancy Spin gesture. Underground you will find in a chest the Primal Glintstone Blade.
    25. Follow the low path to the west side of the area. At the end you will find a merchant, who sells Missionary's Cookbook 7 and a Vagabond set.
    26. Back in the Stargazer's Ruins, follow the path leading to the bridge heading southeast. In the middle of the bridge you will find Corhyn and Goldmask. Talk to them to continue their quest. At this point you can offer to give the Tonic of Forgetfulness to Corhyn. This is entirely optional and will only affect the final location where you will find Corhyn and some of his dialogue. Since there is no difference to the rewards you will get, feel free to choose whatever you want. The walkthrough went with not offering the Tonic to him.
    27. Minor Erdtree: At the north part of the next area after you cross the bridge. Defeat another Erdtree Avatar boss to get the Cerulean Crystal Tear and the Crimson Bubbletear.
    28. Return to the bridge you took to reach the Stargazer's Ruins. Descend the cliffs to the north and you will fight another Death Rite Bird, which will drop Death Ritual Spear.
    29. Head east. Outside the castle you will find another bell-bearing Mausoleum. This is just like the first Mausoleum we found, simply ride with Torrent and attack the white crystals on its feet to make it sit down.

      NOTE: This Mausoleum is bugged for many players. It appears that if it gets bugged, you will not be able to regain a charge for any Remembrance. If this happens to you, you have run out of luck and you will gain one less Remembrance charge for your run.
    30. Castle Sol: At the northern part of the region. Despite being smaller in size than most castles in the game, this is still the biggest area of the region. In the castle's ground level find a tower in the northwest part of the castle that has a lot of slugs inside. Climb the ladder then follow the path to the end until you find a ladder to descend into a room that has the Sorcerer Painting. On the same roof if you jump on top of the tents, you can then jump onto a platform to the side of the building guarded by an Eagle enemy to find a Stormhawk Axe.

      Continue until you reach the Church of the Eclipse Site of Grace. In the church you can find the legendary armament Eclipse Shotel. After exiting the church instead of heading into the next building, go the right side and climb a ladder. This will lead you to an area with a dual-wielding Phantom Banished Knight enemy that guards the Cerulean Amber Medallion +1. Back in the room after the church, once you head outside you will see the mist gate that has the boss. Instead of going there, you can drop to the roof on the left and go to the end of the area with another Phantom Banished Knight enemy accompanied by two dog enemies. Up the stairs you can find a Stonesword Key.

      At the end of the castle fight  Commander Niall, who will drop Veteran's Prosthesis. In the tower past him you can find the Haligtree Secret Medallion (Left) on top of the tower.
    31. With the Sorcerer Painting go to the bridge that leads to Stargazer's Ruins to find a ghost on the east side that will drop the Greathood.
    32. If you head to the Roundtable, Enia will now sell the Veteran set for defeating Commander Niall.
    33. Go to Jarburg and talk with both Diallos and Jar-Bairn. Talk to them, then reload the area and talk to Jar-Bairn. Once you reload the area, you will get Diallos' Mask, another Hoslow's Petal Whip and a Numen Rune.

    Consecrated Snowfield Part 1





    Before we continue with the main story, there are still two more regions that we can explore. Now that we have obtained both halves of the Haligtree Secret Medallion, we can access the Consecrated Snowfield region. The enemies here are extremely strong, so be well prepared. We will split this region into two sub-sections, as the northern part is quite possibly the hardest area in the game, so you should be as high leveled as possible.

    1. Go to the Grand Lift of Rold at the bottom level. Instead of hoistering the Medallion of Rold, choose to hoister the Haligtree Secret Medallion. You will then be transferred to the new region.
    2. Hidden Path to the Haligtree: While you can follow the path to find the Site of Grace and access the rest of the region, there is a secret area that has some items and a boss. From the dungeon's Site of Grace go back towards the Grand Lift. After the octopus that falls from the ceiling and one set of stairs you will see that one part of the railing is missing. Carefully falling directly below will land you on an invisible bridge. You should craft a lot of Rainbow Stones to throw in front of you, so you will know if there is solid ground in front or not. The nearest alcove in the bridge where you landed has a glovewort. One of the walls there can be attacked and will reveal a chest with the Silver Scarab. When you reach the enemy boss room, instead of heading in go to the right path and follow it to the end and up an elevator to get the Spelldrake Talisman +2. Also contains the Blackflame Monk Amon Ashes that is dropped by boss Stray Mimic Tear. The chest in the boss room has the final Deathroot, which you can give to the Beast Clergyman to get an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone, the material required to max level a normal armament. This concludes his quest.
    3. After you exit the dungeon, you will find yourself in a snowfield with very bad visibility as there is a heavy storm going on. Your first order of business here is to find the map, which is due north. As you head north, touch the Consecrated Snowfield Site of Grace. This can be a little difficult to locate but you can find it if you head straight north from the exit and look near some small rocks. Keep heading north until you find the Inner Consecrated Snowfield Site of Grace just before the frozen river. Further north and past the frozen river you can find the region's map.
    4. Return to the Consecrated Snowfield Site. To the west you can find a Rotten Duelist enemy roaming between some sarcophagi that will drop the Rotten Duelist Helm and Rotten Battle Hammer.
    5. North and a little east from the Consecrated Snowfield Site you can find the Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 23. It is a little difficult to locate as the corpse having this item is on the open field. To find it easily, look straight north when at the Site, then slowly rotate your camera towards the east and stop just before you reach the end of the first box in the compass. Head straight for it and you will find the corpse with the item.
    6. Northeast from the Cookbook you can find Golden Seed by a gold tree. It is tucked on the southeast side of the big tree.
    7. Northeast from the the Consecrated Snowfield Site you can find a convoy. In the chest on the back you can find St. Trina's Torch. To easily locate the convoy follow the path as is in the map that goes between the two lines heading northeast.
    8. Consecrated Snowfield Catacombs: Located in the snowfield's eastern part, east of the convoy where you found St. Trina's Torch. Contains Imp Head (Elder) in front of a Watchdog enemy on the second floor. The boss Putrid Grave Warden Duelist at the end of the dungeon drops 2 Great Grave Gloveworts and the Rotten Gravekeeper Cloak.
    9. Southwest from the Inner Consecrated Snowfield Site you can find another convoy. In the chest on the back you can find the Flowing Curved Sword. Near the convoy during nights you can fight the duo boss Night's Cavalry, that will drop the Night's Cavalry set and an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone. You do not have to fight both of the Night's Cavalry at the same time, you can lure one away and then the next one.
    10. Southwest from the boss you can find the Stalwart Horn Charm +1. It is by a fallen tree log guarded by a singing Ancestral Follower enemy.
    11. Yelough Anix Ruins: On the southwestern part of the region. Contains a Stonesword Key on the northwestern part in a small tower guarded by some rats. Also contains the Unendurable Frenzy in a chest underground. This area can be accessed only with Torrent by double jumping inside a closed area from the central part of the ruins.
    12. Yelough Anix Tunnel: South from the ruins. Contains an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone at the bottom of a small canyon in the cave guarded by an Onyx Lord enemy. Also has the Meteorite of Astel that is dropped by boss Astel, Stars of Darkness.
    13. Albinauric Rise: Northeast from the Inner Consecrated Snowfield Site. To unlock the entrance you need to summon your Fanged Imp Ashes and kill one of the Imps outside the tower. Your summons do not need to kill the enemy, you have to kill the Imp while your own Imps are on the field. Contains the Graven-Mass Talisman at the top.
    14. Southwest from the tower you can find the Glintstone Craftsman's Cookbook 8. It is guarded by three giant magic ball enemies.
    15. Cave of the Forlorn: Northeast from Albinauric Rise on the frozen river. Guarded by a giant octopus, you need to use 2 Stonesword Keys to unlock the entrance. Contains the legendary armament Golden Order Greatsword that is dropped by boss Misbegotten Crusader.
    16. East of the cave you can fight the Great Wyrm Theodorix. It will drop 3 Dragon Hearts that you can use for another Incantation in the Cathedral of Dragon Communion. Defeating this boss will also unlock the Theodorix's Magma for purchase in the Cathedral. On the icy ledges east of the wyrm you can find another Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone.
    17. Minor Erdtree: North of the cave. Fight the Putrid Avatar boss to get Ruptured Crystal Tear and the Thorny Cracked Tear.
    18. West of the map's location you can find an invisible scarab that will drop the Ash of War: White Shadow's Lure. You can spot its location by looking at the pack of wolves that is following it.
    19. A little to the northwest and on the river you will be invaded for the final time by Anastasia, Tarnished-Eater, who will drop a Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone, the material required to max level a special armament.


      NOTE: This and the next invasion are very buggy. There is a chance that you will not be invaded by Anastasia at all. It is unknown what is causing this issue. During the author's two runs of the game, Anastasia invaded during the first run at this point of the game, while at the second run she never invaded. Luckily she doesn't drop anything unique, so you shouldn't worry if she is bugged for you too.
    20. To the west by the river you can find a Stonesword Key on a corpse sitting on a chair on the top of a small cliff. Be careful as there are two lightning energy balls guarding the corpse.
    21. Head further west in the middle of the frozen trees and you will be invaded by a Sanguine Noble. Drops the Sanguine set.

      NOTE: This is another invasion that is extremely buggy and it is inconsistent when you will be invaded. Just like Anastasia in one of the author's runs this invasion never happened. If your invasion is bugged as well, unfortunately it seems you ran out of luck on getting the Sanguine set. Your best bet is to keep returning here regularly as you progress through the game and hope you will get invaded. Players have reported that they managed to get the invasion even during the post-game. Good luck.
    22. Go to the northwestern part of the region to find the last bell-bearing Mausoleum outside a church. Be careful while trying to make this one sit down, as it will be shooting magical beams and be more mobile than the rest. The white skulls are located on its feet.
    23. Apostate Derelict: North of the mausoleum. Contains the Silver Mirrorshield. In front of the large woman summon Latenna to get a Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone. This completes her quest.
    24. Southeast from the Derelict at the end of the frozen river during nights you can find another Death Rite Bird boss that will drop Explosive Ghostflame.
    25. Southeast from the boss towards the town and on top of the cliffs you can find another Rotten Duelist enemy that will drop the Rotten Duelist Greaves and Rotten Greataxe.
    26. Ordina, Liturgical Town: At the northern end of the snowfield. Approach the city from the south and then go to the west part outside the city to find a Golden Seed. Under the stairs at the northern end you can find the Black Knife set. Up the stairs to the north is a waygate that is de-activated. You need to solve the town's puzzle before you can activate it. You must enter the Evergaol nearby and in there find and light the 4 candles held by statues. This is an incredibly difficult part as there are invisible Black Knife Assassins trying to hunt you down, as well as magical archers on the roofs. Luckily you can make suicide runs to light some of the candles as when one candle is lit, it will remain so even if you die.

      After you light all 4 candles, the waygate is activated. However, before we use it, we will visit another region first.
    27. West of where the Sanguine Noble invaded you, you can find another waygate covered in blood and guarded by a sole albinauric enemy. Using this waygate will take you to Mohgwyn Palace. Alternatively, you can use the Pureblood Knight's Medal you got from Varre, way back in Liurnia Lake.

    Mohgwyn Palace (Underground)





    The last underground region of the game is also extremely difficulty, being one of the final areas of the game. The enemies here are very high leveled, but thankfully the region is quite small in comparison to other areas. You can access this place by using the waygate in the Consecrated Snowfield or using the Pureblood Knight's Medal you get from Varre's quest.

    1. To get the region's map, it is recommended to use the Pureblood Knight's Medal first, as it will teleport right next to it. Pick the map from the corpse, then use the waygate in the Snowfield to get close to the Palace Approach Ledge-Road Site of Grace.
    2. At this point return to Roundtable and talk to Gideon. He will give you Fevor's Cookbook 3 for discovering the Mausoleum.
    3. From the Palace Approach Ledge-Road Site follow the path to the north. To the right, as you ride through the bloody river, you will find a cave that has a scarab with Ash of War: Blood Tax.
    4. A little to the north inside another cave you will find Swarm of Flies.
    5. Instead of heading towards the palace by following the bloody river to the north, follow the river to the west to reach the bloody lake. Here you will be invaded by 3 Nameless White Mask enemies, one at a time. After defeating all of them you will get the War Surgeon set. This can be extremely hard, especially with the first invader as you will also be attacked by a giant bird. Your best bet for the first invader is to clear the Albinauric enemies before you trigger the invasion and then lure the invader back here so the bird cannot get to you.
    6. In the island in the middle of the bloody lake you can find a Golden Seed.
    7. In the southeast part of the bloody lake at the edge of the cliff find the Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook 24.
    8. In the southwest part of the bloody lake in a graveyard area find the Haligdrake Talisman +2.
    9. Mohgwyn Dynasty Mausoleum: Head to the palace. On your way to the top find a Lord's Rune, a Great Ghost Glovewort in the cave, a Numen's Rune, a Stonesword Key and a merchant that sells Lost Ashes of War. The merchant is located in the tunnels and can be found if you hug the left wall and follow it until you find him.
    10. When you reach the Dynasty Mausoleum Midpoint Site of Grace, head back towards the tunnels and you will find an invasion sign. Invade Varre and defeat him to get Varre's Bouquet. This concludes his questline. You can then move past the Site of Grace and you will find a Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone in front of a statue to your left that Albinauric enemies are revering.
    11. At the end of the area defeat  Mohg, Lord of Blood, who will drop Mohg's Great Rune and the Remembrance of the Blood Lord.
    12. Take Mohg's Remembrance to Enia in Roundtable to choose between Mohgwyn's Sacred Spear OR Bloodboon. If you want both items, you can choose to gain another charge for this Remembrance at a bell-bearing Mausoleum. Enia will also sell Lord of Blood's Robe. Talk to Gideon after defeating Mohg to get Law of Casuality.
    13. Go to the top of Divine Tower of East Altus to activate Mohg's Great Rune.

    Consecrated Snowfield Part 2





    We will now finish up the Consecrated Snowfield. What follows is the hardest area of the game. Max out your armaments' level and bring your best possible equipment, while you have reached a level higher than at least 110. You were warned.

    1. Use the waygate at the end of Ordina, Liturgical Town to reach the next area. The moment you do so, return to Roundtable and talk with Gideon again who will give you Black Flame's Protection for discovering the Haligtree.
    2. Miquella's Haligtree: The moment you are teleported in this area you will be on a branch. If you turn back, carefully reach the corpse to find a Stonesword Key. The branches can be a bit complicated to navigate. Your goal here is to make your way towards the "main branch". You will know it's the right one as some giant ants will be walking on it. In order to progress you must head towards the giant tree, however take the other way past the giant ants and reach the end of the main branch. Guarded by a giant Envoy enemy you can find the Envoy Crown. Moving towards the giant tree, on the left there is a branch with two Envoy enemies using their horns. At the end of this branch past 3 enemies, you can find the Prattling Pate (My Beloved). Back in the main branch you will reach a point where a Giant Envoy enemy is throwing holy bubbles at you, while there are a lot of rotten flowers. If you look to your right, you can carefully fall to another branch. At the end of this branch you can reach the Giant Envoy enemy. Move a little forward and guarded by another Giant Envoy enemy you can find the Oracle Envoy Ashes. Continue on until you reach the next Site of Grace.
    3. From the Haligtree Town Site of Grace move forward and climb the ladder you will first see. On the top run and jump to the next platform. Guarded by a Lion Misbegotten enemy you can find the Pearldrake Talisman +2. Move on through the town. You will soon reach another Lion Misbegotten enemy that guards an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone. A little after this enemy you can rest at the Haligtree Town Plaza Site of Grace. Jump through the roofs until you reach one with 3 Spirit-Caller Snails that summon 3 Crystallian enemies. In the building beneath this roof you can find Viridian Amber Medallion +2. Proceed through the rest of the town. When you reach the area with the two Battlemages, make sure to activate the elevator to create a shortcut to the first Site of Grace.
    4. At the end of the Haligtree fight  Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree, who will drop Loretta's War Sickle and Loretta's Mastery. After you defeat her, return to Enia in Roundtable and she will sell the Royal Knight set. Past the boss and down a ladder, before you use the elevator to proceed, climb this building through the stairs and find an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone in a chest.
    5. Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree: Right after Miquella's Haligtree, this is another complex area and the hardest area of the game. Due to the above this section of the walkthrough will also be quite detailed. At the first Site of Grace, Prayer Room, you can find Millicent. Exhaust the dialogue to continue her quest. In the next pathway there are two doors to enter through to the left. You want to get in the second room, climb the stairs and then jump to the tree branch. Make your way to the tower at the end to find Miquellan Knight's Sword. Descend the ladder from this tower and jump on the next pathway. Here you can fight a Putrid Avatar that will drop the Rotten Staff. In this pathway you can also pick up a Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone, required to max level a special armament.

      Return to the pathway after the Prayer Room Site. As you move forward, you will find two set of stairs one going up and the other going down. Take the stairs that go down, then take the next one. Instead of heading into the next room that has two Cleanrot Knights, turn back and go past the ledge to find a door that can be unlocked by using a Stonesword Key. In the room past the mist gate you can find the Triple Rings of Light. Go back just outside the room with the two Cleanrot Knights. Instead of entering the room, jump over the railing to your right on to the support beam. Carefully fall to the lower level and enter inside the dark building to find a Seedbed Curse.

      Go back to the place where you had to choose to go up or down the stairs at the pathway after the Prayer Room Site. Go down one set of stairs, then carefuly jump to the support beam below and then to the platform that has a gazebo and a flying scarab there. In the small room there is a chest guarded by a Cleanrot Knight that has the legendary summon Cleanrot Knight Finlay Ashes, the last one you need for the  Legendary Ashen Remains. From the gazebo jump to the next support beam and take it all the way to the top. Inside the small alcove you can find a chest with another Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone. In the ledge just outside you will find a corpse with the final Seedbed Curse. Jump to the tree branch to go to the lower level, then jump to the next support beam and cross it to reach an area with some rotten flower and another flying scarab. Fall to the staircase below and go to the top to find some enemies mounting 2 ballistas. Here you can find the Haligtree Soldier Ashes. The room below the platform with the rotten flowers has two Cleanrot Knights. If you exit to the ledge from this room, you can find a ladder that will take you to the lowest level. In this long pathway a lot of Revenant enemies will spawn. In one alcove to your left as you cross the pathway you can find a Lord's Rune. The very end of the pathway has a mist gate that can be unlocked by using 2 Stonesword Keys. Inside you can find the legendary talisman Marika's Soreseal.

      Return to where you found the Haligtree Soldier Ashes. In the platform below you can see one Putrid Avatar enemy that will drop a Lord's Rune upon defeat. The room it guards has the next Site of Grace, Elphael Inner Wall, so rest there first if you need to. Near the Site of Grace you can find a ladder that will lead you to the Haligtree Knight Helm. In the next room behind the big root you can see 3 Crystallian enemies guarding a chest with the Rotten Crystal Sword. Past this room is another dreadful rotten lake, so quickly make it to the other side. Enter the tunnel and continue to find the Drainage Channel Site of Grace. Return to the entrance of the tunnel and climb the nearest hill, then cross the gap onto another tree branch. In the smaller rotten lake you can find a Great Grave Glovewort. However, as soon as you step in, an Ulcerated Tree Spirit enemy will spawn. Defeating it will give you a Golden Seed. After defeating it, two summon signs will appear in the same platform. Here comes another choice you will have to make on how to end Millicent's quest. You will have to either summon and help her defeat her sisters OR invade and kill her. Doing either option will yield a different reward. This means that if you want to get everything, you will have to complete her quest a second time in another playthrough and choose the other option.

      Choosing to summon her will lead you to a fight against Millicent's Four Sisters and award you the Rotten Winged Sword Insignia and the Unalloyed Gold Needle.
      Choosing to invade her will lead you to a fight against Millicent and award you Millicent's Prosthesis.

      The walkthrough will follow the path where we help her instead of killing her. After the fight talk to her, then reload the area to get the Unalloyed Gold Needle once more. You will NOT get the Needle if you chose to kill her, so if you have inherited the Frenzied Flame by this point, be aware that you will not be able to reverse the Flame without this item and by extension you will not be able to get all 3 endings in one playthrough. This concludes Millicent's quest.

      Go back to the Drainage Channel Site of Grace to move on. Climb the tree root and keep moving north until you reach the roof of a building. Carefully fall through the hole onto the wooden beams and then on the high level. Here you will find a chest with the legendary talisman Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman. Exit the building and take a left turn. At the end of this path guarded by many enemies you will find the Ghost-Glovewort Picker's Bell Bearing 3, which unlocks levels 7, 8 & 9 of upgrade materials for renowned ashes, once given to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold. Go back in the building and take the elevator for the next Site of Grace. Before you enter the boss room, look inside the room in the same hallway to find the Traveler's set.
    6. At the end of the area you will fight  Malenia, Blade of Miquella, the hardest boss in the game. Once defeated, she will drop Malenia's Great Rune and the Remembrance of the Rot Goddess. After the boss fight use the Unalloyed Gold Needle at the huge flower in the boss arena to get Miquella's Needle and a Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone.
    7. Return to Roundtable and talk to Enia. Use Malenia's Remembrance to choose between Hand of Malenia OR Scarlet Aeonia. If you want both items, you can choose to gain another charge for this Remembrance at a bell-bearing Mausoleum. Enia will also sell Malenia's set. Talk to Gideon after defeating Malenia to get Lord's Divine Fortification.
    8. Go to the Subterranean Shunning-Grounds and give the last two Seedbed Curses to Dung Eater. You will then receive the Mending Rune of the Fell Curse. This Rune is required to get a specific version of one ending. Reload the area to find the Omen set in his place.

      NOTE: If you decided to give Seluvis' Potion to Dung Eater to get his Ashes, then this step is unavailable.
    9. Isolated Divine Tower: In Leyndell, travel to the Divine Bridge Site of Grace. This is the area you were teleported to from the Tower of Return in Weeping Peninsula. You can also access it by taking the elevator from the courtyard in the Fortified Manor (copy of Roundtable Hold). After using the elevator, the waygate at the end of the area will be activated. Use it to be teleported to the tower. Go to the top to activate Malenia's Great Rune.
    10. Return to Gowry in Caelid. Since we helped Millicent, you can talk to him. At this point he will stop acting as a merchant. You can kill him to get Flock's Canvas Talisman and Gowry's Bell Bearing.
      If you invaded Millicent, then you will find Gowry dead and you can pick his items immediately.

    Mountaintops of Giants East





    Now that we have cleared every optional region, it is time to continue with the main story. You have to access the east part of the Mountaintops of Giants.

    1. The map is in the middle of the region and it will make things easier when it comes to navigation. Head to its location ignoring everything in between. From the Freezing Lake Site of Grace, ride with Torrent all the way to the south, then follow the path to the west. Continue south crossing the huge chain across the canyon. On the other side of the chain you will find a pillar with the region's map.
    2. East of the Freezing Lake Site you can find an invisible scarab circling a tree that will drop Ash of War: Seppuku.
    3. Spiritcaller Cave: On the west side of the frozen lake. Requires 2 Stonesword Keys to be unlocked. Contains a Hero's Rune and the White Reed set. Also contains Black Flame Ritual & Goskin Swaddling Cloth, both dropped by bosses Godskin Apostle, Godskin Noble and Spirit-Caller Snail which are fought successively.
    4. In the middle of the frozen lake you can fight the boss Borealis the Freezing Fog, who will drop a Dragon Heart. You can use it for another Incantation in the Cathedral of Dragon Communion. Defeating this boss will also unlock the Borealis' Mist for purchase in the Cathedral, which is also the last incantation you can purchase from there.
    5. First Church of Marika: On the southeastern part of the frozen lake. Contains a Sacred Tear and the Somberstone Miner's Bell Bearing 3 (found on a corpse just outside the church), which unlocks levels 5 & 6 of upgrade materials for special armaments, once given to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold.
    6. Lord Contender's Evergaol: Contains Vyke's Dragonbolt and Fingerprint set, both dropped by boss Roundtable Knight Vyke.
    7. Guardians' Garrison: West from the evergaol and before the huge chain you cross to get the map. Fight the Chief Guardian Arghanthy at the top of the garrison for the One-Eyed Shield. Contains a Stonesword Key and Giant's Prayerbook that you can give to Corhyn or Miriel for more incantations, Giantsflame Take Thee and Flame, Fall Upon Them.
    8. A little south from the garrison you can find a scarab on a tree with the Ash of War: Prelate's Charge.
    9. Giant-Conquering Hero's Grave: Head west from the map's location and then jump over the canyon to the Mountaintops of Giants West region. The entrance will be on top of a cliff you couldn't access before from the West region. Thankfully unlike the rest Hero's Grave dungeons this one is not so hard to complete. On top of that it is also the last Hero's Grave dungeon in the game. Use a Stonesword Key in the beginning area to get the Flame, Protect Me. Like in the Hero's Grave dungeon in Altus Plateau you have to lure enemies to the light in the ground to be able to damage them. After the first fire-breathing pillar, climb the next stairs and use another Stonesword Key to get the Cranial Vessel Candlestand. The boss' doors will be unlocked once you kill the Giant Troll enemy. You first need to lure it to the light at the beginning of the area. You can activate the light by stepping on the elevator you took to reach this level and then quickly stepping out of it. Before you go to the boss, fall off the bridge that had the Giant Troll to find the Giant's Seal and a Great Grave Glovewort. The boss Ancient Hero of Zamor drops the Zamor set and Zamor Curved Sword.
    10. As you head to the southern part of the region, you will be invaded by Bloody Finger Okina. Drops Rivers of Blood and Okina Mask.
    11. Church of Repose: West from the invasion point. Contains a Sacred Tear.
    12. Southeast from the church on top of the rocks (accessed by a Spiritspring) you can find Ash of War: Troll's Roar and an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone in the giant skull's mouth.
    13. Head east to find the Foot of the Forge Site of Grace.
    14. From the Site head north and then east following the upper path. Under a golden tree you will find a Golden Seed.
    15. To the very north of this elevated area you can get the Crimsonwhorl Bubbletear. The tear is found at the end of a path that is full of hand enemies.
    16. Flame Peak: Return to the Foot of the Forge Site and head east. Fight the  Fire Giant, who will drop the Remembrance of the Fire Giant. Take it to Enia in Roundtable for Giant's Red Braid OR Burn O Flame! If you want both items, you can choose to gain another charge for this Remembrance at any Mausoleum.
    17. Forge of the Giants: East after the boss fight. At the top touch the Site of Grace and rest. This is the last chance you have to choose if you want to inherit the Frenzied Flame, because the cutscene that will follow once you talk to Melina will determine her fate. Read #26 in Leyndell, Royal Capital section for more information. The walkthrough will inherit the Frenzied Flame AFTER the cutscene. Once the cutscene is over, you will get the  Erdtree Aflame and be transported to the next region.

      Make sure to also complete every NPC's quest before burning the Erdtree. NPCs like Corhyn will automatically die when you burn the tree, if you haven't done certain steps of their quests.

    Crumbling Farum Azula





    This region is the second-last area of the game and is located at the very east part of the Lands Between's map. This is also the only region that you do not need to get a map for, as everything is uncovered the moment you are transported here. The region can be a little hard to navigate on the map, since it is a circular area.

    Take note that once you complete this region, everything inside (not underground) the city of Leyndell, Royal Capital will be permanently lost for this run. Completion of this region will also lead to the death of certain NPCs and consequently failure of their quests, so make sure to do all of them before you continue here.

    1. Progress through the new region until you find the first Site of Grace, Crumbling Beast Grave.
    2. It is at this time that the walkthrough decided to inherit the Frenzied Flame. Be warned that once you do so, you are locked into the game's "bad" ending until you reverse this option. Reversing it will PERMANENTLY lock you out of the "bad" ending for this run, because you are not able to inherit the Flame for a second time. So if you want to get all trophies/achievements in one run, then do NOT inherit the Flame until the Endings section or at the very least inherit it now, but do NOT reverse the Frenzied Flame until the walkthrough tells you to do so. If you decided to not assist Millicent at the end of her quest in Elphael and you invaded her, then you will not have Miquella's Needle by this point, meaning you will not be able to reverse the Frenzied Flame at any point during this playthrough. If that's the case, do NOT inherit the flame until you reach the Endings section.

      Be certain that you have followed the walkthrough closely so far if you decide to get the Flame. Make sure to also talk to Hyetta when you inherit it to complete her quest and to receive the Frenzied Flame Seal.
    3. In Roundtable Hold, Gideon, Enia and Roderika will have some new dialogue, if you are interested in the lore/story.
    4. If Nepheli is still at the Roundtable Hold at this point, go to Fort Haight in East Limgrave and talk with Kenneth Haight. Then travel to the area where you fought Godrick in Stormveil Castle. Rest at the Site of Grace there, then move to throne room after the boss room. Nepheli, Kenneth Haight and Gatekeeper Gostoc (supposing you didn't kill him to get your Runes back) will be there. You can talk to them thus completing their quests. Nepheli will give you an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone and Gostoc will have another one in his stock for selling.

      NOTE: If you decided to give Seluvis' Potion to Nepheli to get her Ashes, then this step is unavailable.
    5. Return to Farum Azula. Continue past the Site and soon you will encounter an unnamed Dragon enemy that will drop an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone.
    6. Right after the dragon you will reach the Crumbling Beast Grave Depths Site of Grace. In the room after the Site of Grace, exit through the window, then carefully jump on the roof. Get back in via the window and find a Great Grave Glovewort on this floor. On the bottom floor of the same building you can find the Ancient Dragon Prayerbook that you can give to Corhyn or Miriel for more Incantations; Ancient Dragons' Lightning Spear and Ancient Dragons' Lightning Strike. Exit the building at the southwest part and use the elevator to go to a lower level. At the end of this area guarded by a giant Wormface enemy you can find the Glovewort Picker's Bell Bearing 3 in a gazebo. Return to the building with the prayerbook and exit through the other door to find the Tempest Facing Balcony Site of Grace. The corpse near the Site of Grace has the Somberstone Miner's Bell Bearing 4, which unlocks levels 7 & 8 of upgrade materials for special armaments, once given to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold. Jump through the platforms to reach the next building.
    7. As you move through the platforms you will soon reach a Site of Grace, Dragon Temple. Enter the building and past the enemies patrolling it. On the same floor you will soon reach a balcony with an enemy guarding a Stonesword Key.

      Return to the Site of Grace. Look towards the way you came from and jump down at some stairs. Instead of jumping down again, follow this path until you climb up a ladder. Inside the building after the ladder you can find a Great Ghost Glovewort.

      Return to the Site of Grace again, jump down the stairs and this time jump even further down. You will take some damage upon landing. Follow the path inside the building until you reach a room with some graves and 2 enemies patroling there. On top of a grave you can find the Azula Beastmen Ashes. Continue until you reach the Dragon Temple Transept Site of Grace. After this Site fight the  Godskin Duo, who will drop Ash of War: Black Flame Tornado and Smithing-Stone Miner's Bell Bearing 4, which unlocks levels 7 & 8 of upgrade materials for normal armaments, once given to Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold.
    8. Continue towards the next buildings. After you jump through a window to progress through a platform with some dog enemies, go to the next building and climb to the second level. Look towards the building that had the dogs and carefuly use the floating column to jump on top of that building. Guarded by an enemy you can find an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone. In the same building find a mist gate that can be unlocked by using 2 Stonesword Keys. Climb the platform and reach the Dragon Temple Lift Site of Grace.

      In the new area find a Lord's Rune in the middle of a fountain. A little further from the fountain you can find a Golden Seed by a golden tree. Past the tree you can see the top of some columns that you can jump on to go to the other side of the cliff. Before you go to the other side, carefully fall to the bottom to find a scarab that drops the Golden Lightning Fortification. Past the scarab you can find another unnamed Dragon enemy that will not drop anything of great value (Gravel Stone). Now cross the cliff and follow the path climbing higher and higher. Soon after you will find Alexander. He will ask you to fight him in order to complete his quest. Do so to receive the Shard of Alexander and Alexander's Innards. Continue going to the top and inside a small building guarded by two skeleton enemies you can find the Ancient Dragon Apostle's Cookbook 4.
    9. Return to Jarburg in Liurnia East and talk to Jar-Bairn. Give him Alexander's Innards, then reload the area to find Companion Jar Talisman, thus ending his quest as well.
    10. Back to the Dragon Temple Lift Site of Grace return to the bottom floor and continue through the door on the bottom floor. As you jump over the broken platforms, look carefully for a corpse that has the Dragoncrest Shield Talisman +2. Keep moving through the destroyed buildings until you reach an area with a Crucible Knight enemy. Past this enemy you can jump on some roofs to reach the Dragon Temple Rooftop Site of Grace.
    11. From the Dragon Temple Rooftop Site move forward. You will be in an area with enemy eagles and the final unnamed Dragon enemy that will be throwing lightning strikes at you. When you descend the first ladder after the first couple of Eagle enemies, take a hard left turn and jump onto the floating rock and then to the platform below. At the other side of the balcony you can find the Drake Knight set in a chest.

      Reach the Dragon and defeat him to get an Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone. In the gazebo behind the dragon you can find a Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone. From the Dragon you can take a path that leads to the level that eagles came from to find a Golden Seed. Back at the spot you defeated the dragon, if you look at the edge of the nearby cliff, you will some floating rocks leading to a building below. Go there and past the enemies you can find the Dragon Towershield in a chest. Take the lift and continue past the point where a Crucible Knight is fighting some Misbegotten enemies. Climb the ladder there to find the Boltdrake Talisman +2.
    12. From the place that you defeated the dragon move forward up the slope and past some enemies. You will soon enter a building with more Beastmen enemies. The item in front of the altar is the Somberstone Miner's Bell Bearing 5, which unlocks level 9 of upgrade materials for special armaments, once given to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable Hold. Take the elevator in this building to reach the Beside the Great Bridge Site of Grace.
    13. From the Site of Grace, take the elevator back to the area with the Beastmen and exit the building heading straight towards the edge of the cliff. If you look carefully below the ledge, you can see some more floating rocks that will lead you to a platform. Once there, lie down and you will be to the area where you'll fight  Dragonlord Placidusax, who will drop the Remembrance of the Dragonlord. Take it to Enia in Roundtable for Dragon King's Cragblade or Placidusax’s Ruin. If you want both items, you can choose to gain another charge for this Remembrance at any Mausoleum.

      NOTE: There is a very very rare scenario, in which your character will die at the same time you kill the boss. This results in your character spawning to a Site of Grace outside this arena. Since you have defeated the boss and died before activating the Site of Grace here, you will not be able to gain access to the arena again. As a result this can mess up your run, especially if you have already inherited the Frenzied Flame from the Three Fingers. Without access to the arena you will not be able to reverse the Frenzied Flame and this means the other endings will be unavailable for this run. To avoid this scenario try your best to have a lot of HP when killing the boss and avoid his Lightning Cloud attack when you are about to finish him off. Making a back-up of your save file is also highly recommended.
    14. Now that you have defeated Placidusax, it is at this arena that you can use Miquella's Needle and reverse the Frenzied Flame you received from the Three Fingers. Be very careful as when you reverse it, you will NOT be able to get the Lord of Chaos ending in this run anymore. Reversing the Flame allows you to get the rest of the endings once more. If you are trying to get every trophy/achievement in one run, then make sure to not use the Needle yet, until the Endings section.
    15. Return to the Beside the Great Bridge Site. You want to get on top of the bridge and instead of heading towards the mist gate on the right that features the area's main boss, you want to go to the left and into the building. Once you exit to a balcony, descend a ladder and you will see a tower in front of you. On this bridge you will be invaded by Recusant Bernahl, who will drop the Beast Champion set, the Blasphemous Claw and the legendary armament Devourer's Scepter. This is also the last armament you need for the  Legendary Armaments.
    16. Enter the tower past the invasion to find the legendary Old Lord's Talisman in the chest. Be careful as three beastmen are feigning death nearby.
    17. Return to the bridge that leads to the boss. Fight the Draconic Tree Sentinel guarding the boss' arena to get the Malformed Dragon set.
    18. Completion of this region is done by defeating the next boss. A lot of the game will change regarding NPCs and Leyndell. This is your last chance to get everything you need. In addition to the items that are mentioned in Leyndell's section, you may also want to take note of the following items:

      The Duelist Set
      Duelist Greataxe
      Battle Hammer
      Guardian Garb (Full Bloom)

      These items are dropped by farming kills from enemies inside Leyndell, who will permanently despawn upon defeating the next boss, making them missable. The first three items can be dropped by killing the Duelist enemies near the Colliseum, while the last one can be farmed from the Erdtree Guardians near the Erdtree Sanctuary Site of Grace.

      Back in Farum Azula enter through the gate and defeat  Maliketh the Black Blade, who will drop the Remembrance of the Black Blade. Take it to Enia in Roundtable for Maliketh's Black Blade or Black Blade Incantation. If you want both items, you can choose to gain another charge for this Remembrance at any Mausoleum. Enia will also sell Maliketh's Set.

    You will have to beat Starscourge Radahn in Caelid and Mohg, Lord of Blood in Mohgwyn Palace to access the DLC. The access point to the new area is Miquella's Cocoon in Mohg's arena in the palace.

    The DLC can be started at any point you like after meeting the above requirements. You can do it right after defeating Mohg or after defeating Malenia. You can go to the Land of Shadow before you burn the Erdtree or before you fight the final boss in the next section or even leave it for the post-game. The author's personal choice is to do the DLC right before accessing the final section of the game, hence why the DLC was placed right after Farum Azula. The sole reason for this choice is the story, as the DLC's ending doesn't offer a proper conclusion to your character, rather a conclusion to Miquella's story, who is just one of the many demigods you encounter in Elden Ring. Having the base game's ending as the very last thing your character does feels like the correct closure to your journey in the Lands Between and the Land of Shadow. No matter your choice, however, on when you start it, the DLC can still be enjoyed even if you leave it for the post-game.

    Leyndell, Ash Capital





    Upon completing Farum Azula, once you return to Leyndell, you will see that the capital has undergone a major change. Now that you burned the Giant Erdtree, Leyndell has become the Ash Capital. You will also find out that most of the Sites of Grace in the capital are now gone. When you are transported in this area, touch the Site of Grace Leyndell, Capital of Ash in front of you.

    1. From the Forbidden Lands Site of Grace that leads you to the Mountaintops of Giants, take the lifts back towards Leyndell. After you have used the the second lift to go down in the capital, you will find a big courtyard in which an Ulcerated Tree Spirit is roaming. In this courtyard you can find a broken tree log and on top is the legendary talisman Erdtree's Favor +2. This is also the last one you need for the  Legendary Talismans.
    2. Subterranean Shunning Grounds: While we have explored this area before, there is now one item that we can get that was unavailable before. From the Leyndell, Capital of Ash Site move southwest towards the dragon statue and go towards the buildings. You can see a hole in the ground between these buildings, which is the new entrance for this dungeon. Carefully descend and get on the wooden beam that has a corpse with the Crimson Amber Medallion +2.
    3. From the Leyndell, Capital of Ash Site move west keeping the dragon statue to your left. You can soon enter a destroyed building under one of its wings and climb a ladder. Once up the ladder, look to your right to find a wandering Valiant Gargoyle. A corpse near this enemy has a Somber Ancient Dragon Smithing Stone.
    4. In this new area you can also find Goldmask. He can be found at the cliffs at the very bottom of the coliseum. From the Leyndell, Capital of Ash Site head southwest and you will see a curved staircase. This is where you have to go to reach the end of the game. To the right of this staircase and down a slope, cross the broken bridge and then go left to find Goldmask. Interact with him to get the Mending Rune of Perfect Order. This Rune is required to get a specific version of one ending. Reload the area to find Goldmask's set and complete his quest.
    5. You can also find Corhyn here. He can be found southwest from the Site of Grace at the base of the giant spear that had the Bolt of Gransax. Exhaust the dialogue with him, then reload the area to get Corhyn's Robe, a Flail and Corhyn's Bell Bearing, which you should give to the Twin Maiden Husks in Roundtable. If you offered to give him the Tonic of Forgetfulness, then you will be able to find him in his previous location in the bridge near Stargazer Ruins in Mountaintops of Giants (West). There is no difference to the outcome, only some change in the dialogue.
    6. Go to the curved staircase that you found on your way to Goldmask and climb them into the building. Continue until you encounter Sir Gideon Ofnir, the All-Knowing. Defeat him in combat to get the All-Knowing set and the Scepter of All-Knowing.
    7. Continue up the giant root following the same path as when you went to fight Morgott. At the next Site of Grace, Queen's Bedchamber, you can find the Erdtree Heal.
    8. At the end of the area where you fought Morgott before, fight  Hoarah Loux, the Warrior, who will drop the Remembrance of Hoarah Loux. Take it to Enia in Roundtable for Axe of Godfrey or Ash of War: Hoarah Loux's Earthshaker. If you want both items, you can choose to gain another charge for this Remembrance at any Mausoleum. Enia will also sell the Elden Lord set after defeating this boss.
    9. After the boss you can enter the Giant Erdtree to fight the final two bosses of the game in succession; Radagon of the Golden Order and then the Elden Beast. Defeating both bosses will reward you with the Elden Remembrance.

    Endings & Post Game

    After defeating the Elden Beast, rest at the Site of Grace. In front of you there will be a statue, which once you interact with, will reveal the game's ending. You can travel to any point of the world you want to explore that you didn't go to earlier. But if you followed the walkthrough up to this point, there is only a couple of things you can do;

    1. Go to Roundtable Hold and give to Enia the Elden Remembrance to choose Marika's Hammer OR Sacred Relic Sword. If you want both items, you can choose to gain another charge for this Remembrance at any Mausoleum.
    2. An optional gesture you can get is The Ring gesture. To unlock this one you must have pre-ordered the game before the official release OR play online with someone that has pre-ordered the game, then defeat a boss in co-op together to get it as well. The gesture will NOT unlock if you co-op with someone that has received the gesture from someone else.
    3. If you haven't used any Mausoleums to gain another charge for any of the Remembrances you obtained, make sure to do it now. In general if you want to get every item as soon as possible, you want to use the Remembrances you obtained late in the game, so you won't have to play the entire game a second time. Not using a Mausoleum before finishing the game will waste their charges, as you cannot transfer these charges among playthroughs.
    4. If you haven't done already, you can upgrade any armament, normal or special, to its max level (+25 for normal armaments and +10 for special armaments) to get the  God-Slaying Armament.

    At this point, if you followed the walkthrough, you will have obtained everything you can get in the game, so the only thing left to do is to watch the ending of your choice. Return to the area where you defeated the Elden Beast.

    There are 6 Endings that you can choose from, although Endings 1-4 are essentially the same ending with the last scene playing a little differently. You can choose to view only one. The walkthrough at this point has inherited the Frenzied Flame, so the Lord of Frenzied Flame is the only ending you can get right now. If you want to view any other ending, return to Placidusax' arena in Crumbling Farum Azula and use Miquella's Needle to reverse the flame. As a reminder, this PERMANENTLY reverses the Frenzied Flame, meaning you cannot get it again in this playthrough. Be careful of your choice.

    If, however, you want to get all the ending trophies/achievements without playing the game a second or more times, you can backup your save data to a USB or upload it to PS+ Cloud after you disable auto-updating the save files to the PS+. After you view the ending of your choice (which if you followed the walkthrough will be the Lord of Frenzied Flame first), you can then copy the save from the USB back to your console and view the next ending. Repeat until you have seen all endings and obtained all respective trophies/achievements. The recommended order of viewing the endings if you followed the walkthrough is:

    1. Lord of Frenzied Flame
    2. Elden Lord (any version or all of them if you are a completionist. Only one version is required for the trophy/achievement.)
    3. Age of Stars

    The requirements of each ending are the following:

    Ending 1: Elden Lord - Age of Fracture


    For this ending you simply have to touch the statue after the Elden Beast boss and choose to Mend the Elden Ring. Also awards  Elden Lord.

    Ending 2: Elden Lord - Age of Duskborn


    For this ending you must first obtain the Mending Rune of the Death-Prince by completing Fia's quest. Then you have to touch the statue after the Elden Beast boss and choose to Use Mending Rune of the Death-Prince. Also awards  Elden Lord.

    Ending 3: Elden Lord - Blessing of Despair


    For this ending you must first obtain the Mending Rune of the Fell Curse by completing Dung Eater's quest. Then you have to touch the statue after the Elden Beast boss and choose to Use Mending Rune of the Fell Curse. Also awards  Elden Lord.

    Ending 4: Elden Lord - Age of Order


    For this ending you must first obtain the Mending Rune of Perfect Order by completing Corhyn's and Goldmask's quest. Then you have to touch the statue after the Elden Beast boss and choose to Use Mending Rune of Perfect Order. Also awards  Elden Lord.

    Ending 5: Lord of Frenzied Flame


    For this ending you have to touch the statue after the Elden Beast boss while you have the Frenzied Flame inherited from the Three Fingers. Then choose to Become the Lord of Frenzied Flame. Also awards  Lord of Frenzied Flame.

    Ending 6: Age of the Stars


    For this ending you first have to complete Ranni's quest. Then after the Elden Beast boss you will find a blue summon sign in front of the statue. Use it to summon Ranni and view the game's best ending. Also awards  Age of the Stars.

    4. Make sure to get the Ashes BEFORE giving the Fingerslayer Blade to Ranni

    You must also reach the end of Millicent's quest once more and this time either choose to invade her or summon her (whichever you didn't do during your first run) to get either the Rotten Winged Sword Insignia or Millicent's Prosthesis.

    Steps needed for this quest:
    1. Talk to Gowry in Caelid
    2. Defeat Commander O'Neil to get the Unalloyed Gold Needle
    3. Give the needle to Gowry, wait for him to repair it then take it back
