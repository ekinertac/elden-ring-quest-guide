# Elden Ring DLC - Shadow of the Erdtree Quest Guide

## Graveside Plain/Cerulean Coast
- [ ] Speak to Leda at Cocoon of the Empyrean Grace
- [ ] Speak to Freyja and Hornsent at Three-Path Cross Grace
- [ ] At Main Gate Cross, buy something from Moore, then talk to Moore and Ansbach
- [ ] Reload and talk to Ansbach again
- [ ] Defeat Fire Knight Queelign past Small Private Altar Grace
- [ ] Pick up storage room key, unlock door, speak to Hornsent Grandam
- [ ] After finding cross in Belurat, speak to Ansbach
- [ ] (Optional) Summon Freyja for Dancing Lion
- [ ] Return to Hornsent Grandam with Divine Beast Head
- [ ] Give soup to Hornsent
- [ ] Speak to Thiollier at Pillar Path Cross Grace
- [ ] Speak to Moore, then return to Thiollier
- [ ] Speak to Igon near Pillar Path Waypoint, return and speak again
- [ ] Go through Dragon Pit, speak to Dragon Communion Priestess
- [ ] After finding Cerulean Coast Cross, speak to Ansbach
- [ ] Get Forager Brood Cookbook #3 northeast of Cerulean Coast
- [ ] (Optional) Summon Leda for Rellana

## Scadu Altus/Church District
- [ ] Talk to Leda and Hornsent at Highcross Road Grace
- [ ] Talk to Freyja at Three-Path Cross
- [ ] Defeat Fire Knight Queelign at Church of Crusade
- [ ] Find injured Forager Brood north of Church, use warming stone
- [ ] Return to Moore with Cookbook #4
- [ ] Meet Dryleaf Dane at Moorth Ruins, do "May the Best Win" gesture
- [ ] Talk to Leda at Highcross Road
- [ ] Get Forager Brood Cookbook #1 northwest of Ruined Forge
- [ ] Get Forager Brood Cookbook #5 north of Moorth Highway
- [ ] Find Iris of Occultation in Fort of Reprimand
- [ ] Speak to Ansbach after finding Scaduview Cross
- [ ] Get "O Mother" gesture in Bonny Village
- [ ] Once "Great Rune Has Broken" triggers, exhaust dialogue with all NPCs
- [ ] Get Forager Brood Cookbook #6 above Church District
- [ ] Speak to Moore with all cookbooks
- [ ] Find Iris of Occultation at Ulcerated Tree Spirit
- [ ] Find Iris of Grace beneath Marika statue
- [ ] Use Prayer Room Key to speak to Queelign
- [ ] Speak to Ymir at Cathedral of Manus Metyr
- [ ] Talk to Jolan by the pillar, exhaust dialogue

## Fissure
- [ ] Speak to Thiollier at southern-most Fissure
- [ ] (Optional) Summon Thiollier for Putrescent Knight
- [ ] Speak to Thiollier after Putrescent Knight
- [ ] Imbibe nectar 4 times, talk to Thiollier
- [ ] Imbibe nectar again - Thiollier invades, defeat him
- [ ] Imbibe nectar again, talk to Thiollier

## Jagged Peak
- [ ] Speak to Igon after defeating dragon
- [ ] Return to Dragon Communion Priestess
- [ ] Choose whether to give her Thiollier's potion
- [ ] Summon Igon for Bayle (required)
- [ ] Return to Igon
- [ ] Return to Dragon Communion Priestess

## Shadow Keep/Upper Ancient Ruins
- [ ] (Optional) Summon Freyja and Hornsent for Golden Hippo
- [ ] Speak to Leda about Hornsent
- [ ] Choose Leda or Hornsent (or skip) in burning boats area
- [ ] Reach Abyssal Woods via waterfall and illusory wall
- [ ] Speak to Ansbach at Specimen Storehouse first floor
- [ ] Speak to Freyja at Storehouse Seventh Floor
- [ ] Choose whether to summon Hornsent for Messmer
- [ ] Return to Belurat, speak to Hornsent Grandam
- [ ] Speak to Ansbach about Freyja, give Secret Rite Scroll
- [ ] Choose whether to give Freyja Ansbach's letter
- [ ] Exhaust Leda's dialogue at Highcross Road
- [ ] Return to Specimen Storehouse for Leda vs Ansbach fight
- [ ] Find Iris of Grace on way to Ancient Ruins
- [ ] (Optional) Summon Dane for Romina

## Finger Ruins
- [ ] Go to Finger Ruins of Rhia, use Hanging Bell
- [ ] Return to Ymir and Jolar at Manus Metyr
- [ ] Use "O Mother" gesture at Marika statue near Shadow Keep back gate
- [ ] (Optional) Summon Jolan for Tree Sentinel
- [ ] Go to Finger Ruins of Dheo, use Hanging Bell
- [ ] Return to Ymir and Jolan
- [ ] Exhaust Ymir's dialogue until he leaves throne, examine throne
- [ ] Defeat Swordhand of Night Anna, return to Jolan
- [ ] Fight Metyr, return to examine Ymir's throne
- [ ] Find item in cemetery west of Manus Metyr
- [ ] Choose to give Jolan Iris of Grace or Iris of Occultation
- [ ] If Grace: go to Shaman Village, find dropdowns to Rabbath's Rise

## Enir Ilim
- [ ] After entering, some NPCs will attack based on choices
- [ ] Leda always attacks
- [ ] Freyja attacks if you gave her Ansbach's letter
- [ ] Hornsent attacks if alive and not summoned for Messmer
- [ ] Moore attacks if told to "put it behind him"
- [ ] Dane always attacks
- [ ] Ansbach helps if alive and given scroll
- [ ] Thiollier helps if told about St. Trina
- [ ] Speak to Ansbach at top of elevator after fight
- [ ] (Optional) Summon Ansbach and Thiollier for final boss
- [ ] After final boss, rest at Gate of Divinity, find Ansbach and Thiollier
- [ ] Return to St. Trina after final boss
