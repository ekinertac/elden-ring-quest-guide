# Elden Ring - Main Game Quest Guide

## Limgrave
- [ ] Talk to White-Faced Varre at The First Step Grace
- [ ] Talk to Merchant Kale at Church of Elleh
- [ ] Return to Church of Elleh at night to meet Renna (Ranni)
- [ ] Meet Boc by rolling into talking tree north of Agheel Lake
- [ ] Talk to Boc at Coastal Cave, then kill the boss
- [ ] Give Boc the sewing needle
- [ ] Meet Sorceress Sellen under Waypoint Ruins
- [ ] Meet Bloody Finger Hunter Yura southeast of Agheel Lake
- [ ] Get invaded by Bloody Finger Nerijus - let Yura help kill him
- [ ] Enter Murkwater Cave, defeat Patches, exhaust dialogue
- [ ] Hear howling in Mistwood, return to Kale, use gesture, find Blaidd
- [ ] Find Kenneth Haight, clear Fort Haight, return to him
- [ ] Summon Blaidd to defeat Forlorn Hound Evergael

## Weeping Peninsula
- [ ] Meet Irina past Bridge of Sacrifice
- [ ] Find Irina's father Edgar at Castle Morne, deliver letter
- [ ] Summon Edgar for Castle Morne boss

## Stormhill
- [ ] Meet Roderika at Stormhill Shack, talk multiple times
- [ ] Meet Bernahl at Warmaster's Shack
- [ ] Meet Iron Fist Alexander at Saintsbridge
- [ ] Meet D, Hunter of the Dead at Saintsbridge
- [ ] Take sending gate to Bestial Sanctum, meet Gurranq
- [ ] Give Gurranq Deathroot (#1-9 throughout game)
- [ ] Talk to everyone at Roundtable Hold after arriving
- [ ] Return to The First Step Grace to talk to Varre

## Stormveil Castle
- [ ] Summon Rogier for Margit
- [ ] Talk to Gatekeeper Gostoc
- [ ] Talk to Rogier in chapel, buy something, talk again
- [ ] Find Chrysalid's Memento, give to Roderika
- [ ] Talk to Nepheli before Godrick, summon her for fight
- [ ] After Godrick, talk to everyone at Roundtable Hold
- [ ] Talk to Roderika and Hewg until Hewg takes her as apprentice
- [ ] Return to The First Step Grace for Varre's message

## Liurnia - Southeast
- [ ] Meet Hyetta at Lake-Facing Cliffs, give Shabiri Grape
- [ ] Meet Thops at Church of Irith
- [ ] Find hidden floor in Purified Ruins for Shabiri Grape
- [ ] Give grape to Hyetta
- [ ] Defeat mariner for Deathroot #3 (summon D)
- [ ] Find stone slabs to Jarburg, talk to Jar-Bairn
- [ ] Meet Miriel at Church of Vows
- [ ] Get Gold Sewing Needle from Church of Vows
- [ ] Defeat secret boss in Black Knife Catacombs for Deathroot #4
- [ ] Get Black Knifeprint, give to Rogier, talk to Fia
- [ ] Meet Diallos at Academy Gate Town
- [ ] Talk to Patches at Scenic Isle
- [ ] Meet Rya at pavilion, offer to help
- [ ] Meet Blackguard Big Boggart at Boilprawn Shack
- [ ] Buy necklace from Boggart, buy boiled prawns
- [ ] Go to Village of Albinaurics, meet Nepheli
- [ ] Roll into pot to find Albus, summon Nepheli for Omen Killer
- [ ] Find Latenna in cave west of Village
- [ ] Talk to Varre at Rose Church, do 3 invasions
- [ ] Meet Edgar at Revenger's Shack, get Shabiri Grape
- [ ] Get Glintstone Key near island west of Academy
- [ ] Enter Academy, help Yura kill invader
- [ ] Give extra key to Thops at Church of Irith
- [ ] Find Thops at Academy
- [ ] Give Hyetta grape at Gate Town Bridge
- [ ] Talk to Varre at Bellum Church, give Fingerprint Grape

## Liurnia - Northwest
- [ ] Go to Four Belfries, use key on northeast belfry for Stormhawk King
- [ ] Talk to Iji by main road
- [ ] Go through Caria Manor, speak to Ranni
- [ ] Talk to Blaidd, Iji, Seluvis, then Ranni
- [ ] Go to Seluvis' Rise, give potion to Nepheli, Gideon, or Dung Eater
- [ ] Give Stormhawk King Ashes to Nepheli
- [ ] Find illusory floor near Ranni's rise, return to Seluvis
- [ ] Speak to Pidia at Caria Manor
- [ ] Take elevator to Siofra River, find Blaidd
- [ ] Speak to Seluvis, then Sellen, then Blaidd

## Caelid/Dragonbarrow
- [ ] Go through Gael Tunnel, open door for Alexander
- [ ] Talk to Alexander and Blaidd at Redmane Castle
- [ ] Summon Blaidd and Alexander for Radahn
- [ ] Talk to Witchhunter Jerren at Redmane
- [ ] Meet Gowry at Gowry's Shack
- [ ] Kill Commander O-Neil for needle, give to Gowry
- [ ] Meet Milicent at Church of Plague
- [ ] Give needle to Milicent
- [ ] Return to Gowry's shack, talk to Milicent
- [ ] Find Alexander west of Jarburg, use oil pot
- [ ] Talk to Great Jar, defeat 3 summons for reward

## Altus Plateau
- [ ] Climb Ruin-Strewn Precipice north of The Ravine
- [ ] Talk to everyone at Roundtable Hold
- [ ] Talk to Corhyn near map
- [ ] Find Yura at Second Church of Marika
- [ ] Get Amber Starlight for Seluvis, return to him
- [ ] Get Valkyrie's Prosthesis from Shaded Castle
- [ ] Talk to Milicent at Erdtree Gazing Hill
- [ ] Talk to Goldmask with Corhyn
- [ ] Talk to Milicent at Windmill Heights
- [ ] Find Margit at Outer Wall Battleground
- [ ] Talk to Blackguard Big Boggart at Leyndell moat

## Nokron/Nokstella
- [ ] Find message from Blaidd near Starfall Crater
- [ ] Talk to Iji
- [ ] Free Blaidd at Forlorn Hound Evergael
- [ ] Talk to Iji
- [ ] Go to Nokron, find Fingerslayer Blade, bring to Ranni
- [ ] Go to Ainsel River Main, talk to miniature Ranni
- [ ] Continue through Nokstella, get discarded key
- [ ] Use key in Raya Lucaria Grand Library
- [ ] Cross Lake of Rot, find Ranni
- [ ] Return to Ranni's Rise, talk to Blaidd
- [ ] Talk to Iji

## Deeproot Depths
- [ ] Find speaking man, give him D's armor
- [ ] After boss, find Fia, ask to be held
- [ ] Talk to D next to Fia

## Mt Gelmir
- [ ] Defeat mariner for Deathroot #5
- [ ] Talk to Alexander near Seethewater Terminus
- [ ] Get "You're Beautiful" prattling pate in Hermit Village
- [ ] Talk to Primeval Sorcerer Azur, then Sellen
- [ ] Find Selen at Witchbane Ruins
- [ ] Return to Seluvis' puppets near Ranni's Rise
- [ ] At Raya Lucaria, choose Sellen or Jerren
- [ ] Complete Gelmir Hero's Grave for Deathroot #6
- [ ] Talk to Patches near Gelmir Hero's Grave

## Volcano Manor
- [ ] Meet Rya at Lux Ruins or continue through Mt Gelmir
- [ ] Join Volcano Manor, talk to everyone
- [ ] Complete requests, talk to everyone
- [ ] Find illusory wall, explore to Prison Town Church
- [ ] Talk to Rya and Tanith
- [ ] Complete Patches' quest
- [ ] Get Serpent's Amnion, give to Rya
- [ ] Complete final boss or leave

## Leyndell
- [ ] Give Seedbed Curse to Dung Eater
- [ ] Buy Radahn's armor, give Golden Sewing Needle to Boc
- [ ] Give Boc larval tear OR play "You're Beautiful"
- [ ] Get Seedbed Curse #2 in Leyndell
- [ ] Find Dung Eater's body, release him
- [ ] Talk to Blackguard, then Dung Eater
- [ ] Get Seedbed Curse #3 in alternate Roundtable Hold
- [ ] Complete Bernahl's request
- [ ] Talk to Corhyn and Goldmask
- [ ] Get Golden Order Principia, give to Corhyn
- [ ] Cast Law of Regression at Erdtree Sanctuary
- [ ] Summon Melina for Margott
- [ ] Talk to Nepheli, Kenneth, Gostoc at Lake-Facing Cliffs

## Mountaintops of the Giants
- [ ] Summon Milicent for Black Blade Kindred
- [ ] Talk to Shabriri at Zamor Ruins
- [ ] Get Deathroot #7 in Giants' Mountaintop Catacombs
- [ ] Talk to Milicent at Ancient Snow Valley Ruins
- [ ] Complete Volcano Manor final quest
- [ ] Return to Jarburg, talk to Jar-Bairn
- [ ] Talk to Patches at Shaded Castle
- [ ] Find Patches in Murkwater Cave
- [ ] Get Deathroot #8 at Freezing Lake
- [ ] Get half Haligtree Medallion at Castle Sol
- [ ] Talk to Corhyn and Goldmask
- [ ] Summon Alexander for Fire Giant

## Consecrated Snowfield/Haligtree
- [ ] Get Deathroot #9 in Hidden Path to Haligtree
- [ ] Summon Latenna at Apostate Derelict
- [ ] Go to Mohgwyn Palace, defeat Mohg
- [ ] Talk to Gideon at Haligtree
- [ ] Talk to Milicent at Prayer Room
- [ ] Get Seedbed Curses #4 and #5 in Haligtree
- [ ] Give all Seedbed Curses to Dung Eater
- [ ] Choose to help Milicent or fight her
- [ ] Talk to Gowry
- [ ] After Malenia, talk to Gideon

## Crumbling Farum Azula
- [ ] Talk to everyone at Roundtable Hold
- [ ] Summon Bernahl for Godskin Duo
- [ ] Find Alexander, talk to him
- [ ] Talk to Bernahl on the bridge

## Capital of Ash
- [ ] Talk to everyone at Roundtable Hold
- [ ] Find Goldmask on path to Erdtree Sanctuary
- [ ] Find Corhyn at spiral spear statue
- [ ] Summon Nepheli and Shabriri for First Elden Lord

## Endings
- [ ] Copy save after final boss before making choice
- [ ] For Frenzied Flame: Go to Lyndell sewers, fight Mohg, roll into altar, talk to Melina, talk to Hyetta, speak to Melina at grace, talk to Shabriri
- [ ] To remove Frenzied Flame: Use Unalloyed Gold Needle after Malenia, use Miquella's Needle in Placidusax arena
